# Strategy

In [[Human Design System]], Strategy is the type-specific behavioral guideline that describes how each energy type is designed to engage with life, decisions, and opportunities. Strategy is paired with [[Inner Authority]] as the two primary practical tools the system offers. The purpose of following one's Strategy is to reduce resistance, minimize the not-self, and over time produce the signature state associated with each type.

Strategy does not operate through mental planning or deliberate intention. It is a mechanical description of how each type's aura naturally functions in relation to life and other people.

## Strategy by Type

### Generator: Respond

Generators are designed to respond to life rather than initiate it. Response means waiting for something in the external environment -- a question, an offer, an event, a sound, an image -- to arise, and then checking the sacral response before acting. The sacral center has a voice expressed through sounds (uh-huh for yes, unh-unh for no) that indicate whether the generator's life force is engaged or not.

"All they have to do is respond. They're not here to initiate. And what that means is that they're not here to initiate according to making decisions to initiate a decision." - Ra Uru Hu

Responding does not require passivity in daily life. It means that the generator's actions are most correct when they arise from genuine sacral engagement with something that has presented itself, rather than from mental agenda. A generator who initiates from the mind tends toward frustration -- the not-self theme of the Generator type.

### Manifesting Generator: Respond (and Inform)

Manifesting Generators share the Generator strategy of responding with their sacral center. However, because they also have a motor connected to the throat, they have additional capacity to initiate action. This speed and multi-step capacity means Manifesting Generators often skip steps that others would take sequentially. They are advised to inform others of their intentions before acting, to reduce resistance from their environment, similar to the [[Manifestor]] strategy.

The Manifesting Generator's signature is satisfaction (shared with Generators), and the not-self theme is also frustration, with additional experiences of anger when they fail to inform.

### Manifestor: Inform

Manifestors are the only type that can truly initiate without waiting for an external cue. However, because their aura is closed and repelling, their actions create an impact on others that can generate resistance or suspicion if those people are not forewarned. The Manifestor strategy of informing addresses this. Informing means letting those who will be affected by an action know what is going to happen -- not asking permission, but notifying.

"Informing clears the path. It moves things out of the way. It moves mountains, in fact. And it is the only freedom that can be available to the manifestor." - Ra Uru Hu

Children in the house of their parents are advised to ask permission rather than simply inform, as they are under the authority of others. Adult Manifestors inform.

The signature of the Manifestor is peace. The not-self theme is anger.

### Projector: Wait for the Invitation

Projectors do not have consistent access to the sacral energy that drives Generators, and they do not have the Manifestor's capacity to initiate. Their Strategy is to wait for a formal invitation before entering into significant life situations -- particularly career, relationships, and where they live.

The invitation must be genuine recognition of the Projector's value, offered by someone who has seen and appreciated what the Projector brings. An invitation is not simply someone asking for help or advice in passing. It is a formal recognition that opens the door for the Projector to share their gifts.

Projectors who act without invitation tend toward bitterness -- the not-self theme. The signature is success.

### Reflector: Wait for the Lunar Cycle

Reflectors have no defined centers and therefore no consistent inner authority. Their Strategy is to wait a full lunar cycle (approximately 28 days) before making significant decisions. During this period the Moon transits all 64 gates of the wheel, temporarily activating different definitions in the Reflector's chart. This gives the Reflector a complete experiential sample of all the relevant energies before reaching a decision.

Reflectors are also advised to talk through decisions with many different people rather than relying on any single perspective, as each person will reflect a different aspect of the situation back to them.

The Reflector signature is surprise -- the pleasant surprise of a life that works. The not-self theme is disappointment.

## Strategy vs. Mental Decision-Making

Human Design consistently distinguishes Strategy from mental deliberation. The mind is described as unsuitable for making decisions because it operates from the conditioning absorbed through open centers. Strategy bypasses mental deliberation by anchoring decisions in the body's mechanical response.

"As long as you're making decisions with your mind, you're never going to be free." - Ra Uru Hu

## Relationship to Authority

Strategy and Authority work together. Strategy addresses when and how to engage with life at the level of type. Authority governs the specific decision made within those engagements. For example, a Generator responding to an offer uses Strategy by waiting for response and then checks their [[Sacral Authority]] to determine if the response is yes or no. An emotional Generator applies [[Emotional Authority]] by waiting for emotional clarity before acting on their sacral response.
