# Conditioning

Conditioning is the process through which a person's undefined or open centers absorb and are shaped by the energies of others and the larger transit field. In [[Human Design System]], conditioning is the primary mechanism that produces the [[Not-Self]]. It is described as mechanical and unavoidable given the structure of the human aura, though its effects can be mitigated through awareness and by following one's [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]].

## Mechanism of Conditioning

The [[BodyGraph]] contains nine centers. Centers that are not defined in a person's chart are open and receptive to definition from outside. When another person with a defined center enters the aura of someone with that same center undefined, the undefined center becomes temporarily defined and amplified. This amplification is often more intense than anything the person generates on their own.

"These open centers are receptors. They are receptors to anything that can hook them up." - Ra Uru Hu

Because the amplification feels vivid and real, the mind over time identifies with it and builds strategies around maintaining it. A person with an undefined Heart Center may repeatedly overcommit or overwork trying to prove their worth, because this amplified sense of striving feels like who they are. A person with an undefined Sacral may keep pushing beyond their energy limits because in the presence of sacral beings, they feel temporarily energized.

## Conditioning Agents

Several forces condition the open centers:

**Other people**: The most immediate source. Within the size of a human aura (roughly an arm's length in all directions, penetrating through walls), any defined center will condition the matching undefined center of whoever is present.

**Transits**: The planetary bodies move through the 64 gates of the wheel continuously, activating different gates and channels as they do. These activations condition everyone with corresponding open centers. The neutrino stream -- through which planets transmit their imprint -- is described as a constant background frequency affecting all life on Earth.

**Family and upbringing**: Conditioning from parents and primary caregivers is among the earliest and deepest. A generator mother with a defined sacral, for example, continuously conditions the open sacral of a projector child, which can lead the child to believe they have consistent energy when they do not.

**Culture and education**: Broader social structures homogenize people by imposing uniform expectations (work hours, productivity norms, decision-making styles) that favor some types and disadvantage others.

## Conditioning vs. Wisdom

Human Design distinguishes between being conditioned by open centers and the potential wisdom those centers carry. Open centers are not weaknesses. Over time, a person who observes how they have been conditioned through a particular center can develop understanding of that center's energy that those with it defined may not possess.

"Where your definition is your nature, your openness is where you are nurtured." - Ra Uru Hu

An open Head Center, for example, may be conditioned into endless mental pressure to solve unanswerable questions. But a person who has observed this pattern in themselves can become wise about the nature of inspiration and mental pressure -- capable of recognizing it in others without being driven by it themselves.

## The Conditioning Field

Ra Uru Hu also described what he called the Program -- the background neutrino weather produced by the continuous movement of planetary bodies through the gates. This program conditions humanity collectively and is not personal. Individual conditioning is layered on top of this collective background.

The conditioning is described as neutral in intention: "The program is designed to homogenize. It is not interested in your uniqueness. It is interested in control, in maintaining the status quo."

## Practical Implications

Because conditioning is aura-based, physical proximity matters. The recommendation to sleep alone is specifically about reducing the hours of continuous conditioning during sleep, when there is no waking awareness to observe what is being absorbed.

Choosing environments and relationships consciously -- particularly for types and people with many undefined centers -- is part of living a less conditioned life. This is especially relevant for [[Reflector]] types, who have no defined centers at all and are entirely composed of open receptivity.
