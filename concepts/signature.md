# Signature

In [[Human Design System]], the Signature is the quality of experience that each type naturally arrives at when living in alignment with their [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]]. The Signature is not a goal to pursue mentally or an emotion to manufacture. It is the natural byproduct of correct living according to one's design. Each type has one Signature, which stands in contrast to its not-self theme.

The Signature is used as feedback. When a person experiences their Signature state with regularity, it signals that their Strategy and Authority are working. When the not-self theme dominates instead, it signals a return to conditioning.

## Signatures by Type

### Generator: Satisfaction

The Signature for both [[Generator]] and [[Manifesting Generator]] types is satisfaction. Satisfaction arises when a Generator is using their sacral energy in response to what is genuinely correct for them. When Generators do work they are designed for, respond to the right opportunities, and apply their life force to things that truly engage them, satisfaction is the natural outcome.

Satisfaction in the Human Design sense is not a fleeting pleasure but a stable state of fulfillment that comes from the correct use of energy over time. Ra Uru Hu noted that Generators who find satisfaction reduce the planetary frustration that arises from misused generative energy. "The more satisfaction we have on this plane, the less frustration we have on this plane."

Frustration is the not-self theme -- the signal that a Generator is initiating from the mind, doing work that does not engage their sacral, or ignoring their response mechanism.

### Manifestor: Peace

The Signature for [[Manifestor]] is peace. Peace for the Manifestor arises from informing -- from allowing their power to operate without resistance by letting others know what they intend. When Manifestors inform correctly and act without the constant friction of others' suspicion or control, they can rest within their closed aura in a state of inner peace.

"The reward for the manifestor is truly beautiful. It's peace. To be able to live within oneself within the closed and repelling shell, and to be able to live within that in peace." - Ra Uru Hu

The not-self theme of the Manifestor is anger, which builds when Manifestors are controlled, restricted, or feel they cannot act freely.

### Projector: Success

The Signature for [[Projector]] is success. Success for a Projector is defined as recognition -- being seen accurately, being valued for what they know and can guide, and being invited into roles and relationships that are correct for them. Success is not necessarily material wealth, though it may include this. It is the experience of being recognized and appreciated for the Projector's gifts.

Projectors who wait for genuine invitations before entering major life situations tend to find themselves in roles and relationships where they are valued. This recognition and correct placement is success.

The not-self theme is bitterness, which accumulates when Projectors act without invitation, push their gifts where they are not wanted, or exhaust themselves in work suited to Generators.

### Reflector: Surprise

The Signature for [[Reflector]] is surprise. Because Reflectors have no fixed definition, life offers them a different kind of reward -- the ongoing surprise of what manifests when they are in the right environment, among the right community, and waiting for the lunar cycle before making decisions. Surprise reflects the Reflector's nature as a sampling being: they cannot predict what will arise, but when correct, what arises exceeds expectations.

The not-self theme is disappointment -- the result of making premature decisions, being in the wrong environment, or being among people who do not value the Reflector's unique perception.

## Signature as Practice Feedback

Human Design positions the Signature as a reliable indicator that needs no external validation. A person knows they are living correctly not because a teacher tells them so, but because they notice the Signature quality in their daily experience. Over time, as the deconditioning process unfolds, the Signature becomes more stable and the not-self theme less frequent.

Ra Uru Hu described the relationship between Signature and not-self as a clear binary: "When you look at bitterness on one side and success on the other side, it's a no-brainer where you want to be."
