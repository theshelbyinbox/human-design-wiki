# Solar Plexus Center

The Solar Plexus Center is unique among the nine centers in [[Human Design System]]: it is both a motor and an awareness center. Located to the right of the BodyGraph, it governs emotions, feelings, and the wave pattern of emotional experience. Defined in approximately 50 percent of the population, it is the most common source of [[Emotional Authority]].

## Motor and Awareness

As a motor, the Solar Plexus generates emotional energy that drives action and fuels the BodyGraph. It is one of the most powerful motors in the system. As an awareness center, it provides access to emotional intelligence and empathy -- the capacity to sense and feel the emotional field of others and of oneself.

The dual nature of the Solar Plexus is significant: its power as a motor means emotional energy can be intense enough to overwhelm the awareness function. This is why people with defined Solar Plexus cannot access emotional clarity in the moment -- the motor is running, and clarity requires the motor to settle.

## The Emotional Wave

The Solar Plexus generates a continuous wave that oscillates between hope and pain, high and low. This wave has no resting point. It does not stop. The wave pattern is specific to which channels are active in the Solar Plexus:

- **Tribal waves** (channels 37-40 and 19-49): Build and release in accumulative patterns
- **Abstract waves** (channels 35-36 and 30): Peak sharply and descend gradually
- **Individual waves** (channels 12-22 and 55-39): Spontaneous, unpredictable highs and lows

The wave moves continuously regardless of external circumstances. Emotional lows in the wave do not necessarily indicate that something is wrong; emotional highs do not necessarily indicate that a decision is correct.

## No Truth in the Now

The defining principle for defined Solar Plexus is that there is no emotional truth in the present moment. "No truth in the now" is the central teaching for emotional authority. When on a high of the wave, everything looks positive. When on a low, everything looks negative. Neither is clarity. Clarity arrives through waiting -- allowing the wave to move through enough of its range that a neutral, grounded perspective becomes accessible.

"If you have a defined emotional system, the only thing that is going to save you in this life is understanding that there is no truth in the now." - Ra Uru Hu

## The Future Mutation

Ra Uru Hu described the Solar Plexus as the site of a future awareness mutation in the human species. The current Solar Plexus wave -- oscillating between hope and pain -- represents an early stage of emotional consciousness. The emerging mutation (associated with gate 55 and the shift around 2027) points toward a Solar Plexus that has achieved consistent awareness, transcending the wave pattern. This would represent a new kind of human consciousness: genuinely emotionally aware beings rather than beings driven by the emotional motor.

## Undefined Solar Plexus

People with an undefined Solar Plexus receive and amplify the emotional energy of defined Solar Plexus people around them. This amplification can be experienced as intense emotional reactivity, empathy, or the feeling of absorbing others' moods. A common not-self pattern for undefined Solar Plexus is avoiding confrontation and truth in order to keep the peace -- because conflict triggers the amplified emotional field and feels overwhelming.

## Biological Correlates

Biologically, the Solar Plexus Center is associated with the kidneys, pancreas, prostate, and lungs. Chronic emotional suppression or misuse of emotional energy is considered in Human Design to relate to dysfunction in these organ systems.
