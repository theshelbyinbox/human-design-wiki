# Centers

The Centers are the nine energetic hubs of the BodyGraph. Each center governs a specific kind of awareness, energy, or pressure, and each is shown in the chart as either *defined* (colored in) or *undefined/open* (white). The configuration of defined and open centers determines your type, authority, and the specific patterns of [[Conditioning]] you are most susceptible to.

Defined centers are consistent — they carry their themes reliably and influence others. Undefined centers are sites of openness and wisdom potential, but also of conditioning and not-self decision-making when unconscious.

Human Design teaches that humans shifted from a seven-center to a nine-center configuration in 1781. The current configuration includes the Ajna and Solar Plexus as awareness centers.
