# Heart Center

The Heart Center (also called the Ego Center or Will Center) is a motor center located in the upper-right area of the [[Human Design System]] BodyGraph. It is the smallest center in the system. It governs willpower, ego, the material world, self-worth, promises, and competition. Biologically associated with the physical heart, stomach, thymus, and gall bladder.

## Function

The Heart Center generates willpower -- the sustained energy required to follow through on commitments in the material world. It is also the seat of the ego in the non-pejorative Human Design sense: the capacity to assert one's value, to make and keep promises, and to engage with material reality (work, money, possessions, status).

The Heart is a motor, but a relatively small one. It can only sustain the energy it generates by following through on commitments that the heart genuinely makes. When the Heart makes promises it is not truly committed to, it burns out.

## Defined Heart Center

Only approximately 30 percent of people have a defined Heart Center. Those with a defined Heart have consistent willpower, consistent self-worth, and a clear material drive. Defined Heart people naturally operate in the material world with confidence. They can make promises and keep them when those promises arise from genuine heart commitment.

A common challenge for defined Heart people is the tendency to prove themselves, compete, and demonstrate their worth in material terms. When this operates as not-self, it drives overcommitment, burnout, and resentment.

## Undefined Heart Center

Approximately 70 percent of people have an undefined Heart Center. The most pervasive not-self pattern in Human Design is associated with the undefined Heart: the constant drive to prove worth. An undefined Heart amplifies the willpower and ego of defined Heart people nearby, creating a temporary feeling of self-confidence that collapses when the defined-Heart person leaves.

"If you have an open Heart Center, I tell them they have nothing to prove. Because they don't. It's only if it's colored in that you have to prove anything." - Ra Uru Hu

The wisdom of an undefined Heart is understanding what genuine will feels like in others, and recognizing the difference between authentic commitment and social pressure.

## Heart Center and Ego Authority

A specific channel configuration connecting the Heart to the Throat or G Center creates [[Ego Authority]] -- a rare authority available to some [[Manifestor]] and [[Projector]] types. For these people, the heart's genuine desire is the decision-making mechanism.
