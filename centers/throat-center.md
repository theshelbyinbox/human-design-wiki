# Throat Center

The Throat Center is positioned in the upper-center region of the [[Human Design System]] BodyGraph. It is the center of manifestation and communication -- the point through which all energy in the BodyGraph seeks expression. The Throat is not a motor; it does not generate energy. It is a transformation center that converts energy from the other centers into voice, action, and material manifestation.

## Function

The Throat governs how things come into form: through speaking, through writing, through physical action, through creation. Every center in the BodyGraph ultimately tries to express itself through the Throat. The Throat has eleven gates -- more than any other center -- reflecting the many ways human expression can manifest.

## Motors and the Throat

When a motor center is connected to the Throat through a defined channel or series of channels, the person becomes capable of direct manifestation. This is the defining criterion for [[Manifestor]] and [[Manifesting Generator]] types:

- **Manifesting Generator**: Motor connected to Throat through a path that includes the Sacral
- **Manifestor**: Motor connected to Throat through a path that does not include the Sacral (using the Heart, Solar Plexus, or Root instead)

Generators and Projectors may have definition in the Throat, but not through a direct motor connection. Their Throat energy expresses in response to or in recognition from others, rather than through direct initiation.

## Defined Throat

A defined Throat provides a consistent, fixed way of communicating and manifesting. People with a defined Throat tend to speak in recognizable, characteristic ways -- always expressing from a specific gate or set of gates. Approximately 70 percent of people have a defined Throat.

## Undefined Throat

An undefined Throat is highly sensitive to the speaking patterns and communications styles of those around it. People with undefined Throats may feel pressure to speak, to attract attention, or to make themselves heard. They may speak at inappropriate moments (when not energetically connected) in an attempt to be noticed. This is a classic not-self pattern.

The wisdom of an undefined Throat is the ability to hear the many different ways humans communicate and to adapt fluidly. Undefined Throat people can be extraordinarily versatile communicators once the not-self compulsion to speak for attention is released.

## Throat and the G Center Connection

Some configurations connect the G Center to the Throat, giving certain Projectors access to [[Self-Projected Authority]]. When identity (G Center) is directly connected to expression (Throat), the voice carries the identity, and clarity emerges through speaking.
