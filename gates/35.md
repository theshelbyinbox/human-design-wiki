# Gate 35 — Progress

Gate 35 is the Gate of Change, also called Progress. It sits at the Throat Center and carries the voice of experience — the one who says "I feel." This is the gate of the genetic imperative to grow, to move forward, to collect new experiences and translate them into wisdom. Without 35, the human story does not advance. With it, the story sometimes advances too fast.

## Mechanics

- **Center:** [[Throat]]
- **Circuit:** [[Collective Circuit]] (Sensing / Abstract stream)
- **Channel:** [[Channel 35/36]] — Transitoriness, a design of a Jack of All Trades
- **Hexagram:** 35, Chin — Progress
- **Transit:** Approximately June 1 – 7
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization

Gate 35 is one of the three voices of the Throat in the Collective Abstract circuit. Where Gate 12 says "I try" and Gate 33 says "I remember," Gate 35 says "I feel" — meaning the voice of someone who has actually been through the thing and is reporting back. The 35 must collect experience or it withers. It is paired with the 36 in a single channel that brings emotional crisis and the rising-from-the-ashes wisdom of having lived through it.

## The Hexagram

Hexagram 35 in the I Ching is Chin, "Progress." The image is the sun rising over the earth — light increasing, the day advancing, growth becoming visible. The Wilhelm-Baynes commentary describes a "powerful prince who is honored with horses in large numbers and granted audience three times in a single day." This is the hexagram of the seeker who is recognized and rewarded for the breadth of what they have collected.

The shadow of Chin is exhaustion through over-extension. The sun that rises must also set. Progress without rest becomes restlessness; advancement without integration becomes a kind of running.

## What This Gate Brings

Gate 35 brings the hunger for new experience. The person with this gate activated is a natural traveler, a try-everything-once, a multi-passionate, a polymath. They get bored quickly, not because they are shallow but because their genetic function is to keep moving — keep collecting, keep tasting, keep reporting back.

The gift is the ability to translate experience into advice. The 35 is one of the few gates that can legitimately say "let me tell you what that's actually like." When mature, this person is the trusted friend you call before a big decision, because they have done a version of it themselves and can speak to it.

"This is the gate of progress. It is the voice that says, I feel. It is the voice of experience itself." — Ra Uru Hu

The 35 carries a deep secret: that growth happens through the willingness to be uncomfortable. The boredom of the 35 is not a flaw; it is a signal that the current cycle is complete and a new one is ready to begin — when [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] confirm it.

## The Shadow / Not-Self Expression

The shadow of Gate 35 is the addict of experience. The mind tells the person they must keep doing new things, must keep jumping, must never settle. The result is a life that looks adventurous on the surface but is emotionally hollow underneath — partner after partner, job after job, country after country, none of them integrated.

The [[Not-Self]] expression of the 35 is the person who initiates new experiences without waiting for them to be correct. They burn through relationships, projects, and identities at a rate their nervous system cannot metabolize. They mistake change for progress. They confuse motion with growth.

The 35 in shadow also produces deep boredom and a complacency in the opposite extreme — the person who has been burned so many times by inauthentic experience that they stop seeking entirely. Both extremes — the addict and the stagnated — come from the same root: the gate being run by the mind instead of by correct authority.

## The Six Lines

### Line 1 — Humility

The first line approaches new experience with humility. There is no rush, no demand to be advanced. Line 1 in the 35 is the beginner who knows they are a beginner and learns thoroughly before claiming expertise. In shadow, line 1 becomes the person who never feels they have enough experience to speak.

### Line 2 — Creativity

The second line brings creative engagement with experience. Each new thing becomes raw material for invention. Line 2 in the 35 is the person who, having tried something, immediately sees how it could be done differently — and tries that next.

### Line 3 — Collaboration

The third line learns through experimentation, often with others. Line 3 in the 35 is the natural partner-up-and-try-it person. The wisdom is in the collision with collaborators; the shadow is in becoming the person who never finishes anything because they keep moving to the next collaboration.

### Line 4 — Hunger

The fourth line carries the deepest hunger of all six. Line 4 in the 35 is the friend who calls at midnight saying "I have to do this thing." They cannot rest until the next experience is initiated. The work of this line is to learn that hunger does not equal correctness.

### Line 5 — Generalist

The fifth line is the jack of all trades, master of none — and the wisdom that comes with that breadth. Line 5 in the 35 is the person who has done a little of everything and can speak credibly to all of it. In shadow, the projection lands as "dilettante."

### Line 6 — Rectification

The sixth line corrects course after enough experience has accumulated. Line 6 in the 35 is the elder who, having tried it all, settles into the role of advisor. They no longer need to do every new thing; they have done enough to know what is worth recommending and what is not.

## In the BodyGraph

Gate 35 in the [[Throat]] is a manifesting gate when connected to a motor — meaning when [[Channel 35/36]] is fully defined, the 35 brings emotional motor energy directly to the voice. This person speaks from feeling. They tell you what it was like, and you can hear the weather of the experience in their voice.

When 35 is defined and 36 is open, the person has the voice but is collecting other people's feeling-experiences to speak through. When 36 is defined and 35 is open, the person feels the crises but does not have the easy translation to voice — they may need to write or paint instead of speak.

The 35 is a [[Conditioning]] hot spot for anyone with an open Throat. People without 35 defined will often find themselves saying "I feel" in proximity to a 35 person and may chase experiences that are not theirs to chase. The [[Signature]] of the correctly-lived 35 is satisfaction in the breadth of life; the [[Not-Self]] theme is the frustration of motion that never becomes meaning.

Gate 35 is the upper half of one of the three [[Collective Circuit]] channels feeding the Throat. It is the experiential complement to the abstract recognition of [[Gate 36]] below it.
