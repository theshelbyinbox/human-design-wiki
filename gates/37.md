# Gate 37 — The Family

Gate 37 is the Gate of Friendship, also called The Family. It sits at the Solar Plexus and is the emotional glue of the tribe — the warmth that binds people through clear agreements, physical affection, and mutual loyalty. This is the gate of the handshake, the cuddle, the shared meal, the deal that holds because everyone trusts everyone else to keep their word.

## Mechanics

- **Center:** [[Solar Plexus]]
- **Circuit:** [[Tribal Circuit]] (Defense / Ego stream)
- **Channel:** [[Channel 37/40]] — Community, a design of being a part in search of a whole
- **Hexagram:** 37, Chia Jen — The Family / The Clan
- **Transit:** Approximately February 24 – March 1

Gate 37 is the upper gate of the Community channel, sitting at the bottom-right of the [[Solar Plexus]]. It is the emotional half of a tribal bargain — the warmth, peace, and physical tenderness that makes community feel like home. Its counterpart, Gate 40, sits at the [[Heart/Ego]] and provides the willpower to deliver on what the 37 has promised.

## The Hexagram

Hexagram 37 in the I Ching is Chia Jen, "The Family." The image is fire within the wind — the warmth at the center of the household that radiates outward. The Wilhelm-Baynes commentary describes a well-ordered family in which each member knows their place, their role, and their obligations to the others. The hexagram is about correct relationship within the small group.

This is not a hexagram of romantic love or individual passion. It is the wisdom of clan — the understanding that survival is collective, that affection is a daily practice, and that the strength of the family is the foundation of everything else.

## What This Gate Brings

Gate 37 brings the capacity for friendship in its oldest, most physical sense — the friendship that is sealed with a handshake, the kinship that is maintained through shared meals, the loyalty that is renewed through hugs and touch and presence. The 37 person is the one who keeps the family together emotionally, who remembers the birthdays, who hosts the dinner, who notices when someone needs to be held.

The gift is a deep capacity for clear agreements. The 37 understands intuitively that community runs on contracts — not legal documents but emotional commitments. "I will do this for you. You will do this for me. We will both keep our word." Where these agreements are clear, the 37 thrives. Where they are vague, the 37 leaks energy.

"The 37 is the gate of friendship. It is also the gate of touch. It is what holds the family together — the physicality of the tribe." — Ra Uru Hu

The 37 person is also a natural cuddler. Affection is not optional for this gate; it is fuel. Hugs, handshakes, massages, sex, the casual touch of a hand on a shoulder — these are the daily nutrients of a 37 in correct alignment.

## The Shadow / Not-Self Expression

The shadow of Gate 37 is self-sacrifice. The [[Not-Self]] mind tells the 37 person that to be a good family member, they must give without limit. They take on everyone's needs, they stay loyal to relationships that have become exploitative, they enter agreements that drain them, and they cannot say no because saying no feels like betraying the tribe.

The result is exhaustion and bitterness. The 37 in shadow is the person who has given everything and is now resentful that no one is giving back. The original problem is not the takers; it is the failure to maintain clear agreements at the outset. The 37 must learn that a deal is only correct if it serves both parties — and that loyalty to a one-sided agreement is not loyalty, it is depletion.

In the deepest shadow, the 37 person can become a manipulator — using emotional warmth and the appearance of family loyalty to extract from others. Or they can project their fears onto the family, becoming the anxious one who polices everyone's commitments. Both extremes come from the gate being run by the mind instead of by [[Emotional Authority]].

## The Six Lines

### Line 1 — The Mother / Father

The first line is the foundation of the family — the parent role, regardless of biology. Line 1 in the 37 is the natural caregiver, the one others bring their problems to. The shadow is in being so absorbed in caregiving that the self disappears.

### Line 2 — Responsibility

The second line takes on the responsibility of the family without being asked. Line 2 in the 37 is the one who notices what needs doing and does it. The shadow is in resenting the responsibility while continuing to take it on.

### Line 3 — Evenhandedness

The third line learns through trial and error what makes a fair deal. Line 3 in the 37 will enter into agreements that fail and, through those failures, develop the wisdom of evenhanded contracts. The shadow is in cycling through the same broken deals without learning.

### Line 4 — Leadership

The fourth line leads the family by example. Line 4 in the 37 is the elder sibling, the trusted aunt or uncle, the one whose word is law because they have earned trust over time. The shadow is in becoming bossy or controlling.

### Line 5 — Love

The fifth line embodies the love of the family in its purest form. Line 5 in the 37 is the heart of the clan — the one whose mere presence reminds everyone why they are family. The projection on this line is intense and can be misunderstood as romantic or exclusive when it is simply universal.

### Line 6 — Purpose

The sixth line carries the larger purpose of the family beyond the family itself. Line 6 in the 37 is the one who extends tribal warmth into the wider community — the elder who has integrated family love into a broader expression of belonging.

## In the BodyGraph

Gate 37 in the [[Solar Plexus]] is the emotional warmth half of the [[Tribal Circuit]]'s defense expression. When [[Channel 37/40]] is fully defined, the person carries the complete community bargain — the warmth of the 37 paired with the willpower of the 40 — and is fundamentally designed to be a part of a clan.

When 37 is defined and 40 is open, the person has the affectionate, agreement-making warmth but must connect with others to find the will that delivers on the promises. When 40 is defined and 37 is open, the person has the will to provide but is searching for the warmth that makes the community feel like home.

The 37 is a key piece of the broader [[Tribal Circuit]] — the circuit governed by physical touch, sex, food, money, and resource-sharing. Anyone with the 37 defined has a stake in the maintenance of clan structures. Anyone with the 37 open will be conditioned by these structures and must learn discernment about which families and which agreements are correct for them.

The [[Signature]] of the correctly-lived 37 is peace in community; the [[Not-Self]] theme is anxiety about who is honoring the agreements and who is not. The peace is available — but only through clear deals, made through [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]].
