# Gates Overview

Gates are the 64 archetypal energies that form the building blocks of the [[Human Design System]] BodyGraph. Each gate corresponds to one of the 64 hexagrams of the Chinese I Ching and occupies a specific position in the Rave Mandala (the wheel). Gates are the points at which planetary imprinting enters the chart and the points at which energy flows between centers through channels.

## Structure

The 64 gates are distributed across the nine centers. Each center contains between 3 and 11 gates. A gate sits at one end of a channel; the other end of the channel connects to an adjacent center. Each gate has a partner gate at the opposite end of its channel.

When a gate is activated (defined) -- either by birth imprinting or by a transiting planet -- the gate's energy is available. When both gates of a channel are defined simultaneously (by any combination of Personality imprinting, Design imprinting, and/or transits), the channel is active and both connected centers become defined.

## Defined vs. Undefined Gates

A gate that is defined in someone's chart but whose channel partner is not defined is called a "hanging gate" or "half-channel." The person carries the energy of that gate consistently but does not have the full channel activated. Hanging gates seek their partner gate in others and in the transit field -- this is one of the mechanisms of attraction and conditioning.

"This hanging gate just sits there, out of the center, dangling, always looking to the other side, always dreaming that it could be the thing that it is not." - Ra Uru Hu

## I Ching Correspondence

The 64 hexagrams of the I Ching map directly onto the 64 gates. The themes and imagery of the hexagrams inform the basic meaning of each gate. Each hexagram has six lines, and each gate in Human Design also has six lines -- giving 384 distinct gate-line combinations. The specific line activated by the birth positions shapes the nuance of how the gate expresses.

## Genetic Code

The 64 hexagrams also correspond to the 64 codons of the human genetic code. This correspondence is central to Ra Uru Hu's framing of Human Design as a synthesis of ancient wisdom and modern science.

## Gates and Profile

The line number of the Personality Sun gate and the Design Sun gate together form the Profile (e.g., if the Personality Sun is in line 3 and the Design Sun is in line 6, the profile is 3/6).

## Gates and Incarnation Cross

The four gates formed by Personality Sun, Personality Earth, Design Sun, and Design Earth constitute the Incarnation Cross -- the person's overarching life theme. See [[Incarnation Cross Overview]].

## Circuit Structure

Gates belong to circuits -- larger thematic groupings that describe how energy flows through the BodyGraph at a collective level. The three main circuit groups are:

- **Individual Circuit**: Gates associated with individual mutation, self-empowerment, and the "knowing" intelligence. Individual circuits can feel isolating or melancholic because they carry awareness before it is understood by others.
- **Tribal Circuit**: Gates associated with support, bonding, family, community, and the sustaining of the gene pool. Tribal energy is oriented toward the group.
- **Collective Circuit**: Gates associated with sharing experience and abstraction across the collective. Collective energy is oriented toward humanity as a whole.

## Planetary Imprinting

At the moment of birth (Personality calculation) and approximately 88 days before birth (Design calculation), the Sun, Moon, and eight planets each occupy specific gate positions in the wheel. These positions imprint the gates they occupy into the person's chart. The Sun's position is the most influential (approximately 70 percent of the imprint weight).
