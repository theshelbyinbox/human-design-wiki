# Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 2

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 2 — named in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Detail — is the fixed-fate variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 2. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 62]] (Preponderance of the Small) at line 4, and the whole architecture of the cross is locked into the accent of detail. Juxtaposition crosses are the rarest cross type, present only in the 4/1 profile, and they represent a pure, concentrated expression of a single thematic accent. In the Quarter of Civilization, with its purpose anchored in Form, the Juxtaposition of Detail is the fixed life-task of being the namer in a small, networked context.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 62]] (Preponderance of the Small / Detail), Line 4
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth), Line 4
- Design Sun: [[Gate 42]] or [[Gate 32]], Line 1
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun, Line 1
- Personality Axis: 62 / 61
- Design Axis: 42 / 32
- Quarter: Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe)
- Realm Purpose: Form
- Mystical Theme: From Womb to Room
- Angle Expression: Juxtaposition — fixed fate
- Profile: 4/1 only

## The Four Gates

### Gate 62 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 62]] in the Juxtaposition is the fixed orientation toward detail-naming. At line 4, the gate carries Asceticism — the relational, externalized expression of precision. The carrier names details in service of a network, a team, a family, a small community. The 4/1 profile makes this accent fully social: the carrier's precision is not for themselves but for the chosen circle they belong to. Ra emphasized that the maya grows denser through naming, and the Juxtaposition of Detail is a fixed contribution to that growth — one carrier, one set of bonded others, one continuous stream of accurate names.

### Gate 61 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 61]] at line 4 is Research — the most relational and methodical expression of mystical pressure. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's life of precise naming is rooted in a quiet, ongoing investigation of what sits beneath the names. The 4 line networks this investigation through chosen community. The Juxtaposition fixity means this is a constant, lifelong undercurrent: the carrier names, while simultaneously researching the mystery the names point at.

### Gate 42 (Design)

[[Gate 42]] in the design imprint at line 1 is Diversification — the foundational sacral capacity to spread energy across multiple beginnings. The body of the Juxtaposition Maya 2 carrier knows that the detail-work must respect the natural rhythm of cycles, and at line 1 it has a built-in willingness to start many small detail-tracks rather than committing too early to any one of them.

### Gate 32 (Design)

[[Gate 32]] at line 1 is Conservation — the foundational expression of splenic continuity-judgment. The body has a built-in capacity to preserve what is essential and let the inessential go. In the Maya 2 Juxtaposition, this means the carrier's detail-work is filtered, beneath consciousness, by a quiet sense of what is worth preserving on the record.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the quarter of Form. Every gate of the [[Throat Center]] sits in this quarter, which makes it the realm of voice, articulation, manifestation, and the building of structures. The mystical theme is From Womb to Room: emergence from hidden potential into materially expressed life. The Maya cross in this quarter is the most naturally seated expression of the maya's nature — civilization itself is a vast collection of named things, and the Maya 2 cross is the cross of the namer.

The Juxtaposition of Detail accents this work into pure focus. The carrier is not the broad-scope teacher or the universalizing leader. They are the precise contributor whose specific detail-work belongs in a specific network at a specific time.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. Ra Uru Hu described the Juxtapositions as pure accents — concentrated, narrow, locked expressions of the cross's underlying theme. The 4/1 profile that carries every Juxtaposition is the most fixed profile in Human Design: line 4 is the Opportunist whose life unfolds through friendship and network, line 1 is the Investigator whose foundation is built through deep solitary study. Together, the 4/1 is the person who networks their foundation — the social investigator.

Juxtaposition Maya 2 fuses this fixed profile to a specific accent: detail. The carrier's life is locked into the role of precise namer within a specific network. The mind will want broader scope; the cross teaches narrower focus. Inside the accent, the carrier can be unusually accurate and reliable. Reaching beyond the accent typically produces frustration.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 2 manifests only in the 4/1 profile. The 4/1 is the most genetically fixed profile in the system. Its line 4 is the externalized, networked, social-opportunity expression. Its line 1 is the deep foundational study that must happen in private. Together they produce a person whose foundational work matters to a specific small community and whose social field is structured around that community's needs.

In Maya 2 Juxtaposition, this profile is further fixed into the accent of detail. The 4/1 carrier becomes the trusted detail-keeper inside their network — the friend who has the dates, the spreadsheet, the verified facts, the precise specification.

## Living This Cross

Living the Juxtaposition of Detail well means honoring the fixity of the accent. The 4/1 profile already encourages a fixed life-path; the Juxtaposition locks it further. The carrier's [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] operate inside a narrow band of correct expression: this small network, this specific kind of detail-work, this consistent quiet investigation.

The mind will rebel against the smallness of the role. It will want to reach for the broader civilization-scale work the Right Angle Maya 2 carrier does, or the transpersonal teaching the Left Angle Maya 2 carrier does. The cross teaches the opposite: stay inside the accent. The Juxtaposition's gift is depth in a narrow range, not breadth across a wide one.

The 4/1 line structure makes the network the practical operating field. The carrier's detail-naming is not abstract; it is for the people the carrier is already bonded to. These are the people who recognize when the precision matters. These are the people whose use of the detail completes the carrier's purpose.

The 61 Earth keeps the work humble. The detail is for the network, but the mystery is always larger than the names. The Juxtaposition Maya 2 carrier who forgets this becomes the pedant inside their friend group — the one whose precision becomes a control device. The one who remembers it becomes the trusted precise friend whose work is meaningful precisely because it is offered with respect for what lies beneath the names.

The deeper teaching is that fixity is precision. The Juxtaposition Maya 2 carrier has a smaller, narrower life than the Right Angle or Left Angle variants of the same cross, but inside their accent they can be sharper, more reliable, more deeply embedded in the practical fabric of their network than either. Civilization is built out of small, precise contributions in small, precise networks — and the Juxtaposition of Detail is the cross of that contribution.
