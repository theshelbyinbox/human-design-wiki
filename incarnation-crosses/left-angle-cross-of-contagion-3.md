# Left Angle Cross of Contagion 3

The Left Angle Cross of Contagion 3 is the transpersonal expression of the Contagion gate set in the Quarter of Duality. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Left Angle Cross of Industry 2. Where the Quarter 1 Industry was about turning the fates into livelihood, the Quarter 3 Industry is about selling the fantasy of duality — the overreach of the 29.5 line, the industriousness that promises a soul mate around the corner if you only keep working at it.

The one-line theme: the industry of overreach. The carrier mobilises others to commit to bonds, ideals, and partners that lie just beyond what is actually available.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 2 variant keeps the Personality axis 29–30 of the Right Angle Contagion 3 but rotates the Design axis from the abstract pair (14–8) to the individual pair (55–59). This is a major mechanical shift. The body is no longer fuelled by the collective experiential channel; it is fuelled by individual mutating spirit. The carrier's industriousness in selling duality is driven not by collective want but by individual emotional pulse.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 29 (Personality Sun) — The Abysmal

The sacral yes in the conscious position. The carrier of Industry 2 leads with the yes, but in the Left Angle the yes is transpersonal. They commit to relationships, ideals, and fantasies in order to teach others how to commit. Their conscious life is the lived demonstration of the commitment industry.

### Gate 30 (Personality Earth) — The Clinging Fire

The fire of desire grounds the consciousness. The carrier's commitments are visibly desire-fuelled — their fire becomes the inducement for others to commit similarly.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The unconscious engine is the moody emotional pulse of individual spirit. Beneath the conscious commitments, the body is animated by the 55's fickleness about whether spirit is present. This is what makes Industry 2 different from Industry 1: the carrier is not selling industriousness in general but the industriousness of finding spiritual fulfilment through bonded experience.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The body's intimate biology. The 59 grounds the design in the sexual and intimate field. The Left Angle Industry 2 carrier teaches the industry of duality through their own intimate life — through visible love affairs, marriages, partnerships that demonstrate the overreach in action.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. Within this quarter, Industry 2 serves a specific cultural function: it builds the infrastructure of romantic overreach. The dating industry, the perfume industry, the modelling industry, the wedding industry, the relationship-coaching industry — Ra named these as expressions of the 29.5 line, the line of overreach that says yes to more than can be delivered.

The Left Angle Industry 2 carrier is the architect of that infrastructure. They are the entrepreneurs and sellers of romantic possibility, the publishers of dating advice, the matchmakers and influencers and image-makers. They sell the fantasy of duality because their own design is committed to that fantasy.

Ra was specific that this is a heretical configuration. The orthodoxy of the tribe says marriages are sacred, bonds are permanent, love is sufficient. The 29.5 heretic says: you can keep looking, keep upgrading, keep buying the next product that promises the better partner.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is shaped by what others need from the carrier. For Industry 2, the transpersonal pull is the mobilising of others into committed pursuit of duality. The carrier is here to keep humanity reaching for connection — even when (especially when) the connection cannot deliver what is promised.

Ra named this configuration as one where the heresy is the gift. The honest Industry 2 carrier sells fantasies because the fantasy is what keeps the species reproducing, bonding, mating, repairing the always-broken duality. Without the overreach, the bonded life would collapse under its own disappointment.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who sells the fantasy cynically — who knows the product cannot deliver and sells it anyway. This is the toxic Industry 2: the predatory dating coach, the manipulative matchmaker, the salesman of impossible bonds. The correct Industry 2 sells the fantasy with their own life as the proof — they are visibly reaching, visibly committing, visibly disappointed when the reach fails, and visibly reaching again. The heresy is honest.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Industry 2 manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra described the 5/1 Industry 2 as the heretic who sells the universal product of romance — Hugh Hefner was an example he named. The 5/2 sells fantasy privately, often without the public infrastructure. The 6/2 Industry 2, when awake, recognises that the duality cannot be perfected and offers a more sophisticated product: the love of intimacy itself, not the partner who provides it. The 6/3 closes the Left Angle and is the bridge to the next Right Angle.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept that the life is transpersonal. The carrier of Industry 2 cannot avoid the cultural function. Their work, their intimate life, their public presence will be involved in the broader human industry of romantic overreach.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] either becomes the cynical seller (using the design to extract from others) or the burned-out idealist (collapsing into bitter rejection of the bonded life). Living from [[Signature]] means being honest about the overreach — selling the fantasy as fantasy, committing to the reach as the work of the life, and accepting the disappointments without weaponising them.

The deepest practice for this cross is treating the overreach as the function rather than the flaw. The carrier of Industry 2 was designed to want more bonded fulfilment than the duality can deliver. This is not a personal failing; it is the mechanism by which the design contributes to the larger movement of the human species through its bonding work. The lifelong task is to reach honestly, fail honestly, reach again — and to let one's own visible life become the licence others need to reach in their own.
