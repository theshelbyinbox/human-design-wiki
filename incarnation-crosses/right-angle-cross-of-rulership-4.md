# Right Angle Cross of Rulership 4

The Right Angle Cross of Rulership 4 is the fourth and final variant of the Rulership name, sitting in the Quarter of Mutation with the Personality Sun in Gate 26 — The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist. Where the previous Rulership variants moved through mental rulership (Q1), material rulership (Q2), and bonded rulership (Q3), Rulership 4 is the rulership of mutation — the egoic trickster authority that drives transformation through the salesmanship of what is new.

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm where purpose is fulfilled through Transformation. Every cross in this quarter is a mutative cross, oriented toward the change that breaks open the established order. For Rulership 4, this means the rulership operates not through tradition (Q1), gathering (Q2), or partnership (Q3), but through the egoic capacity to sell the mutation itself — to make the new acceptable, to package transformation in a story the tribe can receive.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 26 (The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 45 (Gathering Together / The King)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 47 (Realization / Oppression)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 22 (Grace / Openness)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose: Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis is the tribal axis 26/45 — the salesman and the king. The Design axis is the abstract mental axis 47/22 — the realization and the grace. The carrier consciously identifies as the egoic operator, with the kingly entitlement grounding the conscious orientation; the unconscious carries the realization and the listening grace.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 26 (Personality Sun) — The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist

[[Gate 26]] is the Heart Center gate of the salesman, the marketer, the propagandist of the tribe. Ra named this position the trickster's authority and noted that this is the most ego-forward variant of the rulership theme. The carrier sells the mutation. They take what is new, dangerous, or hard to swallow, and they find the angle that makes the audience want it.

The shadow of Gate 26 is manipulation, exaggeration, and the willingness to tell people what they want to hear rather than what they need to hear. The gift is the capacity to package complex truths in language that ordinary people can follow. On Rulership 4, this packaging is in service of mutation — the carrier sells transformation, sells change, sells the new direction.

### Gate 45 (Personality Earth) — Gathering Together / The King

[[Gate 45]] grounds the conscious mind in the natural rulership voice of the tribe. The Rulership 4 being is consciously aware of their entitlement, their gathering function, and their authority to lead. Together, the 26 and 45 produce the conscious identity of the trickster king — the ruler who sells, the salesman who rules.

### Gate 47 (Design Sun) — Realization / Oppression

[[Gate 47]] is the unconscious driver. The being carries the abstract sense-making function at the level of the design — the realization that arrives when the past suddenly arranges into meaning. On Rulership 4, this provides the substrate of insight that informs the conscious selling. The trickster king knows things they cannot articulate, and the 47 is the source of that knowing.

### Gate 22 (Design Earth) — Grace / Openness

[[Gate 22]] grounds the unconscious in the emotional grace of selective listening. The Rulership 4 being has the listening grace operating beneath conscious awareness, which means they attract trust through emotional reception they themselves are not fully tracking.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm where Transformation is the purpose. Crosses in this quarter are not concerned with maintaining what exists; they are concerned with mutating what exists into something new. For Rulership 4, this means the rulership theme becomes mutative. The carrier is not the traditional ruler holding the established order together; they are the trickster ruler selling the next iteration.

This is why Ra named the cross with reference to the trickster archetype. The trickster is the figure in myth who upends the established order through cunning, wit, and the willingness to be ethically flexible. The Rulership 4 carrier brings this energy to the rulership theme.

## The Angle Expression

Right Angle Crosses carry Personal Destiny. The Rulership 4 being is here to live the experience of mutative rulership — to discover through direct experience what it means to be the egoic salesman of transformation. The personal destiny is the lifelong work of finding the right packaging, the right timing, the right audience for the mutation the carrier is structurally built to deliver.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The six Right Angle profiles produce six distinct flavors of Rulership 4.

The 1/3 investigates the foundations of salesmanship through trial and error. The carrier tests what works, what fails, what sells. The 1/4 inherits the egoic style of the dominant parent and replicates it through their network. The 2/4 turns the trickster energy inward, often becoming the quiet salesman who works behind the scenes through the network. The 2/5 is projected onto as a natural egoic authority but rarely accepts the role unless self-motivated. The 3/5 lives the trial-and-error of egoic selling, often producing both spectacular wins and spectacular failures of reputation. The 3/6 carries the experimental phase fully and matures into the senior advisor who teaches others how to package mutation.

## Living This Cross

For the Rulership 4 being, the work is to honor the trickster role without becoming the con artist. The 26 will sell — that is its nature — and the question is what is being sold and for whose benefit. Ra noted that the 26 in shadow becomes the manipulator who uses egoic power to extract advantage without contributing back. The 26 in gift becomes the educator-salesman who can move resources, ideas, and energy toward genuinely transformative purposes.

The 45 grounding gives the conscious mind the tribal voice and the entitlement that makes the selling effective. The carrier feels entitled to lead, entitled to gather, entitled to direct the mutation. This entitlement is operational rather than personal — it is what makes the cross work — but it must be balanced by the 45's deeper recognition that the king who does not give back loses the throne.

The shadow path is the not-self trickster who sells without integrity, who uses egoic authority to manipulate the tribe, who confuses personal advantage with collective mutation. The cross can produce both the genuine reformer who sells transformation to a resistant audience and the slick operator who sells whatever produces the most return.

The medicine is to honor Strategy and Inner Authority, and to let the unconscious 47/22 guide the conscious 26/45. The realization arrives when it arrives; forcing the answer produces the oppression. The grace of selective listening is operational; pretending to listen without actually receiving produces the hollow charm that the carrier eventually cannot sustain.

When integrated, the Rulership 4 carrier becomes one of the most effective mutative voices on the wheel. They sell what needs to be sold — the new direction, the next iteration, the transformation that the tribe cannot yet see — and they do so with the egoic authority that makes the selling credible and the tribal voice that makes the message comprehensible. This is the trickster as initiator, the salesman as midwife of mutation, the ruler whose rulership is in service of the change that must come.
