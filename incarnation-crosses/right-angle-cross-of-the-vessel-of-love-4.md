# Right Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 4

The Right Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 4 places the Personality Sun in Gate 10 — Treading, the love of the self — at the midpoint of the Quarter of Mutation. This is the fourth and final Vessel of Love cross on the wheel, and the variation Ra Uru Hu treated as the most foundational of the four loves. Where Quarter 1 holds universal love as mental seed, Quarter 2 holds love of humanity through rhythm, and Quarter 3 holds love of the body through serendipity, Quarter 4 holds love of self — the love Ra placed at the base of the entire architecture. Without love of self, none of the other loves becomes possible. Without love of self, universal love is a performance, love of humanity is tolerance through gritted teeth, and love of the body is body management.

Carriers of this cross arrive with a personal destiny built around the slow, demanding work of treading on the ground of their own life with respect for who they are. The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. The Vessel of Love 4 carrier is here to mutate culture by being themselves correctly.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 10 — Treading / Behavior of the Self
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 15 — Modesty
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 — Innocence
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 — Pushing Upward
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 10–15 is the consciousness axis: love of the self anchored by love of humanity. The Design axis 25–46 is the unconscious vehicle: universal love poured into the body. Together the four gates form the complete Vessel — and in Quarter 4, the lead is the mouth of the vessel, the gate from which love pours.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 10 (Personality Sun) — Treading

The conscious work of self-love. Ra Uru Hu called Gate 10 one of his favorite gates and the foundational love of the four. The carrier of this cross is here to learn that loving oneself is not a feeling but a way of walking — a moment-to-moment behavior of treating one's own form with respect. The 10 is treading on the tail of the tiger: living with one's own dangerous, particular, individual nature without performing virtue and without flinching.

### Gate 15 (Personality Earth) — Modesty

Love of humanity as the conscious ground. The 15 here ensures that the love of the self does not isolate the carrier. Their self-love is connected, through the 15's wide aura, to the love of the wider human field. The carrier's individual mutation contributes to the species' mutation. Their permission to be themselves becomes other people's permission too.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence

Universal love beneath the conscious work. The 25 keeps the 10's self-love innocent — uncalculated, unperformed, unmotivated. The carrier's self-love is fuelled by the same cool, undiscriminating love that fills the entire Vessel. They love themselves the way they would love a stranger.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward

The body as the unconscious vehicle. The 46 ensures that the self-love is embodied — that the carrier's love of themselves is delivered through how they treat their actual flesh, hour by hour. The body is the temple in which the self-love happens.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to change what has been built. Every cross in Quarter 4 contributes to that mutative work. The Vessel of Love 4 contributes the mutation of love itself — the rediscovery that the human animal can love itself without shame, without performance, and without the cultural conditioning that has spent centuries persuading it otherwise.

Ra was clear that Quarter 4 is the close of the wheel, the place where the work of incarnation completes itself and prepares to begin again. The Vessel of Love 4 is the heart of that completion. The carrier is here to seal the work of love by mutating its base — the self — into something honest.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is a cross of personal destiny. The life belongs to the carrier. Their work is the personal recovery of self-love after years of conditioning has taught them to override their own form.

For Vessel of Love 4, this means the carrier is not here to teach self-love. They are here to live it as a slow, private practice. The not-self distortion is the carrier who performs self-love — who narrates it on social media, who turns it into a brand, who collects evidence of their own goodness. Ra called this the publicising of selflessness in the context of Gate 25, but the same dynamic applies to Gate 10. Self-love performed is self-love hollowed.

The correct expression is treading. Walking through one's own life, one foot in front of the other, with respect for the form one is in. No performance. No commentary. Just correct behavior with oneself, day after day, until the cumulative behavior becomes the love.

Right Angles do not exist to find someone else's secret. They exist to do the personal work of becoming who they were designed to be. For this cross, that work is the slow undoing of every conditioning that taught the carrier to override the body — the diets that ignored hunger, the schedules that ignored fatigue, the relationships that ignored their truth, the careers that ignored their nature.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle of this cross is carried by the six classical right-angle profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5 and 3/6.

- **1/3** investigates the self — its needs, its responses, its difference from others — and learns by trial which behaviors actually honor the form
- **1/4** investigates within a friendship network, becoming the trusted source of insight on how to be oneself
- **2/4** is the natural hermit whose individual difference is recognised by friends who come to learn what authentic conduct looks like
- **2/5** is the called hermit who is projected upon as a model of self-love and must protect the practice from being co-opted into a brand
- **3/5** is the trial-and-error mutator whose self-love is forged through bonded failure — the relationships that demanded the carrier abandon themselves and showed them, by the cost, why they could not
- **3/6** is the role-modelling experimenter who moves through three life phases — trial, observation, role model — and eventually becomes the wise demonstration of self-love in maturation

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only reliable navigation system. The carrier's [[Type]] determines how the self-love lands correctly. A Generator responds with the sacral to what is actually theirs to do. A Projector waits to be recognised as the carrier of correct conduct. A Manifestor informs before the conduct cuts across someone else's life. A Reflector samples the lunar cycle and lets the rhythms of the field reveal what was authentically theirs.

The signature of this cross lived well is steady, unforced selfhood. The carrier becomes recognisable as themselves. Their conduct is consistent across contexts. They do not perform different selves for different audiences. They tread on the ground of their own life, and the ground holds.

The not-self signature is shame, self-criticism, and the chronic sense that one is doing it wrong. This is what happens when the mind is in charge of the self-love. The mind cannot love. It can only judge. The recovery is to come back to the body and let the [[Inner Authority]] guide the conduct.

When the carrier honours this cross, they become what Ra called the natural example — the everyday demonstration that a human being can love itself without being narcissistic, can be different without being defensive, can occupy its own form without apology. People near a correctly operating Vessel of Love 4 carrier often feel permission to come back to themselves. They have not been instructed. They have been shown.

This is the cross of the integrated self — the love that begins where all the other loves must begin. Lived correctly, it is a quiet, individual, mutative life. Lived incorrectly, it is a performance of selfhood that exhausts the carrier and persuades no one. The difference is whether the love is in the treading or in the talking.
