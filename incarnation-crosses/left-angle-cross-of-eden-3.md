# Left Angle Cross of the Plane 2

The Left Angle Cross of the Plane 2 is the transpersonal extension of the Eden 3 gate set in the Quarter of Duality. The Personality Sun in Gate 36 turns crisis into the conscious instrument, but with the Design axis rotated to the Vessel of Love axis 25/46, the entire cross is reorganized around witnessing the plane of human bonded experience through the lens of universal love and embodied determination.

Where the Plane 1 cross of the Quarter of Initiation witnesses the plane from the mental side — seeing the structures of inexperience and the foundations of human suffering — the Plane 2 cross of the Quarter of Duality witnesses the plane from the bonded side. The carrier sees what bonded life actually looks like across the human family, and the seeing is the work.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 36 (Crisis / Darkening of the Light)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 6 (Conflict / Friction)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 (Innocence / The Spirit of the Self)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 (Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose: Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Personality axis is the Solar Plexus emotional axis 36/6 — crisis and friction. The Design axis is the Vessel of Love axis 25/46. The conscious mind leads with crisis grounded in friction; the unconscious carries universal love and embodied determination.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 36 (Personality Sun) — Crisis / Darkening of the Light

[[Gate 36]] is the Solar Plexus gate of crisis. As the conscious lead of the Plane 2 cross, Gate 36 places the entire identity at the apex of the witnessing function. The carrier sees the crisis of human bonded life — not as abstract knowledge but as the lived texture of the plane.

### Gate 6 (Personality Earth) — Conflict / Friction

[[Gate 6]] grounds the conscious mind in friction. The Plane 2 being witnesses the friction of bonded life — the conflicts that fill human partnerships, families, communities — and the witnessing is the structural orientation of the cross.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence / The Spirit of the Self

[[Gate 25]] is the unconscious G Center gate of universal love. The Plane 2 being carries the innocent love at the level of design. This love operates beneath conscious awareness and grounds the witnessing in care rather than in detachment.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self

[[Gate 46]] grounds the unconscious in the love of the body. The witness is embodied. They see the plane from within a body that has lived bonded life itself.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality places this cross in the realm of Bonding. The Plane 2 being is built to witness bonded life across the human family — to see the patterns, the suffering, the rare beauty, and to report on what bonded existence actually looks like.

Ra connected this cross to the pursuit of knowledge that emerges in the second half of the Quarter of Duality. Where the first half is concerned with the bond itself, the second half moves toward the recognition that one's partner is also one's teacher, and that knowledge gained through bonded life is one of the most important forms of knowledge available to humans.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle Crosses carry Transpersonal Karma. The Plane 2 being witnesses on behalf of the collective. The karma flows through the carrier as a transpersonal current that compels the witnessing regardless of conscious choice.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The five Left Angle profiles produce five distinct flavors of Plane 2.

The 4/6 witnesses through their network — they see the plane of bonded life as it manifests in their close circle of friends, family, and partners. The 5/1 is the public witness — the writer, researcher, or speaker whose witnessing reaches the universal field; Ra noted that this is the only true exponent of the Left Angle, the one who sells the witnessing. The 5/2 sees and stays mostly silent; their rare communications carry the weight of long observation. The 6/2 is the experiential witness — the carrier who has lived through the bonded suffering, climbed onto the roof, and now speaks from accepted wisdom. The 6/3 carries the bridge between witnessing and surrendering to the structural realities of bonded life.

## Living This Cross

For the Plane 2 being, the work is to witness with love. The 36 crisis is the lens; the 6 friction is the ground; the 25 universal love is the unconscious driver; the 46 embodied determination grounds the whole. All four gates must operate together for the witnessing to be both clear-eyed and care-filled.

Ra connected the 6.5 line — Arbitration — to the emergence of love in this cross. The Plane 2 carrier learns through witnessing that there is always a higher authority, that one does not learn alone, and that partnership is itself a form of teaching. The line says: "Faith derived from analytical diligence and emotional control that permits a higher authority to judge a conflict. Harmony furthered through the avoidance of direct conflict."

The shadow path is the carrier who witnesses without love — who becomes the bitter observer, the cynical critic, the disillusioned commentator who has seen too much and softened too little. The Plane 2 in shadow concludes from witnessing that bonded life is hopeless, that partnership cannot work, that the only sane response is withdrawal.

The medicine is the heretical recognition that the 6.6 line embodies — "my way or the highway." The Plane 2 carrier learns the conditions under which bonded life is workable for them and refuses to compromise on those conditions. This is not coldness; it is the clarity that comes from having witnessed enough.

Ra noted that the 5.5 line — the peacemaker, the gun that ends conflict — is also active on this cross. The carrier may serve as the one who arbitrates conflicts in their close circle, who brings the higher authority of universal love (Gate 25) into situations of bonded friction (Gate 6) and produces resolution.

When integrated, the Left Angle Cross of the Plane 2 produces a witness whose seeing serves the collective understanding of bonded life. The carrier reports on what partnership actually is — not the romantic fiction, not the cynical reduction, but the lived texture of friction, crisis, love, and arbitration that bonded human life actually contains. This is essential work, and the carrier who lives it well becomes one of the more important transpersonal voices on the wheel about the realities of intimate human existence.

The 5/1 Manifestor with this cross can produce work that reshapes how the broader culture understands bonded life. The 6/2 carrier becomes the elder whose mere presence in a community provides a model of how to witness without becoming bitter. Each profile produces a distinct expression of the same structural orientation.
