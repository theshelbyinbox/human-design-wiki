# Left Angle Cross of Laws 2

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 2 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Distraction 1 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Laws cross in Quarter 2. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 56]] (The Wanderer) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Civilization, where purpose moves through Form, the Left Angle Laws 2 is the cross of the transpersonal storyteller — the carrier whose stimulating narratives are karmically routed toward specific others whose lives need the particular stories the carrier is here to tell.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 56]] (The Wanderer / Stimulation), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 60]] (Limitation), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 3]] or [[Gate 50]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 56 / 60
- Design Axis: 3 / 50
- Quarter: Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe)
- Realm Purpose: Form
- Mystical Theme: From Womb to Room
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 56 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 56]] in the Left Angle is the storytelling capacity turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Attraction — the projection field of the expected storyteller, the keynote speaker, the bestselling author, the teacher people travel to study with. At line 6 it carries Selectivity — the mature wanderer who has learned which stories to tell, to whom, and when, whose words have become rare and weighty.

The name "Distraction" given to the Left Angle Laws 2 signals the doubled nature of the work. The 56 line 3 inside the gate itself is named Distraction, and the Left Angle expression points at how the carrier's storytelling can either distract listeners from what matters or focus them on what does — depending on whether the stories are told from Strategy and Authority or from the shadow.

### Gate 60 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 60]] at line 5 is Leadership — the practical projection of acceptance, the leader who demonstrates how to inhabit limitation productively. At line 6 it is Rigidity — the mature acceptance of certain limitations as permanent features of life. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal storytelling is rooted in lived acceptance of what is and what is not currently possible.

### Gate 3 (Design)

[[Gate 3]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the sacral mutation-pulse into the body. Line 2 is Immaturity — the body's foundational pulse that does not yet know its own depth and is often confused by others' projections. Line 3 is Survival — the line of learning by trial and error, the body's experimental engagement with mutation.

### Gate 50 (Design)

[[Gate 50]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the splenic value-awareness into the body. Line 2 is Determination — natural responsibility that does not require thought. Line 3 is Adaptability — bonds made and broken in service of values. Together these design lines provide the body's instinctive ethical compass for the transpersonal work.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization channels purpose through Form. The Laws cross in this quarter is the cross of the storyteller-lawgiver. The Left Angle variant routes the storytelling-work into transpersonal karma. The carrier's stories are karmically directed toward specific others whose lives need exactly these stories at exactly these moments.

The mystical theme of Quarter 2 is From Womb to Room — emergence from hidden potential to materially expressed life. For the Left Angle Laws 2 carrier, this emergence happens through the listeners. The carrier's narratives help other people's hidden potential take material form.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Laws 2 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, deliver specific stories that change those others' lives, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned analytically. The right encounters arrive in their own timing, and only [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] can recognize them as they appear.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 2 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator storyteller. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental life-arcs that distill into role-model storytelling in the third phase.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Laws 2 life unfolds through encounters in which the carrier's stories reorganize the listener's life. A friend stuck in a pattern hears the carrier tell a story that suddenly reframes what is possible. A community wrestling with a stuck question hears the carrier speak the metaphor that breaks the impasse. A stranger in a chance conversation receives the exact narrative their soul has been waiting for.

This is delicate work. The 56 is projected; uninvited storytelling overwhelms the listener and burns the speaker out. The shadow expression Ra named for this Left Angle is Distraction — the carrier whose stories scatter the listener rather than focus them. The work is to wait for the invitation, recognize the right moment through Strategy and Authority, and tell the story that fits.

The 5th-line projection field intensifies the karmic pressure. Others will project onto the Left Angle Laws 2 carrier the role of the one who must always have the right story, the teacher who must produce inspiration on demand. Without [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]], the carrier becomes the conscripted bard — telling stories on others' schedules and watching the depth drain out of the work.

The 60 Earth grounds the work in acceptance of limitation. Some stories cannot be told to some audiences this year. The 60's teaching is that this is not failure but the condition under which the right stories eventually find their right listeners.

The 3 design pulse gives the body a mutative undertone. The Left Angle Laws 2 carrier's stories carry a quiet edge of new ordering — they do not merely repeat existing narratives but introduce subtle mutations that shift the listener's mental field. The 50 design provides the splenic value-filter that keeps the storytelling rooted in what is worth preserving.

The deeper teaching of this cross is that the laws of a culture are stories the culture tells about itself, and some of the most important transpersonal work happens through the people who carry the right stories to the right listeners at the right moment. The Left Angle Laws 2 carrier is one of these karmic storytellers. Their own growth happens through the same encounters: every listener changes them as much as their stories change the listener.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Laws 2 person is often experienced as the voice that arrives at the right moment with the unexpected story that helps. Their presence carries weight not because of accumulated authority but because of the karmic resonance between their narratives and the specific others their life routes them toward. From Womb to Room becomes, in this cross, the lawful storytelling that helps other lives emerge into their proper form.
