# Left Angle Cross of Maya 4

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 4 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Obscuration 2 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 4. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Mutation, where purpose moves through Transformation, the Left Angle Maya 4 is the cross of the transpersonal mystic — the carrier whose 61 pressure is karmically routed toward specific people whose own mutations require the carrier's mystical attention.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 62]] (Detail), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 32]] or [[Gate 42]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 61 / 62
- Design Axis: 32 / 42
- Quarter: Quarter of Mutation (Realm of the Godhead)
- Realm Purpose: Transformation
- Mystical Theme: Accepting Death
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 61 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 61]] in the Left Angle is the inner-truth pressure turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Influence — the practical projection of mystical insight, the teacher whose framework helps others recognize their own truth. At line 6 the gate carries the Bodhisattva of Truth — the mature, withdrawn elder whose example teaches that the mystery is not meant to end. In both higher lines, the mystical work is karmically routed toward specific others.

The name "Obscuration" attached to this Left Angle variant signals the doubled nature of the work: the carrier sees through the maya for others, but the very act of attempting to communicate what is seen can obscure it further. The 61 insights do not translate cleanly into ordinary language. The carrier learns over time which moments allow the truth to land without being immediately reduced to maya by the act of speaking.

### Gate 62 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 62]] at line 5 is Metamorphosis — the projection field that makes the carrier's precise communication sought in moments of collective confusion. At line 6 it is Self-Discipline — the mature elder whose precision has become economical. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal mysticism is rooted in the discipline of precise naming. The carrier offers, to the others their karma routes them toward, both the mystical insight and the precise words that allow it to land.

### Gate 32 (Design)

[[Gate 32]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's splenic continuity-judgment into the cross. At line 2, the body has reserved, naturally-conserving instinct; at line 3, it has experimental trial-and-error continuity-learning. Either way, the design supplies the in-the-now filter that keeps the carrier's mystical insights from being conscripted into false continuity.

### Gate 42 (Design)

[[Gate 42]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the sacral cycle-completion pulse into the body. Line 2 carries Identification — the natural pull toward specific cycles the carrier is meant to mature. Line 3 carries Self-Service — the willingness to walk away from cycles that have revealed themselves to be wrong. The design 42 makes sure the body has the sustained life-force to stay inside any one transpersonal mystical cycle long enough for it to ripen.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation channels purpose through Transformation. The mystical theme of Accepting Death points at the deepest pattern of the quarter: mutation requires the death of the old form before the new can emerge. The Maya cross in this quarter is the cross of the mind that sees through the maya. The Left Angle variant routes this seeing into transpersonal karma.

The carrier is here to meet specific others whose own mutations need the carrier's mystical attention. The encounters are the life. The 61 pressure that drives the carrier's own inner-truth seeking is the same pressure that draws the karmic-others into the carrier's field — the people who need exactly this kind of mind for the next phase of their own transformation.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Maya 4 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges in the field of mystical mutation, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned. The encounters arrive in their own timing, and the work is to be ready for them — which, for the Maya 4 carrier, means being still enough that the 61 insights can land when needed.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 4 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator mystic. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental mystical life-arcs that distill into role-model wisdom.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Maya 4 life unfolds through mystical encounters. The carrier walks into a room and notices, beneath the visible surface, the mutative pulse that is trying to come through one of the people present. Speaking the right word at the right moment — invited, in correct timing — can catalyze a transformation that the other person could not initiate alone.

This is delicate, dangerous work. The 61 insights are not meant for public broadcast. They are meant for specific moments of recognition between specific people. Speaking them uninvited or to the wrong audience produces the Obscuration shadow: the truth becomes a teaching, the teaching becomes a doctrine, the doctrine becomes another layer of maya.

The 5th line projection is especially intense for the Left Angle Maya 4 carrier. Others will project onto the carrier the role of mystical authority and demand insights on schedules that are not the carrier's own. [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only protection. Without them, the carrier becomes the conscripted mystic — performing insight rather than receiving it — and the work loses its substance.

Structured stillness is essential. The 61 pressure is constant, and the karmic encounters can only land cleanly if the carrier has provided the silence in which insight arrives. Meditation, withdrawal, deep solitude — these are the practical operating conditions of the cross. Without them, the encounters degrade into performed mystical conversation.

The 62 Earth grounds the work in precise naming. The carrier's transpersonal insights are most reliable when they can be tied to specific, namable particulars. Vague spiritual generalities are the shadow; precisely named mystical recognition is the gift.

The 32 design pulse keeps the body honest about which insights are real and which are mental loops dressed as karma. The 42 design pulse keeps the carrier inside the cycles of encounter long enough for the karmic work to complete.

The deeper teaching of this cross is that the maya can be seen through by certain minds and that this seeing-through has a transpersonal function. The Left Angle Maya 4 carrier is one of the karmic agents of this function. Their own mutation, in turn, is shaped by the specific others their seeing was offered to — every encounter mutates the carrier as much as the carrier's insight mutates the other.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Maya 4 person is often experienced as the one who can finally name what no one else has been able to see. Their presence can be uncomfortable to those still committed to the maya; their absence can leave a gap that others struggle to fill. The witness, in their case, returns through the karmic encounters — accepting the death of every fixed self-image as each new meeting requires the carrier to be present in a new way.
