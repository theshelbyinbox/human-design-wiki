# Left Angle Cross of Service 4

The Left Angle Cross of Service 4 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Demands 2. The four Service gates — 58, 52, 18, 17 — rotate into a Left Angle configuration in the Quarter of Mutation, producing a transpersonal life whose karmic work is placing energetic demands on others. The Demands 2 carrier is here to require vitality from the people around them. Their love of life is so present in the body that the surrounding system has to either match the energy or admit it has been operating below its capacity. Where Demands 1 in Quarter 2 demands through stillness, Demands 2 in Quarter 4 demands through joy. The carrier's adrenal vitality is itself the demand.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 58 — The Joyous / Vitality
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 52 — Keeping Still
- **Design Sun:** Gate 18 — Work on What Has Been Spoilt
- **Design Earth:** Gate 17 — Opinion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

Same four gates as the Right Angle Service 4 but lived through a transpersonal angle. The carrier's life will not make sense as a personal story. It makes sense as the people who had to rise to the carrier's energy in order to keep up.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 58 (Personality Sun) — The Joyous

The conscious work of demanding vitality. For the Demands 2 carrier, the 58 is not just personal energy. It is the energetic standard the carrier sets for the surrounding system. The body's vitality demands that the surrounding bodies bring their own.

### Gate 52 (Personality Earth) — Keeping Still

Adrenal stillness as the conscious ground. The 52 keeps the carrier's energetic demand focused rather than scattered. The vitality is sustained, concentrated, and directed at a particular refinement.

### Gate 18 (Design Sun) — Work on What Has Been Spoilt

Splenic correction beneath the conscious work. The 18 supplies the carrier's body-level awareness of what is broken in the mutation around them. The vitality has a precise target.

### Gate 17 (Design Earth) — Opinion

Mental opinion as the underlying medium. The 17 ensures that the demand has language when language is required. The carrier can articulate what they are asking for, when invited.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to change what has been built. The Demands 2 carrier contributes to that mutative work by placing energetic demands on the people who would otherwise let the mutation stall. The carrier is the body whose vitality forces the surrounding system to either match or admit defeat.

Ra emphasised that the Left Angle Cross of Demands is a difficult cross to live well. The carrier becomes a high-energy presence that other bodies either match or burn out around. The demand is real. The standards are high. The vitality is sustained.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The life is not the carrier's own. The work is publicising — making available to the collective what the Right Angles have lived through. The Demands 2 publicises the joyful standard: the carrier walks among the under-energised systems and lets the vitality of their presence raise the energy of the people who can rise.

The not-self distortion of this cross is the carrier whose demands have become exhausting — the chronic high-energy person who pushes everyone past their capacity, who runs ahead of every problem, who burns themselves out trying to drag the surrounding system upward. The mind is in charge of the demand, and the demand becomes coercive. The correct expression is body-led joy. The body's vitality is the medium. The carrier's job is to be alive, fully, in their own body, and to let the demand arrive through energetic presence rather than through verbal pressure.

Ra called Left Angles karmic lives. The Demands 2 carrier will meet, again and again, the same kinds of under-energised systems — and the work is to bring the body's full vitality into those systems and let the systems respond. The carrier rarely sees the full effect of the demand. The mutation happens slowly, in the years after the carrier has moved on.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle of this cross is carried by five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2 and 6/3.

- **4/6** is the externalising friend whose energetic demands reach the collective through the friendship network
- **5/1** is the heretic with foundation, the universalising demander whose vitality has the authority of 1st line study behind it
- **5/2** is the heretic hermit, projected upon as a savior of energy — most effective when allowed to remain selective about whose system they enter
- **6/2** is the role model emerging from the rooftop — the elder whose joyful demands shape the community
- **6/3** is the role model who moved through trial and error and now demands from a place of demonstrated vitality

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are non-negotiable. A Left Angle carrier who lives by mind quickly becomes the exhausting high-energy presence whom people avoid. The work is to let the body's joy be the medium of demand. The body's vitality, expressed correctly, is contagious. The mind's demand for vitality is coercive. The difference is enormous.

The signature of this cross lived well is people whose energy levels rose because the carrier was in the room. The collective system mutates upward because the carrier modeled what full vitality looks like.

The not-self signature is exhaustion and resentment — the carrier who has burned out trying to drag everyone upward, and the people around them who have learned to retreat. The recovery is to come back to the body's natural joy, stop pushing, and let the vitality be its own demand.

When the carrier honours this cross, they become what Ra implied was one of the great mutative energisers of the wheel — the one whose presence requires the surrounding system to rise. This is a thankless cross. The carrier rarely gets credit. The reward is the quiet knowledge that the species' mutation moved a little because of their vitality.

This is the cross of the energetic demander, the joyful raiser-of-standards, the body whose vitality is itself a request. Lived correctly, it is a service that lifts every system the carrier enters. Lived incorrectly, it is a life of friction and burnout. The difference is whether the carrier honors the joy.
