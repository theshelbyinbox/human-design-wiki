# Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 3

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 3 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Conservation — is the fixed-fate variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 3. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 32]] (Continuity / Duration) at line 4, and the entire architecture of the cross is locked into the accent of conservation. Juxtaposition crosses are the rarest cross type, present only in the 4/1 profile, and they represent a pure, concentrated expression of a single thematic accent. In the Quarter of Duality, with its purpose anchored in Bonding, the Juxtaposition of Conservation is the fixed life-task of being the conservator inside a specific tribal network.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 32]] (Continuity / Duration), Line 4
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 42]] (Growth / Increase), Line 4
- Design Sun: [[Gate 62]] or [[Gate 61]], Line 1
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun, Line 1
- Personality Axis: 32 / 42
- Design Axis: 62 / 61
- Quarter: Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter)
- Realm Purpose: Bonding
- Mystical Theme: Measure for Measure
- Angle Expression: Juxtaposition — fixed fate
- Profile: 4/1 only

## The Four Gates

### Gate 32 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 32]] in the Juxtaposition is the fixed orientation toward conservation. At line 4, the gate carries Right Action — the conservator's act within the network of bonds, knowing when to commit resources and when to withdraw them. The 4/1 profile makes this energy externally networked: the carrier's conservation-work is offered to specific bonded others, the inner circle of friends, family, and chosen community. Ra emphasized that the 32 is the tribe's intuitive financial manager — and the Juxtaposition of Conservation fixes this faculty into a constant, lifelong role inside a small bonded group.

### Gate 42 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 42]] at line 4 is the Middle Way — maturation through relationship. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's life of conservation is rooted in the relational cycle-completion of the 42 line 4. The cycles being completed are bonded cycles: marriages, friendships, working partnerships, long collaborations. The Juxtaposition fixity means this is the lifelong groove.

### Gate 62 (Design)

[[Gate 62]] in the design imprint at line 1 is Routine — the foundational expression of detail-attention. The body has a built-in capacity for the daily, repetitive precision that keeps bonded arrangements functional. In Maya 3 Juxtaposition, this means the carrier's body silently tends to the small specifics that make the conservation-work practical: the dates, the records, the named obligations.

### Gate 61 (Design)

[[Gate 61]] at line 1 is Occult Knowing — the private, esoteric expression of inner-truth pressure. The body carries a quiet, fixed pulse of mystical attention that does not need external expression. This design-level mysticism keeps the conservator's work humble about what the named arrangements cannot reach.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality channels purpose through Bonding. Its mystical theme is Measure for Measure — the reciprocal arrangements through which the tribe sustains itself. The Maya cross in this quarter is the cross of the bonded conservator; the Juxtaposition of Conservation accents this work into pure focus.

The carrier's life is fixed inside the role of network-keeper. The 4/1 profile makes friendship and chosen community the operating field. The carrier is here to be the trusted conservation-figure inside that specific small group — the one whose splenic in-the-moment judgment about which arrangements will hold is the practical reliability the network is built on.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. The 4/1 profile is the most genetically fixed profile in the system, and the Juxtaposition further locks the cross into a single thematic accent. The Juxtaposition Maya 3 carrier is not the wide-ranging conservator of the Right Angle nor the transpersonal teacher of the Left Angle. They are the precise, narrow, fixed conservator inside their specific bonded network.

The mind will want broader scope. The cross teaches narrower focus. Inside the accent, the carrier can be unusually reliable and accurate. Reaching beyond the accent typically produces frustration and burnout.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 3 manifests only in the 4/1 profile. The 4 line is the Opportunist — externalized, networked, social. The 1 line is the Investigator — deep, foundational, private study. Together the 4/1 is the person who networks their foundation. In the Maya 3 Juxtaposition, this profile is fixed into the accent of conservation: the 4/1 carrier becomes the trusted continuity-figure inside their chosen circle.

## Living This Cross

Living the Juxtaposition of Conservation well means embracing the fixity. The carrier is not designed to advise the wider community, transform whole institutions, or lead public movements. They are designed to conserve, inside a small bonded network, what is worth conserving. The mind will rebel against the smallness; the cross teaches that the smallness is the precision.

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are essential. Without them, the 4/1 conservator becomes brittle, rigid, and over-burdened — the friend who carries everyone's continuity-failures as personal weight. With them, the conservator's splenic in-the-moment judgment operates cleanly, and the burden lifts because the carrier knows which bonds are theirs to hold and which are not.

The fear of failure is part of the design. The Juxtaposition Maya 3 carrier will feel this fear continuously inside their bonded network — the silent worry that the marriage will fail, the partnership will collapse, the family will fracture. Ra was clear that the fear is the calibration mechanism, not the flaw. Listening to the fear as splenic information produces the discernment that keeps the bonds intact. Trying to override or eliminate the fear loses the discernment.

The 42 Earth grounds the work in cycles. Bonds run on seven-year arcs. The Juxtaposition Maya 3 carrier learns over a lifetime which phase of which cycle each of their bonds is currently in. This is not analytical knowledge; it is the body's accumulated familiarity with the rhythms of the people they are bonded to.

The 61 design pulse keeps the work humble. The bonds are named, the arrangements are real, but the mystery beneath them is always larger than any name. The carrier who forgets this becomes the rigid conservator who confuses the named arrangement with the living bond. The one who remembers it becomes the conservator whose work has both reliability and depth.

The deeper teaching of this cross is that civilization is built out of small bonded networks held together by people who can read continuity in the now. The Juxtaposition Maya 3 carrier is one of these people. Their fixed accent is not a smaller life but a more precise one. The bonds they hold across decades are the practical fabric of the tribe.
