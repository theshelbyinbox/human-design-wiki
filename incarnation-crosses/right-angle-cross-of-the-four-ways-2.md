# Right Angle Cross of the Four Ways 2

The Right Angle Cross of the Four Ways 2 is the cross of civilization's retreat — the carrier through whom the Quarter of Form turns inward to process what it has built. The Personality Sun sits in Gate 33, the gate of Retreat, and the carrier closes the Quarter of Civilization with a life shaped around the disciplined withdrawal that lets memory become wisdom.

Ra Uru Hu described Gate 33 as the privacy of the witness — the gate that retreats from the encounter so the encounter can be remembered correctly. The Four Ways 2 carrier lives that retreat as a personal destiny across four 18.6-year nodal cycles. Each cycle is a different mode of withdrawal. Each withdrawal becomes a new layer of what the carrier knows, and through them, what civilization knows.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 33 — Retreat
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 19 — Approach
- **Design Sun:** Gate 44 — Coming to Meet
- **Design Earth:** Gate 24 — Rationalizing / Return
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 33–19 is the consciousness: retreat partnered with the tribal need to approach. The Design axis 44–24 is the body's vehicle: the splenic recognition of pattern partnered with the mental return to what was patterned.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 33 (Personality Sun) — Retreat

The conscious purpose. Gate 33 is the Throat gate of retreat — the disciplined withdrawal during which the encounter is processed into memory. As the lead of this cross it gives the carrier a life-long instruction to step back at the right moments so that what was met can become what is known. The 33 is the witness who saves the story by leaving the scene long enough to remember it.

### Gate 19 (Personality Earth) — Approach

The ground beneath the retreat. Gate 19 is the Root pressure of approach. As the Earth of this cross it keeps the retreating mind tethered to the body's tribal need — the need for the resources, the shelter, the bonded relationships that the retreat is being conducted in service of. Without the 19 the 33 becomes intellectual escape; with it, the retreat is always returning to material life.

### Gate 44 (Design Sun) — Coming to Meet

The unconscious splenic engine. Gate 44 is the splenic pattern-recognition that knows by smell whether the present encounter is a re-encounter with a familiar configuration. Beneath the conscious retreat, the body has already been doing the recognition. The 44 is what the 33 retreats with — the data the splenic body has gathered, ready to be processed into wisdom.

### Gate 24 (Design Earth) — Rationalizing

The unconscious mental ground. Gate 24 is the individual mental gate of return. The body of this carrier is built to process the gathered patterns by returning to them, again and again, until the satori arrives. The 24 grounds the retreat in a mind that knows how to wait for the insight.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. Its purpose is to build the structures, laws, languages, and cultural patterns through which human beings live together. Gate 33 closes this quarter by giving civilization its memory — the disciplined retreat through which the form's lived experience becomes the form's wisdom. The Four Ways 2 carrier contributes to civilization by being the body through which the species learns to remember what it built.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is a personal destiny. The Four Ways 2 carrier lives their own life in four 18.6-year cycles. Each cycle is a different mode of withdrawal — the carrier may spend one nodal cycle in solitude, another in public witness, another as the historian of a community, another as the silent observer. Ra was clear that this cross "deeply impacts the other much more than it is impacting on the person who carries it." Civilization gets the memory the carrier preserves. The carrier themselves may feel that the four ways are just one withdrawal after another.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses the retreat, who treats withdrawal as antisocial, or who clings to one mode of retreat across all four cycles. The correct expression lets each cycle's retreat be its own thing, lets the 44 keep gathering patterns and the 24 keep returning to them, and trusts that the civilization-level use of the memory is what the cross is for.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle is carried by the six classical right-angle profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6.

- **1/3** the investigator-retreater who tests modes of withdrawal through trial and error
- **1/4** the investigator-retreater whose retreats are conducted within a stable friendship network
- **2/4** the natural hermit whose retreats are the structural condition of the life
- **2/5** the called hermit-retreater whose solitude is interrupted by collective projection
- **3/5** the trial-and-error retreater whose mistakes in withdrawal teach the community when to step back
- **3/6** the role-model retreater who matures into the elder whose memory becomes civilization's

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only reliable navigation system. The Four Ways 2 carrier is tempted to retreat from the body's signals as well as from the world. The work is to retreat from the encounter while staying inside the body. A Generator with this cross responds with the sacral to when the retreat is correct; a Projector waits for the invitation back into engagement; a Manifestor informs before withdrawing; a Reflector lets a lunar cycle reveal whether the retreat is the right move in this environment.

The signature of the cross lived well is a life that has gathered four chapters of memory and offered them as the form's wisdom. The not-self signature is the bitterness of the carrier who retreated incorrectly — too far, too long, or out of fear — and lost contact with the body and the tribe.

When the carrier honours the design, they become the witness whose memory civilization uses to know itself. The four nodal cycles deliver four bodies of remembered material, and through the carrier's disciplined retreat, the species learns what it has been doing.
