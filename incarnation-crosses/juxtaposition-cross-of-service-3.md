# Juxtaposition Cross of Service 3

The Juxtaposition Cross of Service 3 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Correction. It is the fixed-fate variant of the four Service gates in the Quarter of Duality, incarnated through the only profile that produces a Juxtaposition Cross: the 4/1. The carrier is here to live a single, unwavering corrective awareness — to be the body that notices, in their friendship network, what is broken in the bonded relationships and to be the steady reference point for repair. Where the Right Angle of this cross is the personal journey of bonded correction and the Left Angle is the disruptive transpersonal work of upheaval, the Juxtaposition is the simple presence of corrective wisdom as a fact in the world.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 18 — Work on What Has Been Spoilt
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 17 — Opinion
- **Design Sun:** Gate 58 — Vitality
- **Design Earth:** Gate 52 — Keeping Still
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Juxtaposition — Fixed Fate
- **Profile:** 4/1 only

The gate set is identical to the other two angles of Service 3. The Juxtaposition difference is the rigidity of the corrective awareness. The 4/1 carrier of Correction is not on a developmental path. The splenic awareness is essentially set at birth.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 18 (Personality Sun) — Work on What Has Been Spoilt

The fixed corrective awareness. The Juxtaposition carrier sees, instinctively and reliably, what is broken in the bonded patterns around them. The 18 is not modulated by context. The carrier sees the breakage in the same way at every age, in every relationship.

### Gate 17 (Personality Earth) — Opinion

The fixed mental articulation. The 17 provides the language for the correction. The carrier's opinions about what should be repaired are essentially stable across life.

### Gate 58 (Design Sun) — Vitality

The fixed adrenal joy beneath the surface. The 58 supplies the energy that keeps the correction work alive. The Juxtaposition carrier's love of life animates the corrective awareness rather than embittering it.

### Gate 52 (Design Earth) — Keeping Still

The fixed adrenal concentration. The 52 gives the carrier the capacity to stay with the relationship long enough for the correction to land.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. Its purpose is the meeting of self and other. The Juxtaposition Correction 3 contributes a fixed pulse of corrective awareness at the centre of that bonding work. The carrier is the steady eye in their friendship network — the one who reliably notices the relational breakage and, when invited, names it.

Ra Uru Hu described the Juxtaposition Crosses as the rarest of the three angles. For Correction 3, the rigidity is the corrective awareness itself, set at birth, available to the carrier's immediate circle.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is a cross of fixed fate. The carrier's destiny is set. The corrective awareness is set. The work is to honor it.

For Correction 3, this means the carrier walks into their friendship network already in possession of the corrective eye. The work is not to learn correction. The work is to deliver corrections only when invited, within the 4/1's natural circle, and to let the consistency of the awareness become the small but reliable contribution.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to correct everyone, becomes the chronic critic in their friend group, and exhausts the relationships through unsolicited splenic commentary. The recovery is to come back to the body, wait for invitation, and deliver the correction only when asked.

Juxtaposition Crosses are demanding. The Correction 3 carrier may feel that their corrective awareness is too sharp, too persistent, too inescapable — and the design says it is meant to be exactly that, but used only when invited. The work is to live with the awareness internally and to release it externally only on cue.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Every Juxtaposition Cross is held by the 4/1 profile only. The 4/1 Correction 3 is the externalising friend with a foundation — the person whose corrective wisdom is grounded in 1st line study of bonded patterns and delivered through 4th line social reach within the friendship network.

The 4/1 is the only fixed profile on the wheel. The carrier of Correction 3 is therefore not on a developmental path. The corrective awareness is in working order at birth, and the work is to deliver it patiently within the limits of the 4/1's natural reach.

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation system. The carrier's [[Type]] determines how the fixed corrective awareness lands correctly. A Generator 4/1 Correction responds with the sacral to invitations for correction from the friendship circle. A Projector 4/1 Correction waits to be recognised by the fixed network as the corrector. A Manifestor 4/1 informs before correcting. A Reflector 4/1 samples the lunar cycle within the friendship circle.

The signature lived well is a friendship network that grows healthier over time because the carrier is consistently noticing and, when invited, gently naming what is broken. The not-self signature is the carrier whose corrections have become unwelcome and who has lost access to the very relationships the design was meant to repair.

When the carrier honours the design, they become the reliable corrector of their immediate friendship circle. People learn to come to the carrier when they sense something is wrong in their bond and need an honest read. The carrier's corrective awareness, delivered patiently, becomes part of the network's infrastructure of repair.

This is one of the most narrowly fixed of the Service expressions and one of the most useful when honored within its limits. The work is to discover that the corrective awareness is a service and to stop using it where it has not been asked for.
