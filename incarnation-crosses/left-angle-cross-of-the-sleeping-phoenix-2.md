# Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2

The Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2 is the transpersonal expression of the Sleeping Phoenix gate set in the Quarter of Civilization. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Left Angle Cross of Duality 1. The name points at its function: the carrier is here to embody the duality of presence and absence, of the awake now and the asleep doing, and to teach this duality to the people who encounter them.

The one-line theme: the duality of presence — the carrier is the visible split between sleeping and waking, here to reveal that civilisation runs on this divide.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 20 — Contemplation
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 34 — The Power of the Great
- **Design Sun:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Design Earth:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 1 variant keeps the Personality axis 20–34 of the Right Angle but rotates the design axis to lead with Gate 59. The body's intimate biology now leads, and the spirit-pulse of the 55 sits as the earth grounding the body. This shift makes the carrier transpersonal in their intimate life — their bonds, their sexuality, their friendships become the medium through which they teach the duality of presence.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 20 (Personality Sun) — Contemplation

The throat voice of the now. In the Left Angle, the carrier's conscious purpose is still presence, but the presence is shared. They are not standing in the gateway alone; they are pulling others into it by example, by intimacy, by the visible difference between their awake state and their asleep state.

### Gate 34 (Personality Earth) — The Power of the Great

Pure individual sacral power grounds the throat voice. The carrier's body is built for sustained presence — when they wake, they have the energy to be present for years. The 34 ensures the awakeness is not a passing state but a sustainable mode.

### Gate 59 (Design Sun) — Dispersion

The unconscious engine of intimate biology. The Left Angle Duality carrier leads with the 59 in the body — their intimate bonds are the laboratory in which the duality plays out. Their lovers, partners, and close friends are the witnesses and beneficiaries of their awakening.

### Gate 55 (Design Earth) — Spirit / Abundance

The moody emotional ground. Beneath the intimate engine, the body pulses with mutating spirit. The 55 keeps the Left Angle Duality carrier honest — the spirit-pulse cannot be faked, cannot be performed, cannot be sustained mechanically. When spirit is present, the awakeness is real; when spirit is absent, the carrier sleeps.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. Within this quarter, Left Angle Duality 1 serves a specific function: making the duality of presence visible. While Right Angle Sleeping Phoenix 2 carriers sleepwalk through civilisation as productive labour, the Left Angle Duality carrier embodies the visible split. They are the friend who is sometimes there and sometimes not, the partner who comes and goes, the artist who produces brilliantly when awake and forgettably when asleep.

This visible duality is itself a teaching. Other people learn from the Left Angle Duality carrier what awakeness looks like by seeing what its absence looks like. The contrast becomes the lesson. Ra emphasised that the Quarter of Civilization needs this teaching because the quarter is built on sleepwalking labour, and without visible carriers of duality, no one would know there was anything else.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is shaped by what others need from the carrier. For Duality 1, the transpersonal pull is the teaching of presence — but the teaching happens through the intimate field, not through public broadcast. The carrier's lovers, partners, close friends are the audience.

Ra noted that this is one of the more challenging Left Angles to live because the duality is uncomfortable for the people in the carrier's life. They learn to anticipate when the carrier is awake and when the carrier is asleep; the relationships have to accommodate the fluctuation. When the carrier is correct, this fluctuation becomes a gift — an honest teaching about the texture of presence. When the carrier is not correct, it becomes intermittent withdrawal that exhausts the bonded other.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses the duality, who pretends to be awake when asleep or who collapses into permanent sleep to avoid the discomfort of fluctuation. The correct expression is honest fluctuation — the carrier shows up when present, withdraws when absent, and lets the people closest to them see both states clearly.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Duality 1 manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra described the 5/1 Duality as the heretic of presence — the visible teacher of awakeness whose own duality is the medium of the teaching. The 5/2 carries the duality privately. The 6/2 Duality, when awake, becomes a deeply present role model whose intimate life is the witness of their awakening.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept that the intimate field is the teaching ground. The carrier of Duality 1 cannot escape the transpersonal demand on their bonds. Their partners, lovers, friends are part of the design.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] either flees intimate bonds (refusing the transpersonal pull) or collapses into intimate bonds that demand permanent presence (refusing the duality). Living from [[Signature]] means letting the intimate field carry the fluctuation. The carrier honours when they are awake by being fully present; they honour when they are asleep by acknowledging the absence rather than masking it.

The deepest practice for this cross is treating sleep as data. The Sleeping Phoenix 2 design will sleep — the body's mechanics demand it. The Left Angle Duality 1 carrier does not fight this. They notice when they have been asleep, recognise what triggered the sleep, and welcome the next waking without trying to engineer it. The lifelong work is to make the cycles cleaner — sleep that is honest sleep, waking that is honest waking, and intimate bonds that can hold both.
