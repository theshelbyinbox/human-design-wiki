# Left Angle Cross of Tension 3

The Left Angle Cross of Tension 3 — known in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Endeavor 2 — is the transpersonal variant of the four-armed Cross of Tension in the Quarter of Bonding. The same four gates that organize every variant of the Tension cross — 21, 48, 38, 39 — are present here, but the Left Angle rotation places the carrier in a karmic field of bonded relationship rather than in a private destiny.

Where the Right Angle Tension 3 lives a personal life of depth-bearing in their own intimate field, the Left Angle Tension 3 carrier is part of an endeavor that bonded relationship makes possible. The depth is not theirs to keep. The endeavor is the work of relationship itself.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 48 — The Well / Depth
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 21 — Control / The Hunter
- **Design Sun:** Gate 39 — Obstruction / The Provocateur
- **Design Earth:** Gate 38 — Opposition / The Fighter
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The four gates of the Tension cross are present in their Tension 3 axis arrangement; the angle is Left, meaning the carrier's life is the karmic life. The Personality axis 48↔21 carries the conscious endeavor of depth and control. The Design axis 39↔38 carries the unconscious individual root provocation.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 48 (Personality Sun) — The Well, Depth

The 48 here is the depth offered to a bonded field. The Left Angle Endeavor 2 carrier is built to be the depth that holds a partnership, a family, a community of bonded ties together. The harmonic 16 turns the depth into enthusiasm and skill that other people can use.

### Gate 21 (Personality Earth) — Control, The Hunter

Gate 21 grounds the well in the right to control. The Left Angle's control is exerted on behalf of the endeavor — the structure that keeps the bonded field functioning.

### Gate 39 (Design Sun) — Obstruction, The Provocateur

The unconscious provocateur of the bonded field. The body tests the spirit of the people the carrier is bonded to. The endeavor cannot be carried by people whose spirit is asleep, so the body's foundation is the obstruction that wakes them up.

### Gate 38 (Design Earth) — Opposition, The Fighter

The unconscious fighter. The vehicle fights for the purpose of the bonded endeavor — for the why that makes the relationship worth its costs.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Bonding is the realm of duality. Its purpose is to organize human beings through intimate connection. The Left Angle Tension 3 contributes to bonding through endeavor: the carrier's life is an undertaking that bonded relationship makes possible, and through which bonded relationship is itself deepened.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The Left Angle Tension 3 carrier does not own their endeavor in the way a Right Angle carrier owns their depth. The endeavor exists because the bonded field exists. The carrier's life is the medium through which that field accomplishes its work.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who treats the endeavor as a personal mission and the bonded others as helpers in their private project. The depth becomes a private resource. The control becomes a private regime. The provocation becomes private impatience. The fight becomes private grievance. The correct expression is the opposite: the endeavor is the carrier, the depth is given, and the bonded field is what makes any of it possible.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left Angle Crosses manifest through 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3.

- **4/6** carries the externalizing endeavor whose network is the channel through which the depth flows
- **5/1** carries the universalizing endeavor with a foundational study underneath the work
- **5/2** carries the heretical hermit endeavor — natural withdrawal meets projection
- **6/2** carries the matured hermit endeavor across three life phases
- **6/3** carries the role-model experimenter endeavor whose trial-and-error becomes the wisdom of an elder

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation. The fear of inadequacy in the 48 and the fear of uselessness in the 38 are part of the splenic awareness of the cross. They are not problems to solve; they are signals to read correctly. The body knows when the endeavor is the real one and when the relationship is the real one.

The signature of the cross lived correctly is a bonded endeavor that lasts — a marriage, a partnership, a long collaboration that built something neither party could have built alone. The signature of the not-self is the carrier whose body holds the tension that the field was supposed to receive, and whose bonded relationships have become the casualties of a fight that never made it outside the carrier's own nervous system.

When the carrier honors the design, the endeavor flowers. The depth gets given to the partner. The control gives the partnership a structure. The provocation tests the spirit of the bond. The fight defends the purpose. Lived correctly, Left Angle Tension 3 is the cross of the long undertaking — the work of a bonded life that outlasts the individuals who built it.

## Endeavor in the Quarter of Bonding

Bonded relationship is one of the most demanding contexts on the wheel. Two beings, each carrying their own design, agree to share enough of a life that something is built between them. That something is the endeavor, and for the Left Angle Endeavor 2 carrier, the endeavor is the structural purpose of the bonded life.

Ra Uru Hu described human beings as creatures in movement. The Left Angle Endeavor crosses are the configurations whose movement is in the service of a sustained undertaking — a project, a partnership, a long collaboration that extends across the life. The Endeavor 2 carrier's specific contribution is depth — the qualitative substrate that makes the bonded endeavor possible at all.

The carrier's tension is the tension of holding the well while bonded life is happening. The 39 provokes the spirit of the partner. The 38 fights for the purpose of the shared work. The 21 controls the material conditions. The 48 supplies the depth. All four arms of the cross are aimed at the bonded endeavor, and the carrier's life is the medium through which the endeavor accomplishes its work.

For the carrier this means accepting that the bonded relationship is not the carrier's private life but the karmic field of their participation. The endeavor belongs to both. The depth is given to the partnership. The work is built across the years, and the carrier's life is one half of the structure that makes it possible.
