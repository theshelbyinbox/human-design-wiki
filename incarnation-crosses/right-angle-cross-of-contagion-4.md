# Right Angle Cross of Contagion 4

The Right Angle Cross of Contagion 4 carries the Personality Sun in Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure — anchored in the Quarter of Mutation. Gate 14 is the gate of power skills, the sacral fuel that retains and channels resources across a lifetime of skilled work. Ra Uru Hu named this cross the Keeper of the Keys, pointing to the material substrate it represents: the resources, the power, the accumulated wealth that fuel mutation when contagion finds the right vessel.

The one-line theme: the keeper of the keys — material resources empowering mutation through contagion. The carrier is the wealth, the power, and the skilled productivity that makes mutation materially possible.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure (Power Skills)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal (Saying Yes)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire (Recognition of Feelings)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of the Center, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 14–8 is the consciousness axis: the sacral power that accumulates resources grounded in the throat voice of unique contribution. The Design axis 29–30 is the body's underlying engine: the sacral yes to experience grounded in the fire of desire. The carrier is a being whose conscious life is shaped by power, resources, and the offering of their unique contribution — and whose body is fuelled by the abstract circuit's commitment and desire.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 14 (Personality Sun) — Power Skills

Ra Uru Hu was specific that the 14 is the most material of the individual gates — not material in the sense of money alone, but in the sense of the accumulation and retention of power. The carrier's conscious life is shaped by their relationship to resources. They are here to acquire power skills, to hold what they accumulate, and to know what to do with the power they retain.

### Gate 8 (Personality Earth) — Holding Together

The throat voice of contribution grounds the carrier's power. The 8 ensures the carrier's resources are not held merely for hoarding; they are held to fuel a specific contribution. Ra called the 14–8 pairing the channel that makes the carrier the spokesperson for their own power — visible, public, recognised as the one who has what is needed to fuel the mutation.

### Gate 29 (Design Sun) — The Abysmal

The unconscious sacral yes. Beneath the consciousness drama of resources, the body says yes — to projects, to people, to experiences that the resources can support. The 29 ensures the carrier's wealth is generative; they keep entering new commitments.

### Gate 30 (Design Earth) — The Clinging Fire

The fire of desire fuels the entire structure. The carrier accumulates power because they burn for what power can do — for the experiences, the contributions, the lives that resources make possible.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. The first three quarters established mind, form, and bonding. The fourth quarter is where everything mutates — where the established mind shifts into new understanding, the established form transforms into new vessel, the established bonds reform into new structure. Ra emphasised that this quarter is opened by Gate 1 (the Creative) and closed by Gate 44 (Coming to Meet) — the entire mutative process of the wheel happens within its borders.

Contagion 4 occupies a specific position in this quarter: it is the material engine of mutation. Mutation needs resources. The new vessel needs to be funded, the new form needs to be paid for, the new contribution needs the material infrastructure to spread. The Keeper of the Keys is here to hold the resources mutation requires.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is personal destiny. For Contagion 4, the right-angle expression is the carrier's own life of accumulated power and skilled work. They are here to be the keeper of their own keys — to acquire the resources, hold the power, and direct it according to their own design.

Ra was specific that this is not a tribal or collective cross — the 14 is individual, the accumulation is personal, the power is the carrier's own. They are not here to redistribute wealth in a collectivist sense; they are here to direct their accumulated power toward what the mutation requires. The collective theatre around money does not govern them.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who confuses power with money alone. Ra called this the dilemma of the 14.1 line — "money isn't everything." The not-self Contagion 4 chases money as if it were the same thing as power and finds that money in itself does not fuel the mutation. The correct expression understands that power is broader than wealth: skill, attention, resource, voice, and accumulated capacity are all forms of the power the 14 retains.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

This Right Angle Cross appears in six profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6. Ra discussed the 1/3 Contagion 4 in terms of the early discovery that money alone does not deliver power. The 1/4 is described as the carrier locked into material one-mindedness. The 2/4 Contagion 4 is the natural manager — the carrier who knows that the wisdom is to invest in others' expertise and let the trickle-down structure do the work. The 3/5 Contagion 4 is the one who blames their material struggles on the configuration; the 3/6 closes the Right Angle and offers the most mature material wisdom.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to let the body respond to what resources should be accumulated, retained, and directed. The carrier of Contagion 4 has a sacral authority (in Generator designs) that knows in the moment which work to do, which investment to make, which power skill to cultivate.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] grasps for money as if it were the answer, exhausts themselves chasing power that does not respond to their body, and ends up materially insecure despite the design's natural capacity for retention. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the body's response guide accumulation. The work the body says yes to becomes the long-term source of the resources the cross is here to retain.

The deepest practice for this cross is recognising that the 14 is a lifelong development gate. Ra noted that every line of the 14 carries a blue header in the Rave I'Ching, meaning every line is a long-term process. The carrier does not accumulate power quickly. They accumulate it across decades through correct work, correct response, and correct retention. The patient delivery of the lifelong process is the practice.

When delivered correctly, the Contagion 4 carrier becomes the visible material engine in their community — the person whose accumulated resources make the next mutation materially possible.
