# Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 4

The Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 4 is the transpersonal expression of the Sleeping Phoenix gate set in the Quarter of Mutation. In the closing quarter of the wheel, the Left Angle 2 variant is the Left Angle Cross of Duality 2 — the cross whose carriers are here to teach, through their intimate life, the duality of power-with-presence and power-without-presence.

The one-line theme: the duality of awakened power. The carrier embodies the difference between power deployed from sleep and power deployed from the now, and teaches that difference to the people who encounter them.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 34 — The Power of the Great
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 20 — Contemplation
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 2 variant of Sleeping Phoenix 4 keeps the Personality axis 34–20 of the Right Angle but rotates the Design axis so that the mutating spirit of the 55 leads the unconscious body, with the intimate role gate of the 59 as the earth. This is a subtle but consequential mechanical shift. The body's leading engine becomes the felt wave of the 55 rather than the genetic role of the 59 — the carrier is therefore more visibly moody, more visibly artistic, more visibly attuned to the pulse of mutating spirit than the Right Angle carrier.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 34 (Personality Sun) — The Power of the Great

Pure individual sacral power in the conscious position. In the Left Angle, the carrier leads with their power, but the leading is transpersonal — they are here to wield their power visibly so that others can learn the difference between power-asleep and power-awake.

### Gate 20 (Personality Earth) — Contemplation

The throat voice of the now grounds the conscious power. The 20 is the design's invitation to step into the gateway; in the Left Angle, the step is made (or refused) publicly. The carrier's presence is visible to the people in their field.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The unconscious engine of mutating spirit. The Left Angle 2 variant leads the body with the wave. The carrier's emotional pulse is the medium of their teaching — when the wave is full, the awakened power they wield is luminous; when the wave is empty, the power is hollow.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The genetic role gate of intimate biology grounds the design. The 59 ensures the carrier's transpersonal work happens through intimate transmission — through the close field of lovers, partners, and protégés.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Within this quarter, Left Angle Duality 2 serves the function of making the duality of awakened power visible. The mutation requires not just awake bodies but bodies whose awakeness can be seen, modelled, and transmitted. The Left Angle Duality 2 carrier is the visible model.

Ra emphasised that the carrier's life is the teaching. Their relationships, their work, their visible deployments of power across their lifetime become the curriculum the people around them learn from. The pattern is often clear in retrospect: a period of unconscious power, followed by a waking trauma, followed by a different deployment of the same power. The carrier's witnesses see the before and the after, and the contrast is the teaching.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is shaped by what others need from the carrier. For Duality 2, the transpersonal pull is the public modelling of awakened power.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses the visibility — who tries to keep their power-deployment private, who hides the waking when it comes, who pretends to have been awake the whole time. The correct expression is honest visibility: the carrier shows the unconscious phase, accepts the waking trauma in view of their witnesses, and then deploys the awakened power publicly so others can see what is possible.

Ra was specific that the Left Angle Duality carriers can be polarising figures. The honest visibility of their before-and-after makes them visible heretics in any community that expects consistent personality. Their value is precisely in their inconsistency: they prove that change is possible, that waking is possible, that the same body can deploy the same power in radically different ways depending on whether the consciousness is awake or asleep.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Duality 2 manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 5/1 in this configuration is the visible heretic whose public life teaches the duality lesson. The 5/2 carries the same teaching privately. The 6/2 Duality 2, when awake, becomes a deeply present teacher whose intimate field models awakened power as a way of being. The 6/3 closes the Left Angle and offers the most mature integration before the cross rotates into the next Right Angle.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept the transpersonal pull of the teaching. The carrier of Duality 2 cannot privatise their power-deployment for the duration of the life — the design demands visibility.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] tries to live in either-or terms: either always awake (impossible) or always asleep (the design refuses). Living from [[Signature]] means letting the cycles be visible. The witnesses see when the carrier is awake and when the carrier is asleep, and the witnesses learn from both states.

The deepest practice for this cross is honouring the 55's wave as the regulator of teaching. The Left Angle Duality 2 carrier does not get to teach on a schedule. They teach when the wave allows and rest when the wave does not. Their lovers, partners, and protégés learn the timing — they show up when the teaching is available and step back when it is not. The carrier's job is not to manufacture the wave but to honour it.

When delivered correctly, this cross produces some of the most useful visible-teacher figures in any community — the carriers whose entire life-arc becomes the lesson, whose witnesses can point to the before, the waking, and the after as the curriculum of awakened power.
