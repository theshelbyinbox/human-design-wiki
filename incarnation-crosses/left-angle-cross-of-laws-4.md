# Left Angle Cross of Laws 4

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 4 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Distraction 2 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Laws cross in Quarter 4. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 60]] (Limitation / Acceptance) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Mutation, where purpose moves through Transformation, the Left Angle Laws 4 is the cross of the transpersonal limitation-keeper — the carrier whose lived acceptance of constraint is karmically routed toward specific others whose own mutations require this kind of patient anchoring.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 60]] (Limitation / Acceptance), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 56]] (The Wanderer), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 50]] or [[Gate 3]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 60 / 56
- Design Axis: 50 / 3
- Quarter: Quarter of Mutation (Realm of the Godhead)
- Realm Purpose: Transformation
- Mystical Theme: Accepting Death
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 60 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 60]] in the Left Angle is the acceptance-of-limitation turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Leadership — the practical projection of the gate, the leader who demonstrates how to inhabit limitation productively, the person others look to for the model of how to wait well. At line 6 it carries Rigidity in its mature form — the elder who has accepted certain limitations as permanent features of life and has built a stable form around them.

Ra's signature teaching of the 60 was "by accepting limitation, it can be transcended." In the Left Angle, this teaching is offered transpersonally — the carrier becomes a karmic agent of acceptance for specific others whose own mutations have been stalled by their refusal to inhabit the constraints they are actually inside.

### Gate 56 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 56]] at line 5 is Attraction — the projection field of the expected storyteller, the keynote speaker, the bestselling author. At line 6 it is Selectivity — the mature wanderer who has learned which stories to tell, to whom, and when. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal acceptance-work is communicated through stories — narratives told to the specific others their karma routes them toward.

The name "Distraction" given to the Left Angle Laws 4 echoes the 56 line 3's name (Distraction itself) and points at the shadow risk: storytelling that scatters rather than focuses, narratives that distract listeners from what really matters. The work of the cross is to keep the storytelling rooted in genuine acceptance.

### Gate 50 (Design)

[[Gate 50]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's splenic value-awareness into the cross. Line 2 is Determination — natural responsibility that does not require thought. Line 3 is Adaptability — bonds made and broken in service of values. These design lines give the body the practical ethical compass for the transpersonal work.

### Gate 3 (Design)

[[Gate 3]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the sacral mutation-pulse into the body. Line 2 is Immaturity — the body's foundational pulse that does not yet know its own depth. Line 3 is Survival — learning by trial and error. These design lines complete the Channel of Mutation in the carrier's bodygraph at the experimental level.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation channels purpose through Transformation. Its mystical theme is Accepting Death — the recognition that mutation requires the death of the old before the new can emerge. The Laws cross in this quarter is the cross of the mutative law itself, the law of limitation. The Left Angle variant routes this lawkeeping into transpersonal karma.

The carrier is here to meet specific others whose mutations have been stalled — usually by the refusal to accept the constraints they are actually inside. The carrier's lived acceptance becomes the karmic gift offered to those others. The encounters are the life.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Laws 4 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges in the field of accepted limitation, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned analytically. The karmic encounters arrive in their own timing. Only [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] can recognize the right ones.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 4 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator acceptance-teacher. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental life-arcs that distill into role-model wisdom in the third phase.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Laws 4 life unfolds through encounters in which the carrier's lived acceptance changes what another person is willing to inhabit. A friend who has been fighting against a constraint for years meets the carrier and finally sees that the constraint must be accepted before the mutation it has been blocking can lift off. A community trying to force a transformation prematurely meets the carrier and recognizes, through the carrier's example, the patience the situation actually requires.

This is delicate work. The 56 line 5 projection makes the carrier the target of demand for inspirational storytelling on others' schedules. The shadow Ra named for this Left Angle — Distraction — is what happens when the carrier becomes the conscripted speaker, producing narratives that scatter listeners rather than focus them.

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only protection. Without them, the carrier becomes a brand — the acceptance-teacher whose example has been performed too often and lost its substance. With them, the carrier's lived acceptance retains its weight, and the stories that emerge from it carry the karmic resonance the cross is designed to produce.

The pulse-mechanism applies to the transpersonal work too. The 3/60 channel does not run continuously, and the karmic encounters tend to arrive during the on-phases of the pulse. During off-phases, the work is the carrier's own acceptance — the inhabitation of the carrier's current limitations that prepares the carrier for the next encounter.

The 50 design provides the splenic value-filter that keeps the karmic work rooted in what is worth preserving. The 3 design provides the foundational mutation-pulse that completes the Channel of Mutation in the body.

The deeper teaching of this cross is that the law of limitation is the meta-law of all mutation — the recognition that real transformation cannot occur in fantasies of unlimited possibility but only inside the specific constraints of the actual moment. The Left Angle Laws 4 carrier is a karmic transmitter of this law. Their own life, in turn, is shaped by the others their lived acceptance was offered to.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Laws 4 person is often experienced as the patient elder, the one whose presence alone helps others stop forcing what cannot yet move. Their stories at line 5 or 6 carry weight not because of accumulated authority but because of the lived karmic resonance between their acceptance and the specific stalled mutations their life routes them toward. Accepting Death, the mystical theme of Quarter 4, becomes in this cross the karmic gift of helping others let the old forms die so the new can emerge.
