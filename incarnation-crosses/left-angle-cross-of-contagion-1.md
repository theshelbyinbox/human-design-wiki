# Left Angle Cross of Contagion 1

The Left Angle Cross of Contagion 1 is the transpersonal expression of the Contagion gate set in the Quarter of Initiation. Where the Right Angle carrier affects others as a by-product of personal destiny, the Left Angle carrier is here to do something with the contagion field — to translate the misdirection of the fates into a form other people can use. This is the Left Angle Cross of Industry, named for its function: to turn what the fates have made available into productive work, into livelihood, into the busy, sustainable expansion of the human experiential way.

The one-line theme: industriousness as the transpersonal use of contagion. The carrier exists to take advantage of the fates and show others how to do the same.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal (Saying Yes / Perseverance)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire (Recognition of Feelings)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure (Power Skills)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

In the Left Angle 1 variant, the personality axis flips so that Gate 29 (the sacral commitment) leads consciously, while Gate 30 (the fire of desire) sits opposite as the earth. The design axis is the same gate set but rotated: Gate 14 leads the body's power, and Gate 8 sits as the voice opposite. This rotation places the carrier in a different relationship to the fates — they are not the unconscious instrument of misdirection, they are the one who responds to the misdirection by getting industrious.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 29 (Personality Sun) — The Abysmal

The 29 is the sacral yes to experience. In the Personality Sun, it becomes the conscious purpose of the life: to commit, to say yes, to enter the experience all the way through. Ra Uru Hu described this line as the draftee — the one who accepts being drafted into a situation and follows it to its conclusion. The carrier of Left Angle Contagion 1 leads with their commitment.

### Gate 30 (Personality Earth) — The Clinging Fire

In the earth position, the fire of desire becomes the field the carrier walks through rather than the conscious focus. The longing is still present, but it is the ground beneath the feet, not the figure in the foreground. Desire fuels the commitment without dictating it.

### Gate 14 (Design Sun) — Power Skills

The unconscious engine is the sacral power that retains and uses resources. The body of this carrier is built for sustained productive work. Ra spoke of the 14 as the gate of working hard — and in the not-self, working hard without progress. In the Left Angle Cross of Industry, that hard work is recovered into its purpose: industriousness as the response to the fates.

### Gate 8 (Design Earth) — Holding Together

The voice of contribution sits as the body's grounding direction. Beneath all the busyness, the body knows it is here to contribute through unique self-expression. The 8 keeps the industry from becoming mere productivity; it ensures the work has a self in it.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the realm of mind. The mind of humanity is initiated through the experiences the abstract circuit collects. Industry 1 contributes by ensuring those experiences have material support — that there is a livelihood, a way to make a living, a means by which people can sustain themselves through the experiments the fates make available.

Ra Uru Hu pointed out that without the Cross of Industry, the human experiential way would collapse under its own variety. There are too many things humans want to do, too many fates that interrupt the plan. Industry is the cross that ensures every disruption can be turned into a business, every interrupted direction can become a livelihood, every unexpected redirection can yield a service that supports more lives. This is why Industry sits in the opening quarter: it gives the mind of humanity material legs.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The life is not the carrier's own in the same way a Right Angle life is. The Left Angle carrier is here for others — their work, their voice, their presence is shaped by what other people need from them. Ra called the Left Angle the publicity department for the Right Angle: the Right Angle has the experience, the Left Angle communicates the lesson.

For Industry 1, the transpersonal expression is teaching others how to take advantage of the fates. The carrier becomes the one who, when life redirects someone away from the plan, points to the possibility hidden in the new direction. They say: "Yes, but look at this. This could be your livelihood. This could be your work. This could be your way."

The not-self distortion is the Left Angle carrier who tries to live as if they were a Right Angle — focused only on their own life, ignoring the transpersonal pull. The Left Angle carrier who refuses to be of use suffers from a chronic sense that their life is not their own; they are correct that it isn't, but the resolution is to live the transpersonal correctly, not to deny it.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle is carried in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra discussed the 5/1 Industry as a heretic who delivers practical heresy — the one who shows others that the unexpected can be turned to profit. The 5/2 Industry is described as the natural industriousness that does not want to teach what it knows; the called 2 hidden in the 5 makes a hermit who refuses to universalise. The 6/2 Industry, when awake, embodies industriousness as way of being rather than means of profit — they are busy being themselves rather than busy producing. The 6/3 closes the cross and is the bridge to the next Right Angle of the wheel.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to follow the [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] of the design vehicle while accepting that the life is transpersonal. The carrier of Industry 1 does not get to make the life entirely about themselves. Their work, their projects, their words, their presence have to be of use to others — that is the karma of the Left Angle.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] becomes either exhausted or grasping. The 20–34 connection that sometimes activates in this design (when the chart includes the channel of Charisma) can drive a carrier into busy-burnout, running endlessly without responding. Or the carrier can become a hustler of the fates, attempting to package and sell what was never theirs to sell. Living from [[Signature]] means being industrious in response to what life actually presents, not in anticipation of what one wants to extract.

The deepest practice for this cross is treating every redirection as raw material. When a plan collapses, when an opportunity disappears, when the fates step in, the carrier does not grieve the lost direction. They look at what is now possible and ask the body whether to commit. The 29 is still the sacral yes; the 30 is still the recognition of feeling. The carrier responds correctly, and the industry follows.
