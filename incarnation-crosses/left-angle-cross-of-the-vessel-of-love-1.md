# Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 1

The Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 1 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Healing 1. The same four gates of the Vessel of Love — 25, 46, 10, 15 — rotate into a Left Angle configuration, which Ra placed in the second half of the wheel and identified as a transpersonal life, a karmic life, a life in which what is being lived is not really for the carrier but for the people the carrier meets. Where the Right Angle carries universal love as a personal destiny, the Left Angle carries it as a service to others. The Healing 1 carrier is here to be a healer for those they encounter, not in the sense of fixing wounds but in the deeper sense of restoring innocence to the people whose paths they cross.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 25 — Innocence (lead gate of the Vessel of Love in Q1)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 46 — Pushing Upward
- **Design Sun:** Gate 10 — Treading
- **Design Earth:** Gate 15 — Modesty
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone, Purpose of Mind)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The gate set is identical to the Right Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 1; what changes is the angle of incarnation. Left Angle Crosses are the second-cycle incarnations of the wheel, the lives in which the soul has already been initiated and is now here in karmic interaction with others. The carrier's life will not make sense if read as their personal story. It only makes sense as the lives they touch.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 25 (Personality Sun) — Innocence

The gate of universal love, the contents of the Vessel. For the Healing 1 carrier, the 25 is the love that radiates from them and that other people register, often without knowing what they have just been touched by. This is the gate that allows the carrier to walk into a room and shift the field of the room simply by being present.

### Gate 46 (Personality Earth) — Pushing Upward

The body as the vessel of healing. Gate 46 grounds the conscious purpose in the carrier's physical form. The Healing 1 lives through the body — touch, presence, the way they sit in a chair, the way they breathe near someone in distress. The body becomes the instrument of the cross.

### Gate 10 (Design Sun) — Treading

The behavior of the self. Beneath the conscious work of healing, the carrier's correct conduct is what does the actual healing. They do not need to perform. They need to be themselves, and the example of their own being is what gives others permission to recover.

### Gate 15 (Design Earth) — Modesty

The love of humanity through extremes. The Healing 1 carrier's aura is wide and tolerant. They draw to themselves the people who have lost their rhythm — the chronically broken, the recovering, the ones whose own pulse has been overwritten by conditioning. The 15 in this configuration is what lets the healing field include the most difficult cases.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is mental. Its purpose is to begin the codification of human experience so that mind can transmit meaning forward. The Healing 1 contributes to that initiation by carrying — at the transpersonal level — the message that innocence can be recovered. The mind of the carrier is here to demonstrate that love survives shock, that universal love is real, and that anyone willing to step into the field of innocence can begin again.

Ra was explicit: this cross is not the same as the Right Angle Vessel of Love. The Right Angle wonders why some have luck and some don't. The Healing 1 walks among the unlucky and quietly restores them, often without speaking, often without ever being identified as the agent of the restoration.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The life is not the carrier's own. Their work is publicising — making available to the collective what the Right Angles have learned. The Healing 1 carrier is therefore a public face of the Vessel of Love. Their suffering, their recoveries, their moments of grace and disgrace, are all happening in the open, where others can see and learn from them.

Ra called Left Angles karmic lives. The carrier of the Healing 1 will meet, again and again, the same archetypes of brokenness — the same wounded patterns, the same cycles of luck and bad luck — until their own innocence has been polished into a clear field that others can drink from. The not-self of this cross is the carrier who tries to be a professional healer in a way the mind can manage. The correct expression is to be the healer the body designed, which often looks like no profession at all.

This cross is one of the strongest Left Angle expressions because the Vessel of Love is naturally transpersonal. Universal love does not belong to anyone. It moves through whoever is willing to host it. The Healing 1 hosts it for an entire quarter — for the part of the wheel where mind is first learning what love is.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle of this cross is carried by five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2 and 6/3.

- **4/6** is the externalising friend whose network becomes the channel of healing — the people who know the carrier are the people who get healed
- **5/1** is the heretic with foundation, the projected-upon healer whose universalising solutions actually work because they are grounded
- **5/2** is the heretic hermit, called for healing but vulnerable to being co-opted by other people's projections
- **6/2** is the role model emerging from the rooftop, holistic healing as a life pattern — Ra said the 6/2 Healing 1 is one of the most natural holistic healers on the wheel
- **6/3** is the role model who moved through trial and error and is now demonstrating, through their visible life, that healing is real

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are non-negotiable. A Left Angle carrier who lives by mind quickly becomes a missionary for ideas about healing that have nothing to do with the actual healing field the body would generate. The work is to be the body, in the room, with whoever shows up, and to let the field do what it was designed to do.

The signature of this cross lived well is people who feel safer, lighter, more themselves after having been near the carrier — even briefly, even silently. The not-self signature is exhaustion, the sense that the carrier is collecting other people's wounds, and the spiritual ego that arises when the mind starts narrating the carrier's role as healer.

The carrier should also be warned: this is not the Right Angle Vessel. There is no private universal love practice. The life is in the open. The healing is happening whether the carrier consents to it or not, and the best service the carrier can offer is to be willing, undefended, and undisplaced from their own body. The healing field does the rest.

When the carrier honours this cross, they become what Ra called the holistic healer of the Quarter of Mind — the one who restores innocence to people whose minds have made them sick. They do not need to advertise this. The right people find them.
