# Right Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2

The Right Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2 carries the Personality Sun in Gate 20 — Contemplation — anchored in the Quarter of Civilization. Gate 20 is the gate of the now, the throat-gate whose entire function is presence in the current moment. To place this gate at the leading edge of the Sleeping Phoenix theme is to create a cross whose carriers are mechanically built for the most demanding form of awakeness — being in the now — and yet are the most asleep beings in the wheel.

The one-line theme: now. The carrier is here to wake up to the present moment so that the mutating spirit can find a vessel that is actually here.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 20 — Contemplation (The Now)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 34 — The Power of the Great
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 20–34 is the conscious axis: the throat voice of the now grounded in the pure individual sacral power. When this axis activates as a defined channel in the chart, it produces the only true Manifesting Generator channel in the bodygraph — the Channel of Charisma. The Design axis 55–59 is the body's emotional engine: the moodiness of spirit grounded in the genetic role gate of intimacy. The carrier is a manifesting-generating body fuelled by mutative emotionality — busy, charismatic, asleep, and capable of waking.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 20 (Personality Sun) — Contemplation

Ra Uru Hu used the image of the Confucian gateway to describe the 20: a freestanding frame on a hilltop path through which one must walk, where everything behind is the past and everything ahead is the future, and the gateway itself is the now. The Personality Sun in the 20 makes the carrier's conscious purpose to stand in that gateway. The not-self carrier is too busy to step into it. The awake carrier becomes the embodiment of presence.

### Gate 34 (Personality Earth) — The Power of the Great

Pure individual sacral power. Ra called the 34 the only gate in the bodygraph that is never compromised by tribal need or collective service — it is power for the sake of power. The Personality Earth grounds the throat voice of the 20 in this pure energetic force, producing the busy, charismatic, mindless doing that the Sleeping Phoenix 2 is mechanically known for.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The unconscious emotional engine. Beneath the conscious busyness, the body is animated by the moodiness of the 55. The carrier is on the wave whether they know it or not. When the wave is full, the busyness has spirit; when the wave is empty, the busyness has no animator and becomes mechanical activity.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The body's intimate ground. The 59 ensures the carrier's life is shaped by intimate biology — by sex, marriage, family, the bonds that hold everyday life together. The Sleeping Phoenix 2 carrier's awakening often comes through betrayal in these intimate bonds, which is why Ra emphasised that the cross wakes through trauma in relationship.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. Ra noted that Sleeping Phoenix 2 carriers are the busiest people in the world, the super-builders of civilisation, the ones whose ceaseless productive activity creates the houses, the institutions, the monuments. They are the drones of form-building, in the bee sense — workers whose collective effort produces what no individual could produce alone.

But Ra also pointed out the tragedy of this. The Sleeping Phoenix 2 builds civilisation while asleep. The pyramids, the cathedrals, the great works of form were built by sleepers who never woke to what they were doing. The Quarter of Civilization runs on this sleepwalking labour, and the cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2 is its archetype.

The mutation of 2027 will not change this. Civilisation will continue to be built by sleepers. What can change is the small number of Sleeping Phoenix 2 carriers who wake — who step into the gateway of the 20, who let the mutating spirit of the 55 find a vessel that is actually present. These few are the seeds of whatever emotional architecture humanity grows into next.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is personal destiny. For Sleeping Phoenix 2, the right-angle expression is the carrier's own mystical death and rebirth — but here the death is specifically the death of misplaced doing. The carrier built a life while asleep, and the awake life requires releasing what was built. Ra emphasised that doing readings for Sleeping Phoenix carriers required the stance of a shaman: you wake them up and they abandon everything.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who stays asleep — productively, charismatically, mindlessly. Ra described them as horses with blinders, capable only of forward motion in the lane they were placed in. They build, they reproduce, they marry, they work, and they never see the gateway of the now they were designed to step through.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

This Right Angle Cross appears in six profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6. Ra noted that the 1/3 has the easiest chance of waking through the breakthrough of the trial-and-error. The 1/4 is one of the hardest because the fixed harmony resists the shattering. The 2/4 Sleeping Phoenix 2 is described as the withdrawn whirlwind — busy in their own enclosed space, requiring the call to be released. The 3/6 closing the Right Angle is the hardest in early life because the trial-and-error compounds the sleepwalking.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] above all else. The 20/34 channel, when defined in this design, makes the carrier a Manifesting Generator whose strategy is to respond — never to initiate the doing, always to wait for the sacral signal. Living from [[Strategy]] is the single most important practice for this cross because the doing-without-responding is the very mechanism of the sleep.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] is the textbook Manifesting Generator burnout. They do everything, finish little, miss steps, skip over what mattered, and never have time to wake up. Ra prescribed lists as a partial corrective — the lists give the busy body something external to track so the mind has something other than the doing to attend to.

The deepest practice for this cross is treating betrayal and trauma as the doorway to waking. Ra was specific: the Sleeping Phoenix is woken by psychological trauma, by the sudden revelation that the life one was living was not real. The carrier cannot manufacture this; they can only refuse to flee it when it arrives. When the veil drops, the awake life can be built — but only if the carrier accepts the death of the life that came before.
