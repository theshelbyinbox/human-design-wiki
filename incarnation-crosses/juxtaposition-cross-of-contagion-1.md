# Juxtaposition Cross of Contagion 1

The Juxtaposition Cross of Contagion 1 is the fixed-fate variant of the Contagion gate set in the Quarter of Initiation. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Juxtaposition Cross of Fates — a name that points directly to its mechanics. The carrier is a fixed point in the larger fate-field of the contagion theme, a 4/1 profile whose life is locked into one specific way of being affected by and affecting the directional shifts that the 30th gate brings into the world.

The one-line theme: the embodied agent of the fates. The carrier is the fate happening to others, and to themselves, on a fixed track that no amount of personal will can redirect.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire (Recognition of Feelings)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal (Saying Yes / Commitment)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure (Power Skills)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation
- **Angle:** Juxtaposition — Fixed Fate

The Juxtaposition shares the same gate set as the Right Angle Cross of Contagion 1, but it manifests exclusively in the 4/1 profile. This profile is what Ra called a fixed harmonic — there is no third-line trial and error in either the personality or the design. The carrier is born with their conscious investigation (1) and their unconscious externalisation (4) locked together as a single, rigid, opportunistic broadcaster of foundation.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 30 (Personality Sun) — The Clinging Fire

The conscious fire of desire. In the Juxtaposition 4/1, this fire is fixed — the carrier does not get to change what they burn for. The desires they were born with, conditioned with, or absorbed in early life become the unchanging focus of the personality.

### Gate 29 (Personality Earth) — The Abysmal

The sacral commitment grounding the fire. In a 4/1 carrier, the 29 anchors the personality into a small set of repeated commitments. The carrier says yes to the same kinds of experiences again and again, building their fixed expertise through repetition rather than experimentation.

### Gate 8 (Design Sun) — Holding Together

The unconscious voice of contribution. The body knows what it is here to contribute, and that contribution is also fixed in the Juxtaposition design. The carrier's unique voice is not negotiable — they were born to say a specific thing, and they will say it on a fixed schedule across their life.

### Gate 14 (Design Earth) — Power Skills

The fuel. The body's sacral power is locked into the same productive pattern across the life. The carrier has sustained energy for their fixed track and very little energy for anything outside it.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the realm of mind. Within this quarter, the Juxtaposition Cross of Fates serves the specific function of being the irreducible fate-event in someone else's experience. While Right Angle Contagion carriers scatter their misdirection across whoever they happen to encounter, and Left Angle Industry carriers translate the misdirection into productive teaching, the Juxtaposition carrier *is* the specific fate that befalls a specific other.

Ra spoke of these carriers as the busy 20–34 force-of-fate beings — when the chart includes the channel of charisma, the Juxtaposition Fates 1 carrier becomes a whirlwind of activity, all of it in tune with deep emotional currents, all of it driven by feelings beyond their comprehension. Ra called them so attuned to the forces that they can tell when the wind god has just woken up. In the not-self, the same carrier curses their fate, repeats the same misdirection on themselves, and never sees the pattern.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. It is the most rigid angle of all — neither personal destiny that one grows into nor transpersonal karma that one teaches outward, but a fixed function in the wheel that the carrier embodies whether or not they understand it. Ra emphasised that 4/1 carriers cannot be argued with, persuaded, or shifted from their foundation. To live with a 4/1 is to accept what they are; to change a 4/1 by force is to break them.

For Juxtaposition Contagion 1, the fixed fate is to be a contagion in others' lives in one specific, repeatable way. Whatever the carrier was conditioned into during their first Saturn return — whatever beliefs, habits, fears, or fascinations were planted in them — those become their fixed broadcast. They cannot stop transmitting them.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who hates their fixedness. They feel cursed; they cannot escape their patterns; they believe themselves trapped in a life they did not choose. The correct expression is the carrier who recognises the fixedness as the design and lets it run cleanly. The contagion they spread, when correct, is irresistibly clarifying for the few people genetically equipped to receive it.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Juxtaposition crosses manifest in the 4/1 profile only. This is the only profile structure that holds the harmonic fixedness Ra associated with the Juxtaposition angle. Approximately 1% of the population carries any given Juxtaposition cross, making this one of the rarer configurations on the wheel.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to follow the [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] of the design vehicle while accepting the fixedness. The 4/1 carrier has the same sacral, emotional, or splenic authority as any other carrier with the same definition; what is fixed is not the authority but the orientation of consciousness around what the body responds to.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] resents the fixedness and spends energy trying to be more flexible than the design permits. They imitate Right Angle versatility or Left Angle teaching, and they fail at both. Living from [[Signature]] means accepting that the fate is fate. The carrier is here to broadcast a specific contagion to a specific audience; their work is not to expand the broadcast but to deliver it cleanly.

The deepest practice for this cross is letting the foundation be the foundation. The 4/1 was conditioned into something during their first thirty years; those things will not unwind. The work is not to dismantle the platform but to ensure the platform is correct. If the foundation was healthy, the contagion is healthy. If the foundation was distorted, the contagion is distorted — and the only practice available is to live correctly from now on, allowing whatever can heal to heal at the body's own pace.
