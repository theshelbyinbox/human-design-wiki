# Juxtaposition Cross of the Trickster

The Juxtaposition Cross of the Trickster is the 4/1 fixed-fate variant that sits between the Right Angle Cross of Rulership 4 and the Left Angle Cross of Confrontation 2 in the Quarter of Mutation. The Personality Sun remains in Gate 26 — the Egoist — but the Design axis rotates to the Channel of Initiation 51/25 (Heart-G). The result is a being fixed in the role of the trickster who sells the spiritual initiation that mutation requires.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 26 (The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 45 (Gathering Together / The King)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 51 (Shock / The Arousing)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 25 (Innocence / The Spirit of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose: Transformation)
- **Angle:** Juxtaposition — Fixed Fate
- **Profile:** 4/1 only

The Personality axis carries the tribal voice of the salesman king. The Design axis carries the Channel of Initiation — the shock that delivers spiritual mutation paired with the innocence of universal love. This is the rare combination of egoic salesmanship with shamanic shock, which produces the trickster archetype in its purest form.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 26 (Personality Sun) — The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist

[[Gate 26]] leads the cross. The being identifies as the egoic salesman, the packager of difficult truths, the operator who makes the unsalable sellable. As a Juxtaposition, this identification is fixed; the carrier cannot easily step out of the salesman role even when they consciously try.

### Gate 45 (Personality Earth) — Gathering Together / The King

[[Gate 45]] grounds the conscious mind in the kingly voice of the tribe. The Trickster being knows themselves as one who has the right to direct the tribe's resources — a conviction operationally useful for the selling work.

### Gate 51 (Design Sun) — Shock / The Arousing

[[Gate 51]] is the Heart Center gate of shock, the lightning that wakes the sleeper. As the unconscious driver, Gate 51 supplies the shamanic intensity that gives the Trickster's selling its mutative edge. The carrier does not just sell — they shock. They wake people up. They deliver the jolt that initiates change.

### Gate 25 (Design Earth) — Innocence / The Spirit of the Self

[[Gate 25]] grounds the unconscious in the innocent love that does not calculate. The Trickster has the universal love of Gate 25 operating beneath the egoic surface, which is what saves the cross from pure manipulation. The selling and the shock arise from a current of innocence, even when the surface looks calculating.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation places this Juxtaposition in the realm where transformation is the purpose. The Trickster being is structurally built to be a mutative operator — a fixed point on the wheel where egoic selling meets shamanic shock to produce initiation.

## The Angle Expression

Juxtaposition crosses carry Fixed Fate. The Trickster being is locked in the trickster role. The 4/1 profile reinforces the network-bound, foundationally-locked structure — the trickster works within a defined circle of influence and operates from deep foundational knowledge of how the audience receives information.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Juxtaposition Crosses appear exclusively in the 4/1 profile.

## Living This Cross

For the 4/1 Trickster, the work is to honor the trickster role without becoming the con artist. The cross is structurally oriented toward selling shock — making the new acceptable through clever packaging — and the carrier cannot escape this orientation. The question is whether the selling serves genuine mutation or whether it serves personal advantage.

The shadow path is the trickster who manipulates without serving the collective, who uses 51's shock to traumatize rather than initiate, who deploys 26's salesmanship for extraction rather than contribution. Ra was clear that egoic configurations require honesty about their nature; pretending the ego is not operating produces the most destructive shadow expressions.

The medicine is to honor the unconscious 25 — to let the innocent love that operates beneath the surface guide the conscious egoic work. The Trickster who remembers that the love is what they are actually delivering, with the shock and the selling as the vehicles, becomes a fixed point of mutative initiation in their close circle.

The 4 profile means the influence operates through the network. The Trickster's transmissions land most powerfully within their defined circle of friends, colleagues, and contacts. Broadcasting widely dilutes the effect; concentrating on the close circle amplifies it.

The 1 unconscious means the carrier requires deep foundational study. The Trickster who has done the work to understand their audience, their topic, and themselves can deliver shock and sale with precision. The Trickster who skips the foundational work delivers nothing but performance.

When integrated, the Juxtaposition Cross of the Trickster produces a fixed point of egoic mutative authority. The carrier becomes the friend, colleague, or partner who can shock the close circle awake to what they need to see, packaging the shock in language they can receive, and grounding the whole operation in the innocent love that makes the shock medicinal rather than traumatic.

This is the trickster as initiator, the salesman as shaman, the egoist whose ego serves the mutation rather than itself.

The combination of Gate 51 and Gate 26 in the same cross is unusual. Gate 51 is the shock gate of the Heart Center, the lightning bolt of competitive shamanic awakening. Gate 26 is the salesman gate of the Heart Center, the egoic packager. Both are Heart gates, which means the entire egoic apparatus of the BodyGraph is engaged. The Trickster being lives at the apex of egoic intensity — the Heart Center is fully active in their design even if not all gates are personally activated — and the cross's fixedness means they cannot easily moderate this intensity.

This is part of why the Juxtaposition Cross of the Trickster is so rare and so specifically charged. The 4/1 profile combined with the egoic intensity of the gate set produces a being who is naturally controversial. The trickster's selling is uncomfortable to those who prefer steady, predictable communication. The trickster's shock is unsettling to those who prefer gentle initiation. The carrier learns early that they cannot please everyone, and that attempting to do so collapses the entire mechanism of the cross.

The 4/1 also produces a being who is deeply attached to home, network, and foundational worldview. This anchoring is what saves the trickster from becoming purely chaotic. The chaos of 51's shock and the cunning of 26's salesmanship are grounded in the steady network of 4 and the foundational study of 1. The Trickster has a stable life from which they deploy their tricks; they are not nomadic operators but rooted figures who work the close circle they know.

Ra noted that the Quarter of Mutation produces beings whose work is to deliver transformation, and that the egoic configurations in this quarter are especially powerful because mutation requires the willingness to be unsettling. The Trickster carrier accepts the role of being uncomfortable to others as the price of doing mutative work. They are not trying to be loved by all; they are trying to deliver shock that produces awakening, packaged in language that produces understanding, grounded in love that produces trust.
