# Left Angle Cross of the Sphinx 2

The Left Angle Cross of the Sphinx 2 carries the same four gates as the right-angle variant — Gates 2, 1, 13 and 7 — with the Personality Sun in Gate 2, the Receptive. The change of angle from right to left changes the relationship between the carrier and the world. The carrier's directional gift is no longer a private compass for their own life; it is a transpersonal compass woven into the lives of others.

The left-angle Sphinx 2 directs the form of civilization not by their own movement alone, but by being placed in the lives of others as a directional presence. People feel the carrier's body sense which way to go and instinctively follow.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 2 — The Receptive (Higher Knowing / The Driver)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 1 — The Creative (Self-Expression)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 13 — The Listener
- **Design Earth:** Gate 7 — Role of the Self
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

Gate composition matches the right-angle Sphinx 2. The mechanical difference is the angle of the Sun at incarnation, which positions this life into the transpersonal half of the wheel. The carrier's directional sense is karmic — it lands in other people's lives whether or not the carrier wants it to.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 2 (Personality Sun) — The Receptive / Higher Knowing

The conscious purpose remains the body's pre-cognitive knowing of where the form must go. In the left angle, this knowing is broadcast — it leaks. People around the carrier sense where the carrier's body is pointing and orient themselves accordingly without the carrier saying a word.

This makes the left-angle Sphinx 2 carrier one of the great quiet directors of social form. They do not lead through speech; they lead through the orientation of their own body in a room.

### Gate 1 (Personality Earth) — The Creative / Self-Expression

The grounding self-expression. The carrier's individual creative signature gives the directional broadcast its particular flavour. Two left-angle Sphinx 2 carriers will both direct, but each will do so with a distinct aesthetic, voice and presence.

### Gate 13 (Design Sun) — The Listener / Fellowship of Man

Underneath the conscious direction is unconscious witnessing. The body of the carrier has heard, absorbed and metabolised the stories of the people they are about to direct. The directional sense the carrier broadcasts is informed by every story the unconscious has quietly recorded.

### Gate 7 (Design Earth) — Role of the Self

The unconscious carrier of role. Beneath the directional work there is an underlying leadership identity. People recognise this carrier as someone whose presence carries authority — even when the carrier has not claimed any.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the quarter of form. Its purpose is the building of social, material and aesthetic structure. Sphinx 2 opens this quarter as the directional engine of form-building.

The left-angle variant places the directional engine into transpersonal service. The carrier does not direct their own civilization in isolation; they direct the form of the civilizations they enter. Their body, simply by being where it is, points the group in the direction the group's body needed to go.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle is transpersonal karma. The carrier's life is not their own. Their decisions, movements and orientations carry weight in the lives of others. Ra Uru Hu described left-angle carriers as having "no exit" from this entanglement — the karma is built into the design.

For Left Angle Cross of the Sphinx 2, the entanglement is directional. People follow this carrier into rooms, neighbourhoods, decisions, even into geographic moves, and often they cannot articulate why. The carrier's body is doing the work, and the field around the carrier registers it.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to manage this through the mind. The mind, sensing the followers, panics and tries to issue instructions — and the followers, who were responding to the body, get false directions from the mind and become confused. The remedy is the carrier returning to their own [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]], letting the body do its work, and trusting that what others receive will land correctly without verbal mediation.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left-angle profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, 6/3. The directional karma flavours through each:

- **4/6** is the Opportunist whose directional gift moves through three life phases; the close network is the early channel, public recognition the late channel
- **5/1** is the Heretic whose direction is projected onto by strangers as universal answer
- **5/2** is the Heretic-Hermit whose direction is broadcast despite reluctance; the carrier is built for public role
- **6/2** is the Role Model-Hermit whose direction matures with age — the elder whose body knows where to go
- **6/3** is the Role Model-Martyr whose direction is forged through visible mistakes that become wisdom

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] govern correct movement. The directional gift is reliable only when the carrier honours their own internal navigation. The not-self carrier reads the broadcast effect of their own body and starts to perform direction — pointing for show, moving for effect, leading by mind rather than by form. The correct carrier lets the body lead and lets the broadcast take care of itself.

The signature lived well is karmic placement — the sense that wherever the carrier ends up, the move turns out, in retrospect, to have been the right move for everyone involved. The signature lived poorly is the carrier who moves the wrong people in the wrong direction because the mind tried to manage what the body already knew.

Ra Uru Hu's phrase about Gate 2 applies in heightened form here: "what the second gate knows, what the Driver knows, is where it has to go." — Ra Uru Hu. For the left-angle Sphinx 2, where the body has to go is also where a small piece of civilization has to go.
