# Left Angle Cross of Laws 3

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 3 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Wishes 2 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Laws cross in Quarter 3. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 50]] (The Cauldron) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Duality, where purpose moves through Bonding, the Left Angle Laws 3 is the cross of the transpersonal lawkeeper — the carrier whose splenic value-recognition is karmically routed toward specific others whose bonded lives need exactly this kind of ethical anchoring.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 50]] (The Cauldron / Values), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 3]] (Ordering), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 60]] or [[Gate 56]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 50 / 3
- Design Axis: 60 / 56
- Quarter: Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter)
- Realm Purpose: Bonding
- Mystical Theme: Measure for Measure
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 50 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 50]] in the Left Angle is the splenic values-recognition turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Consistency — the expected lawgiver, projected onto as the one who will faithfully transmit values; the matriarch, the elder, the trusted enforcer. At line 6 the gate carries The Leader — the mature custodian who holds the values lightly and lives them deeply, so others adopt them by attraction rather than enforcement.

The name "Wishes" given to this Left Angle Laws 3 signals the transpersonal nature of the work. The carrier brings value-recognition to others' wishes — the new shapes the others' longings are trying to take, the bonded arrangements the others are trying to build. The Left Angle Laws 3 carrier helps other people see whether the values underlying their wishes are alive or rotted.

### Gate 3 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 3]] at line 5 is Victimization — the projection field of the leader through confusion, who in the not-self becomes the target of blame. At line 6 it is Surrender — accepting that not every beginning will find its order in this life. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal lawkeeping is rooted in the mutative awareness that values must be updated as the karma of the bonded community requires.

### Gate 60 (Design)

[[Gate 60]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's pulse-mechanism into the cross. Line 2 is Decisiveness — sharp action when the pulse turns on. Line 3 is Conservatism — experimental engagement with limitation. These design lines give the body the practical capacity to act when the karmic moment arrives.

### Gate 56 (Design)

[[Gate 56]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's storytelling capacity into the cross. Line 2 is Connection — natural rapport with the audience. Line 3 is Distraction — trial-and-error in finding which stories land. These design lines give the carrier the means to transmit the values through narrative.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality channels purpose through Bonding. The Laws cross in this quarter is the cross of tribal law — the bedrock values that hold bonded communities together. The Left Angle variant routes the lawkeeping into transpersonal karma. The carrier's value-recognition is offered to specific others whose bonded lives need exactly this kind of ethical anchoring.

The mystical theme of Quarter 3 is Measure for Measure — the reciprocal nature of bonded life. For the Left Angle Laws 3 carrier, this reciprocity operates through the karmic encounters: the carrier offers value-recognition, and the encounter returns to the carrier the lived experience of what bonded ethics actually require.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Laws 3 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges in the field of bonded values, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned analytically. The karmic encounters arrive in their own timing. Only [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] can recognize the right ones.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 3 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator lawkeeper. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental life-arcs that distill into role-model lawkeeping.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Laws 3 life unfolds through bonded encounters in which the carrier's value-recognition reorganizes another person's ethical compass. A friend stuck in a relationship whose values have rotted meets the carrier and finally sees what the splenic alertness of the lawkeeper has been seeing all along. A community wrestling with whether to update an old commitment meets the carrier and, through a moment of recognition, understands which values must hold and which must be released.

This work happens in the now, splenically, in flashes. The 50 awareness gate operates in the present tense, not deliberatively. The Left Angle Laws 3 carrier learns over time to speak the splenic recognition when it arrives and to release attachment to the result.

The 5th-line projection field is intense. Others will project onto the Left Angle Laws 3 carrier the role of the ultimate ethical authority, the one who must adjudicate every dispute and pronounce on every value-question. Without [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]], the carrier becomes conscripted into this role and either burns out under the responsibility (the over-caretaker shadow Ra warned about) or, in the corruption shadow, uses the role for personal gain.

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only protection. The 50's fear of responsibility, when honored as calibration, keeps the carrier from taking on more than they can genuinely hold. The work is to recognize which karmic encounters are theirs to receive and which are projections from a community accustomed to leaning on the lawkeeper.

The 3 Earth keeps the work mutative. The values the carrier helps others recognize must be allowed to update as the bonded life requires. Static lawkeeping becomes dead enforcement; mutative lawkeeping becomes the living ethics that helps each generation inherit a working system.

The 60 design provides the body's pulse-mechanism. The 56 design provides the storytelling capacity that allows the value-transmissions to be communicable.

The deeper teaching of this cross is that tribal law operates across the whole human field, not just inside any one community. The Left Angle Laws 3 carrier is one of the karmic agents through whom values move between communities — the elder whose presence in someone else's tribe helps that tribe recognize what its own splenic awareness has been trying to say. The carrier's own growth happens through the same encounters: every life their value-recognition touched, in turn, refines the carrier's understanding of what bonded ethics actually require.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Laws 3 person is often experienced as the unexpected ethical anchor — the friend who arrives in a moment of bonded crisis and helps the situation find its proper values. From Measure for Measure, the reciprocal architecture of bonded life, comes the karmic exchange that shapes this cross: the carrier gives value-recognition, the encounter gives the carrier a lived education in what values actually mean.
