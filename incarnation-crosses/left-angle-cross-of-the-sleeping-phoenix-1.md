# Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 1

The Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 1 is the transpersonal expression of the Sleeping Phoenix gate set in the Quarter of Initiation. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Left Angle Cross of Spirit — the publicity department, as he liked to put it, for the Right Angle's mutation of the emotional system. Where the Right Angle carrier is busy and asleep, the Left Angle Spirit carrier is here to sell creative spirit to the world, to be the clarion call that awakens the spirit of humanity to itself.

The one-line theme: the marketing of spirit through creative provocation. The carrier exists to use art, sexuality, and emotional pulse to wake the world to something it does not yet know it is becoming.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 59 — Dispersion (Intimacy / Sexuality)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Sun:** Gate 34 — The Power of the Great
- **Design Earth:** Gate 20 — Contemplation (The Now)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 1 variant places the Personality Sun in Gate 59 — the genetic role gate of sexuality and intimate union — with the spirit-fire of the 55 sitting as the earth grounding the consciousness. The design axis flips so that the pure sacral power of Gate 34 leads the body, with Gate 20 sitting as the design earth. This rotation makes the carrier a transpersonal force of intimate and creative provocation, broadcasting spirit outward rather than waiting to be woken.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 59 (Personality Sun) — Dispersion

The 59 is the gate that breaks down barriers to achieve union. In the Personality Sun, the carrier's conscious life is shaped by sexual and intimate provocation — the impulse to penetrate, seduce, or be open to the other. Ra emphasised that the 59 is a genetic role gate; its primary biology is reproduction, but its consciousness in this cross is broader: it is the dispersion of self into the other so that something new can be created.

### Gate 55 (Personality Earth) — Spirit / Abundance

The earth is the moodiest emotional ground in the entire wheel. The 55's uncertainty, its fickleness, its melancholy provide the field beneath the dispersion of the 59. The carrier's intimate provocations are fuelled by the spirit-fire of the 55 — sometimes radiant, sometimes desolate, always pulsing.

### Gate 34 (Design Sun) — The Power of the Great

The body's engine is pure individual sacral power. Ra called the 34 the only gate that is pure power, never compromised by tribal need or collective service. In the Design Sun, this gives the Left Angle Spirit carrier a body that can sustain prolonged creative or sexual broadcast without depletion — as long as it is responding correctly.

### Gate 20 (Design Earth) — Contemplation

The design earth is the now. The body knows it is here in the present moment, and the now is where the spirit can be transmitted. The 20 grounds the busyness of the 34 into the present — the carrier cannot broadcast spirit from the past or the future, only from this moment.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the realm of mind. The mind of humanity is initiated through every cross in this quarter, but Spirit 1 carries a specific function: it is the cross that markets the mutation. The Right Angle Sleeping Phoenix is the carrier of the mutation as personal death. The Left Angle Spirit is the carrier of the mutation as public art.

Ra emphasised that this is one of the most creative configurations on the wheel because three of its four gates (55, 59, 34) are deeply individual, mutative gates, and their combination produces beings who pulse with the creative spirit. The drumming festivals, the artists, the seducers, the heretics of spirit — they are often carriers of this cross. They are here to provoke the awakening, not to wake themselves.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is shaped by what others need from the carrier. For Spirit 1, the transpersonal pull is the awakening of spirit in the people the carrier touches — sometimes through art, sometimes through sexuality, sometimes through the provocation of presence itself. The carrier's intimate partners, audiences, and witnesses are the ground of their work.

Ra was specific that this cross can be deeply heretical. The 5/1 Spirit may sell heresies that the collective finds threatening — euthanasia, sexual freedom, creative iconoclasm — because the spirit it serves is not the spirit of the tribe. The Left Angle Spirit answers to the mutation, not to the established emotional order. This makes its carriers, when they are correct, deeply provocative cultural figures and, when they are not, depressed nihilists who sell hopelessness.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who has the creative configuration but cannot find the spirit. They live in chronic emotional disturbance, in difficulty with intimacy, in the failure to find a reliable partner. The same chemistry that makes the awake Spirit 1 a great artist makes the asleep Spirit 1 a great depressive.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Spirit manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra described the 5/1 Spirit as the heretic of creative spirit — the one who sells provocations. The 5/2 carries the natural creative force but does not want to explain itself. The 6/2 Spirit, when awake, is a deeply creative role model. The 6/3 closes the Left Angle and is the last carrier of the lineage before the cross rotates into the next angle of the wheel.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept that the life is transpersonal. The carrier of Spirit 1 cannot make the life only about themselves — their intimate partners, their audiences, the people who feel them in passing are part of the design. Ra emphasised that this is a difficult social cross; intimacy is the work of the life, and intimacy is hardest in carriers built to seduce.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] uses the seductive power without the underlying spirit, draining themselves and the people they touch. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the creative pulse find its outlet — art, ritual, intimacy, music — and trusting the [[Inner Authority]] to know when the pulse is correct and when it is the not-self trying to compensate for emptiness.

The deepest practice for this cross is honouring the fickleness of the 55. The Spirit 1 carrier does not get certainty. They get the wave. Some days the spirit is full and the work pours out; other days the spirit is empty and there is nothing to give. The carrier learns to ride the wave rather than fight it. When spirit is present, they create. When spirit is absent, they wait. This is the only way the lifelong creative pulse can be sustained.
