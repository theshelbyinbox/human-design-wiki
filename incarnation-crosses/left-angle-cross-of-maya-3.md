# Left Angle Cross of Maya 3

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 3 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Limitation 2 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 3. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 32]] (Continuity / Duration) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Duality, where purpose moves through Bonding, the Left Angle Maya 3 is the cross of the transpersonal conservator — the carrier whose splenic continuity-judgment is karmically routed toward specific others whose bonds need exactly this discernment.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 32]] (Continuity / Duration), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 42]] (Growth / Increase), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 62]] or [[Gate 61]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 32 / 42
- Design Axis: 62 / 61
- Quarter: Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter)
- Realm Purpose: Bonding
- Mystical Theme: Measure for Measure
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 32 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 32]] in the Left Angle is the conservator's continuity-judgment turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Flexibility — the projection field that makes the carrier the one others look to in moments of change for the model of how to preserve what matters while adapting to new conditions. At line 6 the gate carries Tranquility — the mature elder who has settled, beyond worry, into knowing what will last. In both higher lines, the conservator's work is karmically offered to specific others.

The name "Limitation" attached to this Left Angle variant signals the deeper teaching: the carrier sees, in the now, what cannot endure. Naming the limit is part of the transpersonal work. The bonds the carrier helps others see clearly are often bonds that need to end — and the karmic offering is the willingness to be present for that ending.

### Gate 42 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 42]] at line 5 is Self-Actualization — the universalizing projection field through which the carrier's completed cycles become templates for others. At line 6 it is Cultivation — the elder who has completed many cycles and now holds space for the natural ripening of others. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal continuity-work is rooted in cycle-completion offered as teaching.

### Gate 62 (Design)

[[Gate 62]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's detail-instinct into the cross. At line 2 the body has reserved, selective precision; at line 3 it has experimental, trial-and-error precision. Either way, the design provides the practical scaffolding for the transpersonal work — the names, dates, and verifiable specifics that anchor the conservator's judgments in something others can check.

### Gate 61 (Design)

[[Gate 61]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the head-pressure of inner truth into the body. At line 2 the carrier receives insight in solitude; at line 3 the carrier tests mystical insight through real-life trial. This design-level mysticism gives the transpersonal conservator-work its undertone of larger meaning — the carrier is not just judging which bonds will hold, but doing so under a quiet mandate to see through the maya of bonded life itself.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality channels purpose through Bonding. The mystical theme of Measure for Measure points at the reciprocal architecture of tribal life — every bond is an exchange, every continuity is a reciprocal commitment. The Maya cross in this quarter is the cross of the bonded conservator. The Left Angle variant routes the conservator-work into transpersonal karma.

The carrier is here to meet specific people whose bonded lives need the carrier's continuity-discernment. The encounters are the life. The conservator-work happens through the meetings — the conversations, the moments of recognition, the silent splenic alertness offered in the company of those whose karma has brought them to the carrier.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Maya 3 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges in the field of bonded continuity, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned in advance. The carrier cannot select their karmic encounters analytically. Only [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] — sacral response for [[Generator]] designs, emotional clarity for emotional authorities, splenic in-the-now alertness for splenic ones — can recognize the right people as they arrive.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 3 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator within bonded life. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental life-arcs that distill into role-model wisdom in the third phase.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Maya 3 life unfolds through bonded encounters in which the carrier's continuity-judgment changes what others are willing to see. A marriage one of the carrier's clients is clinging to gets named, by the carrier, as already ended. A business partnership a friend is afraid to formalize gets named, by the carrier, as solid enough to commit to. A family pattern someone has carried unconsciously for years gets named, by the carrier, as the load it has been.

This is delicate work. The 32's splenic discernment is offered in the now, once, in passing. Trying to repeat the judgment or extend it into a sustained teaching dissolves the gift. The Left Angle Maya 3 carrier learns over time to speak the splenic note when it arrives and then to step back, leaving the other person to do their own work with the information.

The fear of failure attached to the 32 is amplified for the Left Angle carrier because the failures the carrier names are not the carrier's own — they are someone else's. The work is to keep the discernment honest and to release attachment to the outcome of any one encounter. The karma is the encounter, not the result.

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] keep the work clean. Without them, the conservator's judgment becomes opinion, and the carrier becomes the friend who is always pronouncing on other people's relationships. With them, the judgment arrives only when invited, only when correct, and lands with the weight that the splenic in-the-now carries.

The 42 Earth grounds the work in the rhythm of cycles. The carrier learns over a lifetime that bonded cycles have specific arcs, and that the right time to name the limit is usually not the first or last moment of the cycle but the precise turn-point inside it.

The 61 design pulse keeps the work humble. The named bond, even when correctly named, is still inside a maya that is larger than any name. The carrier who forgets this becomes the projected judge whose pronouncements harden into a brand. The carrier who remembers it becomes the trusted elder whose continuity-discernment carries depth.

The deeper teaching is that bonded life is the densest layer of the maya, and the people who can see through it for others perform a transpersonal service. The Left Angle Maya 3 carrier is one of these people. Their life is the encounters. Their growth, in turn, comes from the people their discernment was offered to.
