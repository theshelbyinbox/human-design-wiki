# Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 1

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 1 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Completion — is the fixed-fate variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 1. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 42]] (Growth / Increase), and the entire architecture of the cross is locked into the energy of finishing what was begun. Juxtaposition crosses are the rarest type, present only in the 4/1 profile, and Ra described them as pure accents — a tightly fixed, condensed expression of the cross's underlying theme. In the Quarter of Initiation, with its purpose anchored in Mind, the Juxtaposition of Completion is the mental and behavioral fixity around closing cycles fully.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 42]] (Growth / Increase), Line 4
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 32]] (Continuity / Duration), Line 4
- Design Sun: [[Gate 61]] or [[Gate 62]], Line 1
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun, Line 1
- Personality Axis: 42 / 32
- Design Axis: 61 / 62
- Quarter: Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone)
- Realm Purpose: Mind
- Mystical Theme: The Witness Returns
- Angle Expression: Juxtaposition — fixed fate
- Profile: 4/1 only

## The Four Gates

### Gate 42 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 42]] in the Juxtaposition is the fixed orientation toward completion. The fourth-line projection field of the 4/1 profile colors this gate with relational continuity — the carrier completes cycles in the context of their friendships, family, and chosen network. Ra emphasized that the 42 line 4 is the Middle Way, finding maturation through relationship. The Juxtaposition seals this energy into a fixed life-task: this person is here to finish, in the company of those bonded to them, the cycles that life sets in motion.

### Gate 32 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 32]] grounds the cross in the splenic recognition of what will endure. In Juxtaposition Maya 1, this is the Earth that holds the carrier rooted in tribal continuity — the family, the household, the small economy. The 32 line 4 is the line of right action within the network of bonds. The fixed nature of Juxtaposition means the splenic discernment about long-term value operates as a constant: the carrier's life is anchored in the in-the-moment alertness to which arrangements are real and which are about to fail.

### Gate 61 (Design)

[[Gate 61]] in the design imprint, line 1, is the foundational expression of inner-truth pressure — the private, occult knowing that does not need to be broadcast. As a design gate, this means the body carries a quiet, fixed pulse of mystical attention. The carrier does not necessarily talk about the mystery; they simply walk through life under its pressure, completing their cycles with an undercurrent of attention to what lies beneath the visible.

### Gate 62 (Design)

[[Gate 62]] in the design imprint, line 1, is Routine — the foundational expression of detail-attention. The body carries a fixed orientation toward the small specifics that make a system work. Ra taught that the maya is concretized through naming, and the 62 line 1 supplies the daily, reliable maintenance of detail that allows a cycle to be witnessed accurately from start to finish.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation channels purpose through Mind. The maya in this quarter is the maya of accumulated mental record — the abstract patterns by which humanity remembers itself. The Juxtaposition of Completion sits inside this realm as a pure accent on closure: the carrier's purpose is to finish what was started, in the mental and experiential mode that the quarter requires. The Witness Returns to the cycle, sees it through to its proper end, and contributes the closed loop to the collective record.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. Ra described Juxtapositions as pure accents — the energy of the cross is concentrated and locked. The 4/1 profile that carries every Juxtaposition is itself the most fixed profile in the system, with both lines being personal-lower-trigram (4 = opportunity, 1 = foundation). The Juxtaposition person is not here for the experimental life of the Right Angle nor the transpersonal karma of the Left Angle. They are here for a specific, narrow, accented expression — in this case, completion.

The fixity of the Juxtaposition does not mean the carrier has no choices. It means the carrier's [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] operate inside a narrower band of correct expression. There are fewer right paths and many wrong ones. The work is to recognize the fixed channel of one's life and live deeply inside it rather than waste energy reaching for lives that were never available.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 1 manifests only in the 4/1 profile. The 4 line is the Opportunist — the externalized, networked, friend-based expression. The 1 line is the Investigator — the deep, foundational study that must be done in private. Together the 4/1 is the person who networks their foundation. The Juxtaposition further fixes this profile into a specific accent: completion. The 4/1 Juxtaposition Maya 1 carrier is here to be the friend-network completer, the person whose foundational study of cycles makes them the one their community calls when something needs to be finished correctly.

## Living This Cross

Living the Juxtaposition of Completion means embracing the fixity of the role. The carrier is not designed to start everything, lead everything, or transform everything. They are designed to finish. The mind will want to reach beyond the role — to become an initiator, a leader, a visionary. The cross teaches the opposite: stay inside the accent. The 42 sacral, when followed through correct response, knows which cycles are this person's to complete. The 32 spleen, in the moment, knows which of those will endure.

The 4/1 profile makes friendship the operating field. The carrier's cycles of completion happen in the company of specific bonded others — and these others are the people for whom the completion is meaningful. Solo completion in a private vacuum tends to dissipate the cross's purpose. Completion witnessed and supported by chosen community produces the fullest expression.

The deeper teaching is that fixity is not limitation but precision. The Juxtaposition Maya 1 carrier has a smaller life than the Right Angle and a less transpersonal life than the Left Angle, but inside their accent they can be more precise, more reliable, more deeply rooted than either. The maya completes itself through their work. The cycles get closed. The record gets made. The community feels held because someone, in their fixed way, finished what needed finishing.

In family and tribal life, the Juxtaposition of Completion person becomes the trusted closer — the one who does not abandon a project, a relationship, or an obligation halfway through. Their splenic in-the-now reliability is their gift. Their mystical undercurrent (the design 61) and their detail-orientation (the design 62) operate beneath the surface, giving their closing-work depth and accuracy. The witness, in their case, returns by quietly finishing.

The shadow expression is the 4/1 who has lost their inner-truth pulse and become mechanical — going through the motions of completion without any feeling for which cycles are theirs. They become the person who finishes things compulsively, often things that should never have been started, and the maya becomes for them a treadmill rather than a curriculum. Returning to [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] restores the discernment that makes the fixed accent meaningful.
