# Right Angle Cross of the Sphinx 4

The Right Angle Cross of the Sphinx 4 opens the Quarter of Mutation — the fourth and final quarter of the wheel, where the purpose is transformation itself. With the Personality Sun in Gate 1, the Creative, the carrier of this cross arrives carrying the most yang hexagram in the I Ching as their conscious purpose. Six unbroken lines. Pure originating force. The mutative self-expression that introduces the new into culture.

Sphinx 4 is the cross of the originating creative as cultural mutation. Where Sphinx 1 listened, Sphinx 2 directed and Sphinx 3 led, Sphinx 4 expresses — and the expression itself is the engine through which the species moves forward.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 1 — The Creative / Self-Expression
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 2 — The Receptive / Higher Knowing
- **Design Sun:** Gate 7 — The Army / Role of the Self
- **Design Earth:** Gate 13 — The Listener / Fellowship of Man
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The consciousness axis 1–2 is the engine of original expression seated in directional form. Pure creative spirit pointed by pure receptive knowing. The unconscious axis 7–13 supplies the collective grounding: the role of the self in interaction and the witness of human experience. The carrier expresses something individuated; underneath, the body has been quietly absorbing the collective story it is about to mutate.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 1 (Personality Sun) — The Creative / Self-Expression

The conscious purpose of this life is unique self-expression. Ra Uru Hu described Gate 1 as a gate yearning for a voice — not in the sense of needing applause, but in the sense of an inner artist needing to come through without distortion. For the Sphinx 4 carrier, this expression is not optional. It is the cross's purpose.

The shadow of Gate 1 is the carrier who has not yet found the medium through which the expression can land. The gift is the carrier who has — and whose expression becomes culturally legible, mutative, unmistakably their own.

### Gate 2 (Personality Earth) — The Receptive / Higher Knowing

Grounds the creative in direction. Gate 2 knows where the form must go. For Sphinx 4 this means the creative expression is not abstract or directionless; it is pointed. The carrier's body knows where the expression must move, what audience or material it must reach.

### Gate 7 (Design Sun) — The Army / Role of the Self

The unconscious engine of role. Beneath the conscious creative work, Sphinx 4 carries a recognisable role in the collective. The expression that comes out is not anonymous — it carries a particular alpha signature, a particular leadership of cultural form, whether the carrier consciously intended it or not.

### Gate 13 (Design Earth) — The Fellowship of Man

The unconscious listener. Underneath the expression there is a lifetime of quiet witnessing. Sphinx 4 mutates the culture by drawing on a substrate of absorbed human experience that they did not consciously gather. The expression that emerges is informed by stories the carrier never asked to hear.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the quarter of transformation. Its work is to introduce the new — the genuinely unprecedented — into the human experience. Every cross in this quarter contributes to mutation in a different way.

Sphinx 4 opens the quarter by carrying mutation as creative expression. The change does not arrive through argument or revolution; it arrives through the originality of one voice that the culture had not heard before. Ra called the individual circuit the circuit of empowerment through mutation, and Gate 1 is its leading creative signature. Sphinx 4 is the cross that places that signature at the opening of the mutation quarter.

## The Angle Expression

The Right Angle of this cross is the personal destiny of the originating self. The carrier's life is the work; their expression is their own. The not-self distortion is the carrier who waits for permission to express, or who tries to express in someone else's voice, or who suppresses the originating impulse because the culture has not yet recognised the medium.

For Sphinx 4 the right-angle work is patient. The expression matures through the carrier's own life, not through external instruction. There are no shortcuts. Ra Uru Hu's framing of the right angle holds especially here: the carrier is not here to find someone else's secret; they are here to find their own. The originating impulse is the secret, and it can only emerge through the carrier's own honoured strategy and authority.

When honoured, this cross produces what Ra called the creative role model — the person whose unique self-expression becomes a permission slip for others to find their own. The mutation is not in the carrier alone. It is in the way the carrier's life unlocks creative permission in everyone who witnesses it.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle profiles for Sphinx 4 are 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5 and 3/6:

- **1/3** investigates the medium of expression through study, then refines through trial-and-error
- **1/4** investigates the medium and lets close friends carry the work outward
- **2/4** is the natural hermit whose expression is drawn out into the world by friends
- **2/5** is the called hermit projected onto as creative saviour
- **3/5** is the trial-and-error creative whose mutations are visible to all
- **3/6** moves through three life phases of expression — experimenter, observer, role model

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] govern correct expression for Sphinx 4. The creative urge of Gate 1 is intense and can spike at any moment; the work is to filter that urge through the body's response so the expression lands in form rather than dissipating.

The not-self signature is creative frustration — the carrier full of original impulse who cannot find the channel to deliver it. The signature lived well is the originator at peace with their voice — the carrier whose creative life has the quality of inevitability about it, as if what they made could not have come from anyone else.

Ra Uru Hu summarised the gift of Gate 1 this way: "We as humans are not creative — we are reactive. But because we are unique and we take in the experience of life uniquely, what comes out of us as a reflection is unique." — Ra Uru Hu. The Sphinx 4 carrier embodies this principle as a whole life.
