# Right Angle Cross of Contagion 3

The Right Angle Cross of Contagion 3 carries the Personality Sun in Gate 29 — the Abysmal — anchored in the Quarter of Duality. Gate 29 is the sacral yes, the commitment to enter experience, the gate Ra Uru Hu called the Draftee. To place this gate at the leading edge of the Contagion theme in the Quarter of Bonding is to create a cross whose carriers are here to commit to relationship — and to spread that commitment as contagion to whoever they bond with.

The one-line theme: the yes that commits. Saturation and perseverance in bonded experience are the carrier's lifelong fate.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal (Saying Yes)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire (Recognition of Feelings)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure (Power Skills)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 29–30 is the consciousness axis: the sacral yes grounded in the fire of desire. The Design axis 14–8 is the body's engine: the sacral power that retains resources grounded in the throat voice of unique contribution. Together this creates a being whose conscious purpose is committed presence in relationship, fuelled by deep desire, supported by power and voice.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 29 (Personality Sun) — The Abysmal

Gate 29 is the sacral yes — the commitment to enter experience all the way through. Ra called this gate the Draftee, the one who accepts being conscripted into life. In the Personality Sun, the carrier's conscious purpose is shaped by their yeses. They commit to relationships, projects, experiences with full sacral force, and they are obligated to follow through.

### Gate 30 (Personality Earth) — The Clinging Fire

The fire of desire grounds the yes. The carrier's commitments are not made coolly; they are made in the heat of recognition, in the moment when the fire of the 30 says "this experience could fulfil." The combination of 29 and 30 makes the Contagion 3 carrier capable of intensely emotional, intensely committed bonding.

### Gate 14 (Design Sun) — Power Skills

The unconscious sacral fuel. Beneath the consciousness drama of the yes and the fire, the body has the sustained power to deliver on the commitments. The 14 ensures the carrier can do the work that relationships require — the years of attention, the resources, the labour of staying bonded.

### Gate 8 (Design Earth) — Holding Together

The body's voice of contribution. Beneath all the bonding, the body knows what it is here to contribute. The 8 in the Design Earth keeps the carrier's bonded life pointed at the unique offering of self to other.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. Its purpose is to negotiate the polarity of self and other — to figure out how two separate beings can be in relationship with each other across the inherent gap between them. Each cross in this quarter takes a different stance on the duality problem. Contagion 3 takes the abstract stance: jump in, commit, see what happens.

Ra emphasised that the abstract approach to duality is different from the logical approach. The logical mind tries to find a pattern that works; the abstract mind tries to find a partner who works. The 29 in the abstract circuit is the gate of jumping in — the gate that says, "Yes, I'll commit; I'll find out if this is the one by being inside of it." The carrier of Contagion 3 is the embodiment of this approach.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is personal destiny. For Contagion 3, the right-angle expression is the carrier's own committed bonded life. They are not here to teach others how to commit; they are here to commit themselves — to say their own yeses, to live their own commitments, to discover firsthand what bonded experience yields.

Ra emphasised that the abstract circuit learns by experiencing what doesn't work as much as by experiencing what does. The Contagion 3 carrier will commit to relationships that fail, projects that collapse, experiences that disappoint. This is not the design malfunctioning; this is the design working as Ra described it. The carrier learns by going through. The bonded experiences that succeed are valuable; the bonded experiences that fail are equally valuable, because they teach the carrier (and through the carrier, the wider human archive) what experience this configuration produces.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to avoid committing because they fear the failures. They sit on the edges of relationships, never quite saying yes, never quite saying no. The not-self Contagion 3 collects unfinished bonds and accumulates regrets.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

This Right Angle Cross appears in six profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6. Ra discussed the 1/3 Contagion 3 as the one who finds out right away that commitment is full of broken promises. The 2/4 Contagion 3 is named as deeply seductive — the carrier whose closed window is the irresistible draw. The 3/5 Contagion 3 carries the heaviest burden of being blamed for the bonds that fail. The 3/6 in this quarter is described by Ra as one of the more stable variants because of the deep accommodation to the unworkability of duality itself.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to let the sacral select what to commit to. The carrier of Contagion 3 has a sacral authority (when defined sacrally) that knows in the moment which yes is correct and which yes is not. The conscious mind cannot make this decision well; the body can.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] commits to bonds the body did not respond to. They marry people the body did not want; they enter projects the body did not consent to; they say yes with the mind while the body says no underneath. The result is the catalogue of failed commitments the abstract not-self drags through life. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the body's yes be the yes and the body's no be the no.

The deepest practice for this cross is honouring the abstract law that what matters is the experience, not the outcome. The carrier of Contagion 3 will go through many bonded experiences in a lifetime. Some will last; most will not. The work is not to find the bond that lasts forever; the work is to enter each bond correctly, live each bond fully, and let it end correctly when it ends. The accumulated wisdom of all those bonds becomes the contagion the carrier passes on — by being the person who has committed and survived, who has loved and lost, who has been drafted and finished the tour.
