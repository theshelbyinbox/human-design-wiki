# Left Angle Cross of Laws 1

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 1 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Wishes 1 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Laws cross in Quarter 1. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 3]] (Ordering / Difficulty at the Beginning) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In the Quarter of Initiation, where purpose moves through Mind, the Left Angle Laws 1 is the cross of the transpersonal mutative ordering — the carrier whose 3/60 format energy is karmically routed toward specific others whose mental lives need exactly this kind of mutation.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 3]] (Ordering / Difficulty at the Beginning), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 50]] (The Cauldron / Values), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 56]] or [[Gate 60]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 3 / 50
- Design Axis: 56 / 60
- Quarter: Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone)
- Realm Purpose: Mind
- Mystical Theme: The Witness Returns
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 3 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 3]] in the Left Angle is the mutative ordering-energy turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Victimization — the projection field of the leader through confusion, who in the not-self becomes the target of blame, and in the higher expression has the courage to stand by their convictions through chaos. At line 6 the gate carries Surrender — the line of accepting limitation, the elder who has learned that not every beginning will find its order in this life.

The name "Wishes" given to the Left Angle Laws 1 signals the transpersonal nature of the work: the carrier brings mutative ordering to others' wishes — the new shapes that other people's longings are trying to take. Ra was clear that the 3/60 format is the deepest mutation channel in the BodyGraph, and in the Left Angle, this mutation is offered transpersonally.

### Gate 50 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 50]] at line 5 is Consistency — the expected lawgiver, projected onto as the one who will faithfully transmit values. At line 6 it is the Leader — the mature custodian who holds the values lightly and lives them deeply, so others adopt them by attraction rather than enforcement. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's transpersonal mutation-work is rooted in values offered to the broader community.

### Gate 56 (Design)

[[Gate 56]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the body's storytelling capacity into the cross. Line 2 is Connection — natural rapport with the audience. Line 3 is Distraction — trial-and-error in finding which stories land. Together with the 3 Sun, this design gate gives the Left Angle Laws 1 the practical means to communicate the mutative orderings to the others their karma routes them toward.

### Gate 60 (Design)

[[Gate 60]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the adrenal pulse of mutation into the body. Line 2 is Decisiveness — sharp action when the pulse turns on. Line 3 is Conservatism — the experimental approach to limitation, learning through bumping into edges. The 60 design ensures the body's pulse-mechanism operates correctly so the carrier's mutations can land in the lives of others.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation channels purpose through Mind. The Laws cross in this quarter is the cross of mental ordering — the laws of how the mind itself can be lawful. The Left Angle variant routes this ordering-work into transpersonal karma: the carrier's mutations are offered to specific others whose mental lives need exactly this reorganization.

The mystical theme of Quarter 1 is The Witness Returns. For the Left Angle Laws 1 carrier, the Witness returns not to a private life but to the lives of others — the carrier becomes the witness for someone else's mutation, the friend or elder whose presence helps another person see the new ordering their own life has been trying to bring through.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Left Angle Laws 1 carrier is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges in the field of mutative ordering, and have their own life shaped by those encounters in turn.

Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned analytically. The karmic-encounters arrive in their own timing, and only [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] can recognize the right ones as they appear.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Laws 1 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in foundational study — the heretic-investigator lawkeeper. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings the role-model life lived between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings experimental life-arcs that distill into role-model wisdom.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Laws 1 life unfolds through encounters in which the carrier's mutative ordering helps someone else's wishes take their proper shape. A friend who has been trying to start a new chapter and cannot find the order to make it work meets the carrier and, through some combination of presence and timing, finds the ordering principle that allows the chapter to begin. A community wrestling with which values to preserve as it changes meets the carrier and, through a quiet word, recognizes the value that had been there all along.

This work happens through the pulse. The 3/60 format does not run continuously — there are mutation-on phases and quiet off-phases. The carrier learns to honor the pulse. During the on-phases, the karmic-encounters tend to arrive and the mutative ordering lands cleanly. During the off-phases, the work is to rest, accept, and let the pulse come back when it is ready.

The 5th line projection that the carrier carries (when the Sun is in line 5) is intense. Others will project onto the Left Angle Laws 1 carrier the role of the one who must lead them through confusion, who must produce the new ordering on demand. Without [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]], the carrier becomes conscripted into performance — and the shadow of the 3 line 5, Victimization, takes over. The carrier becomes the target of blame for failures that were not theirs.

The 50 Earth keeps the work rooted in values. The mutations the carrier brings to others' lives must be in service of values worth preserving. Mutation severed from values becomes destruction; mutation tethered to splenic value-awareness becomes the kind of help that actually helps.

The 56 design gives the carrier the storytelling means to communicate. The 60 design supplies the body's pulse-mechanism. Together they form the practical scaffolding for the transpersonal work.

The deeper teaching is that all laws will be broken, including the laws the Left Angle Laws 1 carrier helps others see. The carrier's contribution is the mutation that fits this moment in this life of this specific other — not a permanent ordering. Ra was clear that the mature wisdom of the Laws cross is the recognition that the laws are mutable, and the Left Angle carrier learns this through the encounters.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Laws 1 person is often experienced as the catalyst — their presence helps others' stuck mutations finally complete. Their own growth comes through the same encounters: every life they touch, in turn, mutates them. The witness returns by being present at someone else's beginning.
