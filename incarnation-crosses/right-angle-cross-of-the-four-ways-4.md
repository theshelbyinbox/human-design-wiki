# Right Angle Cross of the Four Ways 4

The Right Angle Cross of the Four Ways 4 is the closing cross of the Quarter of Mutation and, in a sense, the closing cross of the entire wheel. The Personality Sun sits in Gate 19, the gate of Approach, and the carrier lives a life shaped around the sensitive tribal need that closes the mutation cycle and seeds the next round of initiation. Where the Right Angle Four Ways 1 opens with the rationalising return at the end of the mental quarter, the Four Ways 4 closes with the approach at the end of the mutation quarter, ready for the new cycle to begin.

Ra Uru Hu placed Gate 19 in the Root Center as a tribal pressure gate — the need to come close, to draw near, to ask the other for what cannot be gotten alone. The Four Ways 4 carrier lives that approach as a personal destiny across four 18.6-year nodal cycles. Each cycle is a different way of approaching, and through the four ways the carrier becomes the body in which the wheel completes its turn and prepares to begin again.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 19 — Approach
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 33 — Retreat
- **Design Sun:** Gate 44 — Coming to Meet
- **Design Earth:** Gate 24 — Rationalizing / Return
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 19–33 is the consciousness: the tribal need to approach partnered with the Throat gate of retreat. The Design axis 44–24 is the body's vehicle: the splenic recognition of pattern partnered with the mental return.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 19 (Personality Sun) — Approach

The conscious purpose. Gate 19 is the Root pressure of tribal approach — the need to come close, to ask, to receive. As the lead of this cross it gives the carrier a life-long instruction to approach the other for what cannot be gotten alone. The 19 is the sensitive part of the tribe, the gate that knows the body's need for resources, shelter, food, and bonded relationship.

### Gate 33 (Personality Earth) — Retreat

The ground beneath the approach. Gate 33 is the disciplined withdrawal that lets the approach be processed. As the Earth of this cross it grounds the 19 in the knowledge that approach without retreat exhausts the body. The 33 is what makes the carrier's approaches sustainable. The carrier approaches, retreats to digest, and approaches again under a new condition.

### Gate 44 (Design Sun) — Coming to Meet

The unconscious splenic engine. Gate 44 is the splenic pattern-recognition that knows whether the present encounter is a re-encounter. Beneath the conscious approach, the body has already been recognising who is being approached. The 44 is what makes the carrier's approach intelligent — they are drawn toward the encounters their splenic memory has flagged as relevant.

### Gate 24 (Design Earth) — Rationalizing

The unconscious mental ground. Gate 24 is the mental gate of return. The body of this carrier is built to process the approach by returning to it mentally — going over the encounter, the asking, the receiving, again and again until insight arrives. The 24 grounds the approach in a mind that learns from what was approached.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to break the patterns of what was and to seed the patterns of what will be. Gate 19 closes this quarter at the level of tribal need. The Four Ways 4 carrier contributes to the mutation quarter by being the body through which the mutated cycle closes itself — the sensitive approach that reaches toward the people who must be involved in the next round, and through their inclusion, seeds the initiation that opens the next quarter of mind.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is a personal destiny. The Four Ways 4 carrier lives their own life across four 18.6-year nodal cycles. In each cycle the carrier approaches a different set of people, asks for a different set of resources, builds a different set of bonds. The cycles do not connect smoothly. Each approach belongs to its own season. The carrier may, looking back, find that the four ways were the four lives.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses to approach, who treats tribal need as weakness, or who clings to the people of one nodal cycle when the next cycle requires a new approach. The correct expression honours the sensitivity, lets the body approach when the body says approach, retreats when retreat is correct, and trusts that the four ways are the structure of a life of progressive bonded development.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle is carried by the six classical right-angle profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6.

- **1/3** the investigator-approacher whose approaches are tested through trial and error
- **1/4** the investigator-approacher whose approaches are mediated by a stable friendship network
- **2/4** the natural hermit pulled into approach through known relationships
- **2/5** the called hermit-approacher projected onto by the collective as the one who must come close
- **3/5** the trial-and-error approacher whose practical wisdom emerges from a lifetime of asking
- **3/6** the role-model approacher who matures across the four cycles into the elder whose approaches are the community's instruction

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only reliable navigation system. The Four Ways 4 carrier is sensitive — Ra noted that Gate 19 produces some of the most sensitive carriers in the wheel — and the sensitivity is the gift, not the problem. The work is to honour the body's need to approach, to refuse the cultural shame around asking, and to let the four nodal cycles deliver the four bodies of bonded learning the cross is built for.

The signature of the cross lived well is a life of correct approaches — a carrier who, by the end of the fourth cycle, has built four different bodies of relationship and seeded the wheel for its next turn. The not-self signature is the loneliness of the carrier who refused the approach, who stayed independent past the point of body-need, or who approached people the splenic 44 had flagged as wrong patterns.

When the carrier honours the design, they become the body through which the wheel completes its mutation and the next initiation becomes possible. The four ways are the four phases of a life that closes the cycle and opens the door for what comes next.
