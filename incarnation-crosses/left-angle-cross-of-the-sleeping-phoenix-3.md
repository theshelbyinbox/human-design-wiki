# Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 3

The Left Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 3 is the transpersonal expression of the Sleeping Phoenix gate set in the Quarter of Duality. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Left Angle Cross of Spirit 2. Where the Quarter 1 Spirit was the marketing of mutating creative spirit, the Quarter 3 Spirit is the sexual heresy that says intimate excitement matters more than tribal continuity.

The one-line theme: the heresy of sexual spirit. The carrier insists that intimate excitement, not reproduction, is what makes bonded life worth living — and they teach this heresy through their visible intimate life.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Sun:** Gate 8 — Holding Together
- **Design Earth:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 2 variant keeps the Personality axis 59–55 of the Right Angle Sleeping Phoenix 3 but rotates the Design axis from the individual pair (34–20) to the abstract pair (8–14). The body is no longer the pure individual sacral power; it is the throat voice of contribution grounded in the resource of the 14. The Spirit 2 carrier is therefore more vocal, more verbal, more publicly broadcasting than the Right Angle Sleeping Phoenix 3 — they have the 8 in the design to speak.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 59 (Personality Sun) — Dispersion

The genetic role gate of intimate union in the conscious position. In the Left Angle, the carrier's consciousness is shaped by the impulse to disperse into others — sexually, romantically, intimately — and the dispersion is transpersonal. Their intimate life is the teaching.

### Gate 55 (Personality Earth) — Spirit / Abundance

The moody emotional pulse grounds the consciousness. The 55 is what makes Spirit 2 a heresy — the carrier insists that without the mutating spirit present in the intimate field, the bond is hollow. The tribal mind says "bond and reproduce regardless"; the 59.5 Spirit 2 carrier says "no, the spirit has to be there."

### Gate 8 (Design Sun) — Holding Together

The unconscious throat voice of unique contribution. The body is here to speak the heresy. The 8 ensures the carrier's intimate teaching has a voice — that they don't just live it, they say it. They write the books, give the talks, model the relationships visibly.

### Gate 14 (Design Earth) — Power Skills

The sacral fuel. The 14 ensures the body has sustained energy for the lifetime broadcast of the heresy. The carrier of Spirit 2 has the resources to keep teaching across years and decades.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. Within this quarter, Spirit 2 is the heretical voice insisting that bonded life requires sexual and intimate spirit to be worth living. The tribe says: "Marry, have children, stay together." The Left Angle Spirit 2 carrier says: "Yes, and also — the sex has to be good. The intimacy has to be present. The spirit has to be in the bed."

Ra was specific that this configuration sells what he called the 59.5 heresy — the proposition that intimate excitement is sufficient justification for a bond. The collective tribal mind finds this threatening because it destabilises arranged marriages, contractual partnerships, and family alliances. But the Left Angle Spirit 2 carrier is here to introduce the destabilisation. Without them, bonded life would harden into pure tribal contract.

Ra also noted that this is a dying heresy. The chemistry of the 59 is mutating; sexual potency is declining across the species. The Spirit 2 carrier is one of the last public broadcasters of a heresy that may not be available to future humanity in the same form.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is for others. For Spirit 2, the transpersonal pull is the teaching of intimate spirit through visible intimate life. The carrier's lovers, partners, and audiences are the field of the teaching.

Ra emphasised that this Left Angle is provocative — the carrier is here to upset the apple cart. They are not here to be a quiet contributor; they are here to make visible the heresy that the spirit of the 59 matters more than the duty of the bond. This produces public scandal as often as it produces public art.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who lives the heresy cynically — who uses the design to extract sexual or romantic pleasure without delivering the teaching. The not-self Spirit 2 becomes the predator, the seducer who never bonds, the spiritual fraud. The correct expression is honest heresy: the carrier lives the intimate life publicly, the spirit is real or visibly absent, and the teaching is the witness of both.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Spirit 2 manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra described the 5/1 Spirit 2 as the Casanova heretic — the carrier whose sexual power is the medium of the teaching. The 5/2 Spirit 2 holds the heresy privately. The 6/2 Spirit 2, when awake, embodies the love of intimacy rather than the love of any particular partner — the awake form of the heresy is the recognition that intimacy itself is the spirit, not the lover. The 6/3 closes the Left Angle and is the bridge to the next Right Angle.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept the transpersonal pull of the intimate field. The carrier of Spirit 2 cannot privatise the intimate life — the design is built to broadcast.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] either uses the design to consume others or collapses into hiding the design. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the intimate life be the teaching, with honesty about both when spirit is present and when it is absent. The lifelong work is to refine the heresy — to teach more clearly, to broadcast more cleanly, to leave behind a body of work that licences other humans to demand spirit in their bonds.

The deepest practice for this cross is honouring the 55's fickleness. The Spirit 2 carrier does not get to manufacture intimate spirit on command. When the wave is full, the bonded life is luminous; when the wave is empty, the bonded life is dust. The carrier learns to honour both — to bring the luminosity when it is available and to wait when it is not, refusing to perform spirit that is not there. The intimate partners who can hold the cycles become the long-term collaborators of the teaching.
