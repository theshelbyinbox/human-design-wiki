# Left Angle Cross of Informing 1

The Left Angle Cross of Informing 1 is the transpersonal extension of the Rulership 1 gate set in the Quarter of Initiation. Where the Right Angle variant centers on the personal work of becoming a ruler and the Juxtaposition fixes the carrier in social grace, this Left Angle expression turns the entire apparatus outward. The conscious Sun in Gate 12 makes articulation the primary instrument, and the work shifts from being a ruler to informing the collective about what rulership is, how it operates, and where it breaks down.

Ra called Informing the basic strategy of the adult Manifestor, and that framing is key to the cross. Informing means giving notice. It does not mean controlling, manipulating, or selling. It means delivering information that the receiver needs in order to act with awareness, and then letting the receiver respond as they will. This cross is built to inform on rulership — to teach, to leak, to articulate, to make public what would otherwise remain hidden.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 12 (Standstill / Caution)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 11 (Peace / Ideas)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 (Innocence / The Spirit of the Self)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 (Pushing Upward / The Determination of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone, Purpose: Mind)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

Note that the Left Angle gate set differs significantly from the Right Angle Rulership gate set. The four Right Angle Rulership gates (22, 47, 26, 45) belong to the Personality and Design axes of the Right Angle and Juxtaposition Rulership crosses. The Left Angle Informing 1 cross retains the Personality axis of 12/11 (which was the Design axis of the Juxtaposition) and brings in the Vessel of Love axis 25/46 as the Design. This is the meeting of articulation with the universal love of the body and the spirit of the self — a configuration that gives Informing its transpersonal authority.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 12 (Personality Sun) — Standstill / Caution

[[Gate 12]] is the Throat voice of the individual circuit and the gate of articulation. As the lead of this cross, Gate 12 makes the Throat the primary instrument. The being is here to speak, to articulate, to put into language what others cannot yet name. The line 4 of Gate 12 — the prophet — is one of the most powerful expressions of this gate, and Informing 1 carriers often find themselves in roles where their words carry mutative weight regardless of their conscious intention.

### Gate 11 (Personality Earth) — Peace / Ideas

[[Gate 11]] grounds the conscious mind in the conceptual room where ideas accumulate. As Earth on this cross, Gate 11 supplies the steady stream of conceptual content that the 12 will eventually articulate. The 11.4 — the teacher, the guru, the sage — pairs with the 12.4 to produce one of the most teaching-oriented configurations on the wheel.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence / The Spirit of the Self

[[Gate 25]] is the G Center gate of universal love, the innocence that loves what it loves without calculation. As the unconscious driver of this cross, Gate 25 grounds the informing in the spirit of the self. The being is not informing in order to gain advantage, score points, or destroy enemies; the informing arises from the innocent recognition that people have a right to know what is happening.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self

[[Gate 46]] is the G Center gate of the love of the body, the embodiment that knows the body is the vehicle of the journey. As Earth, Gate 46 grounds the informing in the physical body itself — the carrier is not a disembodied voice but a body that has lived what it informs about.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the realm where Mind establishes the foundations of human experience. Crosses in this quarter are initiating, originating, and codifying. For the Informing 1 carrier, the work is to put into language what the rulership crosses have lived. The Right Angle Rulership beings are doing the actual rulership; the Informing beings are observing, articulating, and making the lessons available to the collective.

This is why Informing is the Left Angle expression of the Rulership name. The Left Angle is transpersonal — it operates on others and through others rather than for the self — and the Rulership theme at the transpersonal level cannot remain absolute. The Left Angle carrier knows things that the rulers do not want known, and the informing is the act of releasing that knowledge into the collective field.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle Crosses carry Transpersonal Karma. The work is not personal destiny but karmic exchange with the collective. The Informing 1 being is here not to be a ruler but to inform on rulership — to teach, to leak, to articulate, to make public what would otherwise remain hidden. The karma flows both directions: the carrier brings information to the collective, and the collective brings consequences back to the carrier.

Ra was explicit that informing includes leaking, whistleblowing, and what would today be called investigative journalism. The Left Angle Informing 1 being is structurally built to release information that the powerful would prefer to suppress. This carries cost. The 5/1 Manifestor on this cross will be screaming truth from every available platform, and the collective response is rarely friendly.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left Angle Crosses appear in the five Left Angle profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Each produces a distinct flavor of the informing.

The 4/6 informs through their network of friends and community. The informing is intimate, personal, and reaches a defined circle of influence. The 5/1 is the classic informer — the Manifestor or Projector who broadcasts foundationally researched information to the universal field. The 5/1 pays a heavy reputational price for this work, but the practical depth they bring is unmatched. The 5/2 knows things and keeps them mostly to themselves. They will inform only when self-motivated, and their reluctance to speak is often what makes their occasional pronouncements so powerful. The 6/2 is the experiential sage who speaks without agenda. They have lived through the trial-and-error of the first Saturn return, climbed onto the roof, and now speak from a place of detached wisdom. The 6/3 carries the bridge between the truth that must be told and the chaos that telling it will produce. They oscillate between speaking out and pulling back.

## Living This Cross

For the Informing 1 being, the work is to honor the role of informer without becoming the snitch, the tattletale, or the bitter critic. The informing must arise from the innocent love of Gate 25 — the genuine sense that the receiver has a right to know — rather than from grievance, revenge, or the need to be right.

The cross requires courage. Informers are rarely loved by those they inform on, and they are not always loved by those they inform either. The 5/1 child who calls out unfair behavior in the classroom is labeled a snitch and pays for it for years. The 5/1 adult who leaks corporate or governmental wrongdoing pays in legal fees, social ostracism, and sometimes physical danger. Yet the cross is structurally committed to the work. Ra was clear that the Informing being must inform; not informing is the not-self path that leads to internal collapse.

The other side of the cross is the teaching function. Informing is not only about exposure; it is about making the new available to the collective. The Channel 12/22 carries mutation from the individual to the stranger, and the Informing 1 being is one of the primary conduits for this transmission. When something new arrives in the cultural field — a new idea, a new aesthetic, a new way of seeing — it often arrives through carriers of this cross.

The medicine for the cross is to wait for the moment when the informing is correct. Ra emphasized that the informing must be timed: too early and the collective rejects it; too late and the moment has passed. The Strategy and Inner Authority of the individual chart determines when the moment is right, and the carrier who honors their authority finds that the informing happens at the right time without force.
