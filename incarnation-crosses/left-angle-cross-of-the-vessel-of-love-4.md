# Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 4

The Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 4 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Prevention 2. The four Vessel of Love gates — 10, 15, 25, 46 — rotate into a Left Angle configuration in the Quarter of Mutation, producing a transpersonal life whose karmic work is preventive behavior. The Prevention 2 carrier is here to prevent — by the example of their own conduct — the mutational dead-ends that the people around them would otherwise walk into. Where Prevention 1 in Quarter 2 prevents through aura and rhythm, Prevention 2 in Quarter 4 prevents through behavior. The carrier's daily conduct is the medicine.

This is the second Prevention cross in Ra's teaching. The first, in Quarter 2, prevents rhythmic spoilage in civilization. The second, in Quarter 4, prevents behavioral dead-ends in the mutation of culture. Both are transpersonal expressions of universal love, but the Q4 variation is grounded in conduct rather than aura.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 10 — Treading / Behavior of the Self
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 15 — Modesty
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 — Innocence
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 — Pushing Upward
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

Same four gates as the Right Angle Vessel of Love 4 but lived through a transpersonal angle. The carrier's life will not make sense as a personal story. It makes sense as the behaviors that did not get adopted because the carrier was demonstrating an alternative.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 10 (Personality Sun) — Treading

Behavior of the self as the conscious medium of prevention. For the Prevention 2 carrier, the 10 is the way they walk through their own life — and the way they walk is the warning that other people register. The carrier is not here to instruct. They are here to behave correctly, in public, where others can see, and to let the conduct be the prevention.

### Gate 15 (Personality Earth) — Modesty

Love of humanity as the conscious ground. The 15's wide aura ensures that the prevention reaches diverse people. The Prevention 2 carrier does not prevent only the people who look like them. The aura is wide enough to catch the difference of others, and the behavior demonstrates self-love in a way that crosses cultural lines.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence

Universal love beneath the conscious work. The 25 ensures that the prevention is delivered without judgment, without superiority, without the moralising tone that turns example-setting into condemnation. The carrier prevents because they love, not because they are appalled.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward

The body as the unconscious vehicle. The 46's serendipity places the carrier in front of the people whose mutational trajectories are about to misfire. The body delivers the carrier to the right rooms, the right relationships, the right moments, where their conduct can do its preventive work.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to change what has been built. The Prevention 2 carrier contributes to that mutative work by interrupting the dead-end mutations — the cultural experiments that would have led nowhere — through the simple force of their own correct conduct. The carrier's life is a demonstration that another way is possible, and the demonstration shifts the trajectories of the people who notice.

Ra was clear that Quarter 4 is where the wheel completes itself in mutation. The Prevention 2 carrier is here to prevent the mutations that would have spoiled the close of the wheel — the cultural experiments that look attractive but lead to fragmentation. The work is preventive, transpersonal, and lived through ordinary daily behavior.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The life belongs to others. The work is publicising — making available to the collective what the Right Angles have lived through. The Prevention 2 publicises correct conduct: the carrier walks through the cultural moment and lets the example of their own treading be the preventive demonstration.

The not-self distortion of this cross is the carrier who tries to prevent verbally — who lectures, instructs, posts, criticises. The mind decides that the prevention should be delivered in language. The design says the prevention is delivered through conduct. When the carrier speaks too much, the prevention fails. When the carrier walks the walk, the prevention succeeds without ever being named.

Ra called Left Angles karmic lives. The Prevention 2 carrier will meet, again and again, the same mutational dead-ends — the same cultural traps that the people around them are about to step into — and the work is to be in front of those dead-ends, walking the correct path, available to be observed. The mutation of the wider culture happens through the cumulative behavior of carriers like this. Few of them are famous. Many of them never know what they prevented.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle of this cross is carried by five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2 and 6/3.

- **4/6** is the externalising friend whose preventive behavior flows through their friendship network — friends and the friends of friends learn from the carrier's example
- **5/1** is the heretic with foundation, the universalising behavioral example whose conduct has the authority of 1st line study behind it
- **5/2** is the heretic hermit, projected upon as a savior of behavior, most effective when allowed to remain quiet and let the conduct speak
- **6/2** is the role model emerging from the rooftop — the wise elder whose behavior is recognised by their community as the example to follow
- **6/3** is the role model who moved through trial and error and is now the visible demonstration that another way of being is possible

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are non-negotiable. A Left Angle carrier who lives by mind quickly becomes a missionary for correct conduct — the person whose lectures and posts irritate everyone — when the design is to be silent and let the body live the example. The work is to be in one's own body, walking correctly, in public, where others can see, and to let the conduct be the message.

The signature of this cross lived well is people whose lives shifted because they noticed the carrier — sometimes without knowing they noticed. The carrier becomes the unconscious reference point for the people around them, the example they did not know they were watching.

The not-self signature is the carrier who tries to manage everyone, becomes preachy or sanctimonious, and exhausts themselves running ahead of every possible cultural dead-end. The correction is to come back to the body, walk one's own path, and trust that the example is enough.

When the carrier honours this cross, they become what Ra implied was one of the great quiet preventers of the wheel — the one whose conduct shifts the trajectory of others without ever naming the shift. This is a thankless cross, lived correctly. The carrier rarely gets credit. The reward is the quiet knowledge that the mutation of culture is moving through their feet.

This is the cross of the behavioral example, the integrated walker, the body whose conduct is medicine. Lived correctly, it is a quietly public life. Lived incorrectly, it is an exhausting performance of how-to. The difference is whether the carrier remains in their own treading or tries to instruct everyone else's.
