# Right Angle Cross of Planning 4

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 4 places [[Gate 9]] — Focus, The Taming Power of the Small — in the Personality Sun position and [[Gate 16]] — Enthusiasm — in the Personality Earth. In this fourth and final quarterly rotation of the Cross of Planning, focus itself leads. The plan is no longer the bargain (Planning 1), no longer the experiment (Planning 2), no longer the lone deliverer (Planning 3) — it is the granular concentration that survives the mutation, the attention to detail that endures while everything else is transformed. The Design Sun in [[Gate 40]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 37]] hold the tribal will and bargain quietly underneath. This is Planning made into a mutative skill: focus as the seed that carries the bargain through the next cycle.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 9 — The Taming Power of the Small (Focus)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Design Sun:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose: Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — personal destiny
- **Axis A (Personality):** 9 ↔ 16
- **Axis B (Design):** 37 ↔ 40

## The Four Gates

### Gate 9 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral gate of focus, the engineering capacity for sustained attention on small detail. As the lead gate of the Cross of Planning 4, Gate 9 makes concentration itself the mutative power. In a quarter governed by transformation, what survives is not the grand plan but the granular attention that gives the plan something to land on. Gate 9 is the patient eye that knits one, purls two — and changes the world by it.

### Gate 16 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat gate of enthusiasm, the willingness to try. Beneath Gate 9, Gate 16 supplies the artistic motion of repetition. The focus has something to focus on because Gate 16 keeps trying. Together, the [[Channel 9/16]] of Identification is the personality axis: the channel of talent.

### Gate 40 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 40]] is the will-energy of deliverance, the lone provider's stomach. Beneath the surface, Gate 40 supplies the will that keeps the focus fed. Without Gate 40, Gate 9 would have nothing to draw on; the focus would die at the first sustained demand.

### Gate 37 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain, the gate of friendship. As Design Earth, Gate 37 grounds the cross in the community the focused work serves. The 37/40 design axis is the [[Channel 37/40]] of Community — the tribal bargain that survives, quietly, beneath the mutative focus.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the Quarter of Transformation, governed by the Realm of Mutation. Its purpose is the breakthrough that ends one cycle and seeds the next. The Cross of Planning 4, sitting in this quarter, is the survival skill of the Planning era. As the 400-year cycle of the Cross of Planning closes toward 2027, the Cross of Planning 4 person is the one who carries the practical skill — the trained attention, the disciplined focus — through the mutation. They are not nostalgic for the bargain; they are the discipline that lets the next cycle find a foothold.

## The Angle Expression

The Right Angle is personal destiny: the karma of the self for the self. A Right Angle Cross of Planning 4 person is not here to teach the world how to focus; they are here to live focus from the inside, to feel what concentration costs and what it returns. The cross is lived as one's own life, not as a mission delivered to others.

The 1/3 of this cross dives into the focus, then collides with the parts that won't yield — pruning the practice through failure. The 2/4 is the natural focuser called out by friends to show what they did not know they could see. The 2/5 carries the projection of being the expert in the detail, expected to deliver clarity on demand. The 3/5 throws themselves at the focus, fails forward through experiments, and projects their hard-won precision outward. The 3/6 spends the first thirty years deep inside the granular work, then climbs onto the roof to become the elder who knows what mattered.

## Living This Cross

For the Right Angle Cross of Planning 4, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are how the focus stays honest. Gate 9 wants to focus — but the body must tell it what to focus on. Mind-led focus in this cross becomes the obsessive detail loop: hours spent perfecting something the body never asked to perfect. The shadow of the cross is the person who has impressive concentration aimed at the wrong target, who can grind for years on something that was never theirs to do.

The healthiest expression is focus that emerges from the body's correct response. When the [[Sacral]] has answered, the focus is alive — and a Cross of Planning 4 person in this state is unusually productive, because Gate 9 is one of the most powerful endurance gates in the BodyGraph. Combined with Gate 16's enthusiasm, this is the cross that produces the master craftsperson, the deep technician, the long-arc scholar.

The deeper invitation of the Quarter of Mutation is to let the bargain dissolve. The Cross of Planning 4 person does not need to save the plan. They need to live their own focus correctly, and the focus itself becomes the seed. Ra noted that the closing of the Planning cycle is not a failure but a passage — the cycle has done its work, and the new cycle of the Sleeping Phoenix (gates 20, 34, 55, 59) is rising. The Cross of Planning 4 person, anchored in the mutation quarter, is positioned to carry the disciplined attention forward while the rest of the planning architecture changes.

The gift, lived in alignment, is the granular work that outlasts the era. Cross of Planning 4 people are the engineers whose attention to small things builds what the next civilization will rest on. They are not the planners of a new plan; they are the focus that will be there, in the body, when the next plan needs concentration to become real.
