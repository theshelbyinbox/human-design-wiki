# Right Angle Cross of Contagion 2

The Right Angle Cross of Contagion 2 carries the Personality Sun in Gate 8 — Holding Together — anchored in the Quarter of Civilization. This is the quarter whose purpose is to put form into the world, to civilise the raw experience that the Quarter of Initiation gathered. Carriers of Contagion 2 are placed at the throat-gate of the individual circuit, charged with making contagion concrete: not merely spreading desire, but spreading what gets built.

The one-line theme: the contribution of the unique individual to the collective — contagion through self-expression. The carrier is here to make a thing irresistible, to ensure that what they put into form is something other people want to have.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure (Power Skills)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 30 — The Clinging Fire (Recognition of Feelings)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 29 — The Abysmal (Saying Yes)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 8–14 is the consciousness axis: the throat voice of contribution fuelled by the sacral power of resource. The Design axis 30–29 is the body's underlying engine: the fire of desire grounded in the sacral yes. Where the Quarter 1 Contagion led with the fire and the commitment, Quarter 2 Contagion leads with the voice and the power. The carrier knows what they are here to contribute and has the energetic resources to deliver it across a lifetime.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 8 (Personality Sun) — Holding Together

Gate 8 is the throat voice of the individual circuit — the voice that contributes from self to self. Ra Uru Hu described it as one of the eleven voices of the throat, the one that says "I know I can contribute." In the Personality Sun, the carrier's conscious life is shaped by the impulse to make a contribution that others can gather around. The 8 does not promote itself; it promotes what it values, and that promotion is contagious to those who can hear the unique voice underneath.

### Gate 14 (Personality Earth) — Power Skills

Gate 14 is the sacral fuel that retains and channels power. Ra called the 14 the gate of working hard, but in the Right Angle Contagion 2 it is also the gate of skilled creative work — the place where the carrier's resources accumulate over a lifetime of doing the work. The Personality Earth grounds the throat voice in the body's sustained productive capacity.

### Gate 30 (Design Sun) — The Clinging Fire

The unconscious engine is the fire of desire. Beneath the voice and the power, the body burns for experience. The carrier's contagion is fuelled by the recognition of feelings — the longing for what could be — which gives the throat voice its emotional charge.

### Gate 29 (Design Earth) — The Abysmal

The fuel beneath the fuel. Gate 29 is the sacral yes to experience. It grounds the body in the response that allows the carrier to commit to the work that makes the contribution possible. Without the 29, the 8's contribution would never get built; with the 29, the body says yes to the years of sustained work that the 14 requires.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. Its purpose is to put what the mind initiated into concrete shape — to build the houses, the institutions, the products, the trends, the customs that collective humanity inhabits. Contagion 2 contributes to this civilisation by making the new form irresistible. Ra used the example of the hula hoop, of denim jeans, of the household computer — items that emerged out of nowhere and quickly became indispensable. The contagion of form is what civilisation runs on.

The carrier of Contagion 2 is placed in this quarter to ensure that what gets built spreads. Ra said this is the cross of contagion that turns "I have an idea" into "everyone has the thing." The 8 is the voice that says "look at this," the 14 is the power that produces it, the 30 is the fire that wants it, the 29 is the commitment that builds it. The whole cross is a contagion engine for form.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is personal destiny. For Contagion 2, the right-angle expression is the carrier's own life of contribution. They are not here to manage the contagion they cause; they are here to live the life of contribution and let the spread happen. Ra emphasised that beings on this cross cannot help but affect others. The carrier's correct expression is to make their contribution as cleanly as possible and trust the contagion to carry it where it needs to go.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to engineer the spread — who optimises for virality, who manipulates the broadcast, who treats their contribution as a product to be marketed. Ra noted that this not-self Contagion produces some of the most harmful spreaders in the world: the people who get others addicted to junk, who turn whole populations toward harmful trends, who use the contagion power without the underlying contribution.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

This Right Angle Cross appears in six profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6. The 1/3 Contagion 2 risks turning the wrong thing contagious because the third line in the unconscious has not finished its trial-and-error. The 2/4 Contagion 2 is described by Ra as a tiger when called — the sinner who becomes the saint, whose conversion becomes irresistible to the community around them. The 3/5 Contagion 2 carries the heaviest burden of blame; the 3/6 Contagion 2 (described by Ra as deeply disadvantaged in not-self) requires especially attentive parenting to come to its gifts.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to make the contribution the body responds to making. The carrier of Contagion 2 has a specific contribution to make in a specific lifetime — not the contributions other carriers are making, not the contributions the mind wants to make, but the one the body responds to. Living from [[Strategy]] is the only way to find it.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] becomes a salesperson for whatever the conditioning installed. The 1/3 not-self Contagion 2 spreads neo-Nazism if raised in a neo-Nazi family; the 2/4 not-self spreads whatever the community values without examining whether the community is healthy; the 3/5 not-self spreads the contagion until it fails publicly and then spreads the next one. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the body select what gets contributed and what does not.

The deepest practice for this cross is recognising that the contribution is not negotiable. The carrier did not choose what their unique voice would say; they only get to choose whether to say it correctly. The lifelong work is to refine the delivery — to live correctly enough that the voice carries its true cargo and the contagion serves the contribution rather than poisoning it.
