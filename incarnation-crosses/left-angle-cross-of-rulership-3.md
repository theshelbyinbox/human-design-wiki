# Left Angle Cross of Informing 2

The Left Angle Cross of Informing 2 is the transpersonal extension of the Rulership 3 gate set in the Quarter of Duality. The Personality Sun in Gate 12 makes articulation the conscious instrument, but with the Design axis rotated to Gates 25 and 46 (the Vessel of Love axis), the entire cross is reorganized around the saintly informing of those caught in the oppression of bonded life.

Ra named the 47.5 — the line at the heart of this cross — The Saint, and noted that this is one of the rare lines with only an exaltation and no detriment. Every planet brings out the saintly quality. The Informing 2 being is structurally built to offer aid and succor to the oppressed while maintaining harmonic relationships with the oppressors, without hypocrisy. This is one of the most demanding karmic configurations on the wheel.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 12 (Standstill / Caution)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 11 (Peace / Ideas)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 (Innocence / The Spirit of the Self)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 (Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose: Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Personality axis is the individual articulation channel 12/11. The Design axis is the Vessel of Love axis 25/46. This is the meeting of individual mutation with universal love and embodied determination. The carrier informs through the body, with the spirit of the self, on what oppression and grace truly are inside bonded life.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 12 (Personality Sun) — Standstill / Caution

[[Gate 12]] is the Throat voice of the individual circuit. As the lead of this Left Angle cross, Gate 12 turns the articulation outward. The being is here to speak — to inform — about the oppression of bonded life. The 12.4 prophet quality is especially active here; the carrier's words can carry the weight of mutation when timing allows.

### Gate 11 (Personality Earth) — Peace / Ideas

[[Gate 11]] grounds the conscious mind in the conceptual room of ideas. The Informing 2 being is filled with ideas about partnership, oppression, and how human beings might live together more wisely. The 11.4 — the teacher, guru, sage — pairs with the 12.4 prophet to produce one of the strongest teaching configurations on the wheel.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence / The Spirit of the Self

[[Gate 25]] is the G Center gate of universal love. As the unconscious driver, Gate 25 grounds the informing in the innocent love that does not calculate. The Informing 2 being does not inform from grievance or revenge; the informing arises from the innocent recognition that those who are caught in bonded oppression deserve to be told what is happening.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self

[[Gate 46]] is the G Center gate of the love of the body. As Earth, Gate 46 grounds the informing in the physical body itself. The carrier is not a disembodied voice but a body that has lived through the oppression they inform about.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality places this cross in the realm where bonding is the purpose. The Informing 2 being takes the bonding theme and turns it into teaching. They have lived the oppression of bonded life, they have seen through it, and they are now structurally built to articulate what they have seen — not for their own benefit, but for the benefit of those still caught in the trap.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle Crosses carry Transpersonal Karma. The Informing 2 being is here to inform on bonded oppression on behalf of those who cannot inform for themselves. The karma flows through the carrier as a transpersonal current — the informing is the work, and the work serves the collective consciousness about partnership.

Ra connected this cross to the saintly archetype that emerges in repressed societies. The saint embraces the not-self with grace, eats lunch with those who do not yet see, and offers aid without hypocrisy. The Informing 2 being can play this role at scale or in intimate settings — the same structural orientation applies whether the carrier is teaching a class or simply being present with a struggling friend.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The five Left Angle profiles produce five distinct flavors of Informing 2.

The 4/6 informs through their network of friends. The informing is intimate, personal, and reaches a defined circle. The 5/1 is the public informer — the teacher, writer, or speaker who broadcasts the insights about bonded oppression to the universal field. The 5/1 carries foundational research and universal projection, and they pay a price for the role they play. The 5/2 knows the truth about partnership and rarely speaks; when they do speak, the words carry the weight of self-motivated authority. The 6/2 is the experiential sage — the role model who has lived through the oppression, climbed onto the roof, and now speaks from detached wisdom. Ra noted that this 6/2 line is the line of futility, and the carrier's gift is to recognize that bonded life cannot be perfected, only inhabited consciously. The 6/3 carries the hypocrisy line — they may speak about partnership in ways that contradict how they live it, and this gap is itself one of the most teaching things about the cross.

## Living This Cross

For the Informing 2 being, the work is saintly informing without becoming the martyr. The 12 must speak when timing is right and go silent when timing is wrong. The 25 must love without calculation. The 46 must inhabit the body. The 11 must hold the ideas without becoming attached to whether they are received.

Ra was clear that this cross requires acceptance of oppression as a feature of bonded life, not a bug to be eliminated. The carrier who fights to eliminate all partnership oppression will burn out. The carrier who accepts that oppression is structurally present and works to make it conscious will become the saint who can offer real aid.

The shadow path is the carrier who becomes preachy, hypocritical, or martyred. The 6/3 hypocrisy line shows up here — telling others how to partner while privately failing in their own bonds. Ra noted this is not always destructive; sometimes the best advice is "do as I say, not as I do." But unconscious hypocrisy collapses the cross's authority.

The medicine is to honor Strategy and Inner Authority. The Informing 2 being who waits for the right moment to speak, who lets the 12 wave finish before articulating, who allows the 25 innocence to lead the informing rather than the mental urgency of the 11 — this carrier becomes one of the most powerful teaching voices on the wheel.

The 5/1 Manifestor with this cross can become a saintly public teacher whose informing transforms how the collective understands partnership. The 6/2 carrier becomes the wise elder whose mere presence teaches more than their words. Each profile expression produces a distinct saintly form, and the integrated cross delivers transpersonal informing that can mutate the collective field about bonded life.
