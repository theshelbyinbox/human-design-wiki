# Juxtaposition Cross of the Vessel of Love 4

The Juxtaposition Cross of the Vessel of Love 4 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Behavior. It is the fixed-fate variant of the four Vessel of Love gates in the Quarter of Mutation, incarnated through the only profile that produces a Juxtaposition Cross: the 4/1. The carrier is here to live a single, unmoving, exemplary conduct — to be themselves in a fixed way, in their own body, in their own circle, and to let the consistency of that conduct become the contribution. Where the Right Angle of this cross is the personal journey of self-love and the Left Angle is self-love as preventive social work, the Juxtaposition is simply the unwavering presence of correct behavior as a fact in the world.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 10 — Treading / Behavior of the Self
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 15 — Modesty
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 — Innocence
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 — Pushing Upward
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Juxtaposition — Fixed Fate
- **Profile:** 4/1 only

The gate set is identical to the other two angles of the Vessel of Love 4. The Juxtaposition difference is the rigidity of the conduct. The 4/1 carrier of Behavior is not on a developmental path. The behavior is set at birth. The work is to honor it.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 10 (Personality Sun) — Treading

The fixed behavior of the self. The Juxtaposition carrier walks through their own life in a recognisable, repeatable way. They are themselves. They do not modulate the self for different audiences. The 4/1's fixed profile makes the behavior reliable — what the carrier did yesterday is essentially what the carrier will do tomorrow, modified only by the body's natural rhythms.

### Gate 15 (Personality Earth) — Modesty

Love of humanity as the underlying ground. The 15 ensures that the fixed behavior does not isolate the carrier. The wide aura keeps the carrier connected to the human field even as the behavior remains unwavering. The carrier is consistent without being rigid; the consistency is animated by the 15's tolerance for difference.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence

Universal love beneath the surface. The 25 protects the behavior from becoming a performance. The Juxtaposition carrier's conduct is innocent — not strategic, not calculated, not curated for an audience. It is simply what the body does.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward

The body as the fixed vessel. The carrier's serendipity places them in the right rooms at the right times, where their fixed behavior can do its work. The body delivers. The carrier walks.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to change what has been built. The Juxtaposition Behavior 4 contributes a fixed pulse of correct conduct at the midpoint of that mutative work. The carrier is the steady example in a quarter otherwise defined by change. Their conduct does not change — and the unchangingness is the contribution.

Ra Uru Hu placed the Vessel of Love at the midpoint of every quarter and called it the heart of the wheel. The Juxtaposition Behavior 4 is that heart, in Quarter 4, frozen into a single body whose unchanging conduct anchors the surrounding mutation.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is a cross of fixed fate. The carrier's destiny is set. The frequency is set. The work is not to develop the cross but to honor what was complete at birth.

For Behavior 4, this means the carrier walks into the world already in possession of their correct conduct. The work is not to learn it. The work is not to improve it. The work is to live it without compromise, in the carrier's own body, in the carrier's own circle, and to let the consistency become a quiet medicine.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to change their behavior to please others. The 4/1 who lets the surrounding culture dictate their conduct loses access to the cross's design. The recovery is to come back to the body and resume the original behavior.

Juxtaposition Crosses are the rarest of the three angles, and Ra described them as the rigid centre of the cross's expression. For Behavior 4, the rigidity is the conduct itself, set at birth, demanding only the carrier's continued surrender to it.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Every Juxtaposition Cross is held by the 4/1 profile only. The 4/1 Behavior 4 is the externalising friend with a foundation — the person whose social conduct is grounded in their own 1st line study of what their body actually wants. The carrier is the example, in their immediate friend-and-acquaintance network, of what consistent self-love looks like across years.

The 4/1 is the only fixed profile on the wheel. The carrier of Behavior 4 is therefore not on a developmental path. They are born complete. The conduct is in working order from the start, and the only thing that interferes is the cultural pressure to behave differently.

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation system. The carrier's [[Type]] determines how the fixed behavior lands correctly. A Generator 4/1 Behavior responds with the sacral and walks the path the body recognises. A Projector 4/1 Behavior waits to be recognised by their fixed network as the carrier of correct conduct. A Manifestor 4/1 informs and proceeds. A Reflector 4/1 samples the lunar cycle within a fixed circle.

The signature lived well is a quietly consistent life. The carrier becomes recognisable, predictable, and reliable in their conduct — not in a rigid or performative way, but in the gentle way of someone who is fully themselves. Their friends and acquaintances learn what to expect from the carrier, and what they expect becomes a slow medicine.

The not-self signature is exhaustion from chronic self-modification. The 4/1 who tries to be different for different audiences burns out quickly, because the design does not support variability. The correction is to stop modulating and resume the original conduct.

When the carrier honours the design, they become a quiet still-point of self-love in their immediate circle. They are the one whose ordinary daily life — how they cook, how they sleep, how they speak — becomes a small but persistent demonstration that another way of being is possible. They do not have to teach this. They simply live it.

This is the rarest and quietest of the Vessel of Love 4 expressions and the one most easily missed by the carrier themselves. The work is to notice that one is already in possession of the correct conduct, and to stop trying to become someone else.
