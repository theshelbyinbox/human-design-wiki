# Left Angle Cross of Explanation 2

The Left Angle Cross of Explanation 2 carries Gates 23, 43, 49 and 4, with the Personality Sun in Gate 23 — Splitting Apart, the throat gate of the individual knower — at the angle of transpersonal karma. The change from right to left angle places the structuring voice of this carrier into karmic service. The voice is not for the carrier's private knowing; it is karmically placed in the lives of others as a mutative tongue.

This is one of the most mechanically loaded incarnation crosses on the wheel for the work of language. The carrier's voice is built to mutate the people who hear it — and the people who hear it are not random. They are karmically magnetised to the carrier's field.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 23 — Splitting Apart
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 43 — Breakthrough / Insight
- **Design Sun:** Gate 49 — Principles / Revolution
- **Design Earth:** Gate 4 — Youthful Folly / Formulization
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

Gate composition matches the right-angle Explanation 2. The Sun's angle at incarnation places this life into the transpersonal half of the wheel. The carrier's voice is karmic.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 23 (Personality Sun) — Splitting Apart

The conscious purpose is the structuring voice of individual knowing. In transpersonal service, this voice is broadcast. The carrier's listeners are not accidental; they arrive in the carrier's field karmically.

The voice's timing is still everything — Gate 23 is famously the gate that lands as genius or as freak depending on whether the listener is ready — but the karmic placement of left-angle Explanation 2 means the right listeners do arrive, even when the carrier does not know how to find them. The work is to honour strategy and authority so that when the listener arrives, the voice is ready.

### Gate 43 (Personality Earth) — Breakthrough / Insight

Grounds the voice in the wordless inner knowing it is translating. The carrier's structuring voice has substance beneath it; what comes out is not opinion but report.

### Gate 49 (Design Sun) — Principles / Revolution

The unconscious carrier of principles. Beneath the conscious voice, the body holds emotional verdicts about what is principled. The voice's articulations are emotionally weighted in ways the conscious mind does not always track.

### Gate 4 (Design Earth) — Youthful Folly / Formulization

The unconscious formulator. The Ajna produces possibilities, formulas, mental hypotheses that the voice can shape. The structuring voice has access to a continuous stream of mental possibilities to refine.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the quarter of form. The Cross of Explanation contributes the articulations that civilization needs in order to encode its mutations into shared language.

The left-angle variant places this articulation work into transpersonal service. The carrier does not translate individual knowing for their own use; they translate it for the civilizations they are karmically embedded in. Ra Uru Hu himself is a striking historical example: a left-angle carrier of related gates whose structuring voice produced an entire body of structured knowledge that mutated the field it landed in.

## The Angle Expression

The Left Angle is transpersonal karma. The carrier's voice has karmic weight. The mutation it carries does not stay private; it ripples out into the cultural fields the carrier touches.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who, sensing the weight of the voice, becomes afraid of speaking. The carrier withholds, hides, performs muteness — and feels the unspoken knowing curdle into bitterness. The opposite distortion is the carrier who, sensing the weight of the voice, speaks too freely, without strategy or authority, and finds the voice consistently dismissed because the timing is wrong.

The correct expression is patient and surrendered. The voice waits. The insight ripens. The right listener arrives. The carrier speaks when their authority clears the speaking, and the structured knowing lands cleanly. The karmic ripple takes care of itself.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left-angle profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, 6/3.

- **4/6** is the Opportunist whose structuring voice lands through the close network in the first life phase, through retreat in the second, through public role-modelling in the third
- **5/1** is the Heretic whose voice is projected onto by strangers as universal solution
- **5/2** is the Heretic-Hermit whose voice is broadcast despite the carrier's desire for solitude
- **6/2** is the Role Model-Hermit whose voice matures with age into elder wisdom
- **6/3** is the Role Model-Martyr whose voice is forged through visible mistakes that become wisdom

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are non-negotiable. Gate 23 will not operate safely on mind alone. The carrier must trust their authority to determine when the voice should actually speak. The mind will always argue for speaking sooner, more loudly, to more people; the body knows when the structured knowing is actually ready to land.

The not-self signature is the carrier exhausted by being misheard — the structured knower whose voice keeps getting dismissed because the timing or audience is wrong. The signature lived well is the carrier whose voice, when it finally speaks, produces real mutation in the cultural fields it enters.

Ra Uru Hu's framing of the gate stands: "The 23 is the gate of the knower. The voice that says 'I know' — and actually does." — Ra Uru Hu. For the left-angle Explanation 2 carrier, the voice is karmic; the knowing is real; the work is to honour the timing.

The placement of this cross in the Quarter of Civilization gives the voice a particular material weight. Civilization runs on shared language. The Cross of Explanation in this quarter is one of the gates through which civilization receives the new language it needs to articulate its own mutations. When the carrier's voice lands cleanly, the listener's vocabulary changes; when the vocabulary changes, the listener's perception changes; when the perception changes, the form of civilization itself begins to shift. The mutation is not abstract — it is encoded in the language the carrier brings into circulation.

The left-angle carrier also has to learn to release the work after speaking. Once the voice has delivered the structured knowing, the listener takes it where it will go. The carrier does not get to control the use of their own words. People will misquote, misapply, take fragments out of context, and build things the carrier never imagined. This is part of the karmic placement. The carrier delivers; the karmic field disposes. The signature lived well is the carrier at peace with this — speaking what is theirs to speak, then letting the ripple do its own work in fields they will never see.
