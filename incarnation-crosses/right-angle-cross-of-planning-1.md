# Right Angle Cross of Planning 1

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 1 is the lead variant of the Cross of Planning, anchored in the Quarter of Initiation. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 37]] (The Family) and the Personality Earth in [[Gate 40]] (Deliverance), this cross carries the tribal bargain forward as a mental project: an agreement between mouth and stomach, between feeling and provision, that organizes community life. The Design Sun in [[Gate 16]] (Enthusiasm) and Design Earth in [[Gate 9]] (Focus) provide the underlying skill and concentration that make the bargain functional. Ra Uru Hu placed this cross at the heart of a 400-year planetary cycle — the era of community, nation-state, and institution that began in 1615 and is now closing — and people who incarnate on this cross feel deeply at home in the architecture they helped to build.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Design Sun:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Design Earth:** Gate 9 — The Taming Power of the Small (Focus)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone, Purpose: Mind)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — personal destiny, the karma of self
- **Axis A (Personality):** 37 ↔ 40
- **Axis B (Design):** 9 ↔ 16

## The Four Gates

### Gate 37 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the tribe, the gate of friendship and the bargain. It carries the touch, the affection, and the open hand that holds a community together. As the lead gate of the Cross of Planning 1, Gate 37 frames the entire purpose: the plan exists to serve the family, to give the bargain a structure it can survive in.

### Gate 40 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 40]] is the stomach, the gate of deliverance. It is the will-energy that says "alone" — that withdraws to recover so it can keep providing. As the grounding earth gate, Gate 40 anchors the cross in the work it takes to feed the tribe. The 37/40 channel, the [[Channel 37/40]] of Community, is what makes the bargain real.

### Gate 16 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 16]] is enthusiasm at the Throat — the experimental voice that says "I'll try." It carries the artistic, repetitive quality of skill. In the design body of this cross, Gate 16 supplies the willingness that lets focus become practiced expression.

### Gate 40 — Design Earth correction
**Design Earth:** Gate 9 — Focus. [[Gate 9]] is the Sacral capacity to break the future into small enough pieces to power it. Beneath the Cross of Planning 1, Gate 9 supplies the granular attention that civilization is built from. Together with Gate 16, the 9/16 axis is the [[Channel 9/16]] of Identification — talent meeting concentration.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the Quarter of Mind, governed by the Realm of Alcyone. This is the opening quarter of the wheel — where new direction is sought through mental processes, where consciousness reaches out to make sense of life. The Cross of Planning 1, sitting in this quarter, makes planning itself a mental initiation. The plan is a thought-form. It tries to imagine what the family will need next, what the community must build, how the bargain holds. Ra called this quarter the place of Purpose Through Mind — and the Cross of Planning 1 turns that mind toward the maintenance of tribal bonds.

## The Angle Expression

Right Angle crosses are crosses of personal destiny. The life is lived for the self, by the self, with the self as the central karmic vehicle. A Right Angle Cross of Planning 1 person is not here to plan for someone else's enlightenment; they are here to live out the bargain, to feel it from the inside, to discover through their own design what their bargain is worth. This makes the Cross of Planning 1 deeply self-referential. The plan is personal. The family is personal. The deliverance is personal.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 1 manifests in the six Right Angle profiles:
[[1/3]] | [[1/4]] | [[2/4]] | [[2/5]] | [[3/5]] | [[3/6]]

Each profile colors the cross differently. The 1/3 plans, then learns through trial and error which plans don't work — and this is precisely their gift: to remove failed plans from the recipe mix so others don't waste time on them. The 1/4 plans for the friends, building bargains inside their network. The 2/4 is the ultimate tribal natural, whose plan emerges from being called by name. The 2/5 plans with the projection of leadership upon them, often resented when the plan disappoints. The 3/5 says "great plan" and goes, learning by collision what the bargain costs. The 3/6 spends the first thirty years inside trial and error before stepping onto the roof to plan from a different altitude.

## Living This Cross

For people on the Right Angle Cross of Planning 1, the most reliable doorway is [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]]. The plan is a mental construction. The 37/40 wants the bargain, the 9/16 wants to focus and try — but none of that should be initiated from the mind. The plan only becomes nourishing when the body has been asked first.

The shadow of this cross is the bargain made under pressure: the family that demands provision before the body is ready, the community that calls on Gate 40 to deliver when Gate 40 needs to be alone. Ra noted that Gate 40's healthiest expression is the "no" that allows the eventual "yes" — the withdrawal that makes future generosity possible. For a Cross of Planning 1, deliverance always begins with deliverance from the bargain itself, returning to [[Not-Self]] mind so the real bargain can be heard.

The gift, when lived in alignment, is community design. These are the people who can see how a family system holds together, what the next plan needs to be, where the skill of Gate 16 can be aimed, and how Gate 9 must focus to make any of it real. Ra was clear: "The Cross of Planning has been very successful in many ways." That success came from people who lived the bargain from the body, not the brain.

This cross is also subject to the closing of its own era. Ra placed the Cross of Planning cycle's end at 2027. The Right Angle Cross of Planning 1 person, born inside the cycle, often feels the tension between the institutions they were built to maintain and the dissolution of those institutions in the new cycle to come. Their purpose is not to save the plan but to live their own bargain honestly while the larger plan dissolves.
