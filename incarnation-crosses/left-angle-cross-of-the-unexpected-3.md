# Left Angle Cross of the Unexpected 3

The Left Angle Cross of the Unexpected 3 is the transpersonal risk-taker — the karmic configuration of the Game Player in the Quarter of Duality. The Personality Sun sits in Gate 28, the splenic gate of struggle, and the carrier is here to take the risks the bonded community needs taken on its behalf. Where the Right Angle Unexpected 3 carrier takes the risk to find their own purpose, the Left Angle carrier takes the risk for the species.

Ra Uru Hu placed the 28 at the centre of the question of purpose: "There‟s no point doing anything unless we have a purpose." The Left Angle Unexpected 3 carrier lives that question in the public arena. Their risks become other people's models. Their struggle becomes the lesson the community uses to clarify its own purpose.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 28 — Preponderance of the Great (The Game Player)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 27 — Nourishment / Caring
- **Design Sun:** Gate 41 — Decrease / Contraction
- **Design Earth:** Gate 31 — Influence
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal / Karmic Destiny

The same four gates as the Right Angle Cross of the Unexpected 3 build the configuration, but the angle relocates the function. The risk is taken for others.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 28 (Personality Sun) — The Game Player

The conscious purpose. Gate 28 is the splenic awareness that purpose is worth dying for. In the Left Angle expression the carrier is here to take risks the community needs taken — to be the one who walks into the burning building, the unstable conversation, the unfinanced project — so that the community can witness what purpose looks like under pressure. The unexpected nature of the cross guarantees that the risks will not be the ones the carrier prepared for.

### Gate 27 (Personality Earth) — Nourishment

The ground beneath the risk. Gate 27 keeps the Left Angle Game Player tethered to the actual people the risk is being taken for. The caring is what distinguishes the karmic risk-taker from the reckless adventurer. The 27 of this configuration is wide and tribal; it cares for the community whose risk is being absorbed by the carrier's body.

### Gate 41 (Design Sun) — Decrease

The unconscious root pressure. The body keeps generating new contracted starting points — new fantasies of risk worth taking, new longings to engage on behalf of the bonded life. The 41 is what keeps the Left Angle Game Player supplied with the next opportunity to demonstrate purpose.

### Gate 31 (Design Earth) — Influence

The unconscious leadership ground. The body is built to be watched. The 31 ensures that the risks the Left Angle carrier takes become visible to the community, models for the community, and eventually the leadership the community asks for.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. The Left Angle Unexpected 3 carrier contributes to the bonded quarter by being the one whose risks define the community's understanding of purpose. The bond is the field within which the risk has meaning. Without the bond the risk is private; with the bond the risk becomes a public clarification.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a transpersonal destiny. The Left Angle Unexpected 3 carrier is here to risk for others. The not-self distortion is the carrier who hoards the risk, who refuses to engage the struggle, or who takes risks that have no relationship to the community's actual need. The correct expression engages the risk when the unexpected delivers it, lets the 27 keep the caring real, and trusts that the karmic role is being fulfilled even when the outcome is unflattering.

Ra noted that 3rd lines on the Right Angle of this cross have a particular gift for trial and error. On the Left Angle the gift scales up — the 3/5, 6/3, and similar profiles carry mistakes that the community studies for generations. The Left Angle Unexpected 3 is the karmic position of the public failure that turns out to have been the necessary lesson.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle is carried by the five classical left-angle profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3.

- **4/6** the externalising risk-taker whose risks are absorbed by a stable network across three life phases
- **5/1** the projected practical risk-taker called by crisis to engage the struggle on the collective's behalf
- **5/2** the projected hermit-risktaker pulled out of solitude by the projection of the community
- **6/2** the role-model risk-taker who matures into the elder whose past struggles become the community's wisdom
- **6/3** the role-model trial-and-error risk-taker whose entire life is a public demonstration of the cost of purpose

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only reliable navigation system. A Projector with this cross waits for the invitation before engaging the risk; a Generator responds with the sacral to which risks are theirs to take; a Manifestor informs before the risk implicates the community; a Reflector lets a lunar cycle reveal whether the risk's environment is genuinely correct.

The signature of the cross lived well is a karmic risk taken with full integrity. The not-self signature is the bitterness of the carrier who took risks the community had not asked them to take, or the regret of the carrier who refused the risk the community had clearly delegated. The work is to let the unexpected select the risk, to engage it with the 27 fully fed and the 31 underneath, and to let the public mistake become public wisdom.

When the carrier honours the design, they become the person the community remembers as the one who walked into the fire so that the community could see what purpose under pressure actually looks like — and whose 18-year-cycle stories become the inheritance of the next generation of risk-takers.
