# Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 4

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 4 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Juxtaposition Cross of Thinking — is the fixed-fate variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 4. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth) at line 4, and the entire architecture of the cross is locked into the accent of thinking. Juxtaposition crosses are the rarest cross type, present only in the 4/1 profile, and they represent a pure, concentrated expression of a single thematic accent. In the Quarter of Mutation, where purpose moves through Transformation, the Juxtaposition of Thinking is the fixed life-task of being the mutative thinker inside a specific small network.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth), Line 4
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 62]] (Detail), Line 4
- Design Sun: [[Gate 32]] or [[Gate 42]], Line 1
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun, Line 1
- Personality Axis: 61 / 62
- Design Axis: 32 / 42
- Quarter: Quarter of Mutation (Realm of the Godhead)
- Realm Purpose: Transformation
- Mystical Theme: Accepting Death
- Angle Expression: Juxtaposition — fixed fate
- Profile: 4/1 only

## The Four Gates

### Gate 61 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 61]] in the Juxtaposition is the fixed orientation toward inner-truth pressure. At line 4, the gate carries Research — the most relational and methodical expression of mystical inquiry. The 4/1 profile makes this research externally networked: the carrier's thinking is offered to a specific bonded community. The Juxtaposition of Thinking fixes this into a constant, lifelong role — the carrier is here to be the one who thinks the unthought thoughts inside their network.

Ra emphasized that the 61 line 4 carries the gift of organized, systematic mystical research often pursued in dialogue with a chosen community. The shadow is the fourth line whose research becomes endless, who confuses gathering material for arriving at insight.

### Gate 62 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 62]] at line 4 is Asceticism — the relational, externalized expression of precision. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's life of mystical thinking is rooted in detail-work offered to the same chosen community. The thinking has practical anchors: dates, references, named specifics that make the research transmissible.

### Gate 32 (Design)

[[Gate 32]] in the design imprint at line 1 is Conservation — the foundational expression of splenic continuity-judgment. The body has a built-in instinct about which thoughts are worth preserving and which should be released. In the Maya 4 Juxtaposition, this means the carrier's body silently filters the constant mental pressure of the 61.

### Gate 42 (Design)

[[Gate 42]] at line 1 is Diversification — the foundational sacral willingness to spread energy across multiple beginnings. The body knows that mutative thinking must respect the natural rhythm of cycles, and at line 1 it has the foundational capacity to start many tracks of inquiry rather than committing too early to any one of them.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation channels purpose through Transformation. Its mystical theme is Accepting Death — the recognition that real mutation requires the death of the old before the new can emerge. The Maya cross in this quarter is the cross of the mind that sees through the maya; the Juxtaposition of Thinking accents this work into pure focus.

The carrier's life is fixed inside the role of network-thinker. The 4/1 profile makes the small bonded community the operating field. The carrier is here to be the trusted mutative thinker inside that specific group — the one whose 61 pressure produces the unusual thoughts the community needs, organized through line 4's relational research method.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. The 4/1 profile is the most genetically fixed profile in Human Design, and the Juxtaposition further locks the cross into a single thematic accent. The Juxtaposition Maya 4 carrier is not the wide-ranging mystic of the Right Angle nor the transpersonal teacher of the Left Angle. They are the precise, fixed thinker inside their specific bonded network.

The mind will rebel against the smallness of the role. The cross teaches that the smallness is the precision. Inside the accent, the carrier can be unusually deep. Reaching beyond the accent — trying to be a public mystic, a published teacher, a wide-scope thought leader — typically produces the shadow expression of the 61: compulsive seeking, mental loops, the inability to be still long enough for insight to arrive.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Juxtaposition Cross of Maya 4 manifests only in the 4/1 profile. The 4 line is the Opportunist — externalized, networked, social. The 1 line is the Investigator — deep, foundational, private study. Together the 4/1 is the person who networks their foundation. In the Maya 4 Juxtaposition, this profile is fixed into the accent of thinking: the 4/1 carrier becomes the trusted mutative thinker inside their chosen circle.

## Living This Cross

Living the Juxtaposition of Thinking well means embracing the fixity. The carrier is not designed to broadcast their mysticism to the world. They are designed to think, deeply and systematically, inside a small bonded community whose members can receive what they produce. The mind will want broader scope; the cross teaches narrower focus.

Structured stillness is essential. The 61 pressure is constant, and without the silence in which insight can arrive, the carrier descends into mental loops dressed as mysticism. Meditation, walks, time alone in nature, periods of withdrawal from the network — these are not luxuries but the operating conditions of the cross.

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] filter which research-tracks are correct. Without them, the carrier becomes the friend who is always proposing the next mystical inquiry, accumulating material that never resolves into insight. With them, the carrier learns which threads belong to this life and which are conditioning that can be released.

The 62 Earth grounds the thinking in precise particulars. The carrier's most reliable insights are the ones that can be named — connected to specific lived events, specific references, specific dates. Vague mystical speculation is the shadow; precisely named mystical thinking is the gift.

The 32 design pulse keeps the body honest about which lines of thinking will hold and which are passing. The 42 design pulse keeps the carrier inside any one cycle of inquiry long enough for it to mature. Together they give the Juxtaposition Maya 4 body a quiet, fixed filtering capacity for the 61 pressure.

The deeper teaching is that the maya is dense, the mind is mutative, and the work of seeing through the maya happens in small, fixed accents inside bonded networks. The Juxtaposition Maya 4 carrier is one of these accents. Their fixity is their precision. Inside their narrow role, their contribution to the network's understanding of reality can be unusually deep.

In the chosen community, the Juxtaposition Maya 4 person becomes the trusted thinker — the one whose unusual perceptions are sought when the group encounters a question no one else can quite frame. Their contribution is rarely public and rarely visible to those outside the network, but inside the network it is foundational. The witness, in their case, returns by thinking what the others have not yet been able to think, inside the bonded company of those who can use the result.
