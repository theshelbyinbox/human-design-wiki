# Juxtaposition Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2

The Juxtaposition Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2 is the fixed-fate variant of the Sleeping Phoenix gate set in the Quarter of Civilization. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Juxtaposition Cross of The Now. The name is precise: the carrier is a 4/1 profile whose entire fixed fate is the gateway of the present moment that Gate 20 represents.

The one-line theme: the now as fixed fate. The carrier is here to embody presence — or its absence — in a single, rigid, unchanging stance toward the current moment.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 20 — Contemplation
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 34 — The Power of the Great
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization
- **Angle:** Juxtaposition — Fixed Fate

The Juxtaposition shares the gate set of the Right Angle Cross of the Sleeping Phoenix 2 but manifests exclusively in the 4/1 profile. The personality is fixed around the throat voice of the now and the pure sacral power of the 34; the design is fixed around the moody emotional pulse of the 55 grounded in the genetic role gate of the 59. Whatever stance the carrier takes toward presence — awake or asleep — that stance is fixed for life.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 20 (Personality Sun) — Contemplation

The throat voice of the now. In the Juxtaposition fixed-fate position, the carrier's relationship to the present moment is locked. They are either fixedly present or fixedly absent; they do not get to fluctuate the way the Right Angle and Left Angle carriers do.

### Gate 34 (Personality Earth) — The Power of the Great

The sacral power that fuels the personality. The 4/1 carrier's body has sustained energy for whatever fixed stance the Personality is taking. If the carrier is fixedly present, the body sustains presence. If the carrier is fixedly asleep, the body sustains the sleeping busyness.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The moodiness of mutating spirit grounds the body. The 4/1 carrier of The Now still has the wave underneath, but the wave does not break through the fixed personality stance the way it does in other angles. The mood is constant background rather than felt foreground.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The body's intimate biology. The 4/1's social bonds are fixed — they form the kinds of friendships and partnerships their early conditioning installed, and those bonds become the constant social environment of the life.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. Within this quarter, the Juxtaposition The Now serves the specific function of embodying a fixed stance toward presence. While the Right Angle Sleeping Phoenix 2 sleepwalks through civilisation and the Left Angle Duality 1 teaches presence through visible fluctuation, the Juxtaposition The Now is a fixed point of either awakeness or sleep that other people encounter and respond to.

Ra emphasised that the Juxtaposition is rare (approximately 1% of the population per cross) and that the 4/1 fixed harmony cannot be argued out of its stance. If the carrier is correctly conditioned, they become a fixed point of presence in a sleepwalking civilisation. If they are incorrectly conditioned, they become a fixed point of sleep that nothing can wake.

## The Angle Expression

A Juxtaposition Cross is fixed fate. The 4/1 carrier of The Now does not get to choose between awakeness and sleep. They were conditioned into one stance or the other during their first Saturn cycle, and that stance becomes the rest of their life.

For Juxtaposition The Now, the fixed fate is the carrier's stance toward presence. Those rare 4/1 carriers who were raised in healthy presence — by parents or guardians who modelled awakeness — become some of the most enduringly present people in the wheel. They are the steady, anchored, here-now humans whose presence others find restorative.

Those 4/1 carriers raised in distraction become the opposite — fixedly absent, mechanically busy, unwakeable. Ra was clear that no amount of intervention will shift this in adulthood. The not-self 4/1 The Now is one of the hardest configurations to work with because the fixedness resists every approach.

The correct expression is the carrier accepting their fixedness. If they are present, they let the presence be the gift; if they are absent, they at least admit it, refusing to perform a fluctuation they cannot deliver.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Juxtaposition crosses manifest in the 4/1 profile only. Approximately 1% of the population carries any given Juxtaposition cross. The 4/1 is the only profile with the fixed harmonic Ra associated with the Juxtaposition angle.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept the fixedness. The carrier has whatever inner authority their design provides; the fixedness applies to the orientation of consciousness, not to the authority itself.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] resents their stance and tries to perform the opposite. Living from [[Signature]] means letting the fixed stance be the offering. The present carrier offers presence; the absent carrier offers honest absence. Either is more useful than performance.

The deepest practice for this cross is acceptance. The 4/1 was conditioned into something; that something is the life. The work is not to escape the conditioning but to live it cleanly. If the carrier is present, the practice is to remain present without performing it; if the carrier is absent, the practice is to remain absent without faking presence.

The healthiest 4/1 carriers of this cross become quiet anchors in their communities. They are not the visible teachers; they are the steady people whose presence (or honest absence) other people learn to rely on without naming why.
