# Right Angle Cross of Planning 2

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 carries the same four gates as every Cross of Planning — [[Gate 37]], [[Gate 40]], [[Gate 9]], and [[Gate 16]] — but rotates the wheel so that Gate 16, Enthusiasm, becomes the Personality Sun. In this variant, the bargain is no longer the leading note; the leading note is the skill, the artistic trying, the experimental enthusiasm that civilizes the plan. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 16]] and the Personality Earth in [[Gate 9]], the cross asks: what can be tried, what can be refined, what talent can be brought to the bargain that makes community life beautiful rather than merely functional? The Design Sun in [[Gate 37]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 40]] hold the tribal undercurrent steady beneath the experiment.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 9 — Focus (The Taming Power of the Small)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Design Earth:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose: Form)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — personal destiny
- **Axis A (Personality):** 9 ↔ 16
- **Axis B (Design):** 37 ↔ 40

## The Four Gates

### Gate 16 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat gate of enthusiasm, the voice that says "I'll have a go." It is the manifesting expression of skill and the willingness to repeat. In the Cross of Planning 2, Gate 16 leads — and so the entire plan is framed through enthusiasm for the experiment. Civilization needs people willing to try the new craft, learn the new instrument, master the new method. This cross provides them.

### Gate 9 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral capacity for focused attention on small detail. It is the engineering gate that makes any skill real by tending to the part of the work no one else has the patience for. As the Earth of the Cross of Planning 2, Gate 9 grounds the enthusiasm in the granular reality of practice. Talent without Gate 9 has no place to land.

### Gate 37 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the tribe, the gate of friendship and the bargain. Beneath the experimental civilizing surface of this cross, Gate 37 carries the social agreement that gives the experiment a community to serve. The bargain is the design context inside which the enthusiasm operates.

### Gate 40 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 40]] is the will-energy of deliverance, the gate that withdraws to renew. Below the surface of the Cross of Planning 2, Gate 40 supplies the alone-time the artist needs in order to come back and try again. Without Gate 40 in the design, Gate 16 burns through its enthusiasm too quickly.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the Quarter of Form, governed by the Realm of Duhbe. Its purpose is to take the mental initiations of Quarter 1 and put them into matter, into culture, into structures we can see and touch. The Cross of Planning 2, sitting in this quarter, is the experimental form-builder of the Planning cross family. While the Right Angle Cross of Planning 1 leads with the bargain, the Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 leads with the artistic repetition that civilizes the bargain — the song that holds the family together, the craft that gives the community its identity, the practiced enthusiasm that shapes form.

## The Angle Expression

The Right Angle is personal destiny: the karma of the self for the self. A Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 person is not here to teach civilization what to try; they are here to live out their own civilization, to discover through their own life what enthusiasm refined by focus actually produces. The fixity is on the self. The experiment is personal.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 manifests in the six Right Angle profiles:
[[1/3]] | [[1/4]] | [[2/4]] | [[2/5]] | [[3/5]] | [[3/6]]

The 1/3 of this cross will try a skill, study it, then collide with the parts that don't work — refining the practice through hands-on failure. The 1/4 brings the experimental enthusiasm to a network, becoming the friend-of-friends who teaches the craft. The 2/4 is the natural with the instrument, called out by the network to demonstrate what they did not know they could do. The 2/5 carries the projection of being the expert, and learns the cost of being expected to perform on demand. The 3/5 is the experimenter who collides with skills until one sticks, then projects that solution outward. The 3/6 spends the first life-phase trying everything, the second observing what worked, the third becoming the role-model of refined practice.

## Living This Cross

For the Right Angle Cross of Planning 2, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are how the enthusiasm gets aimed honestly. The mind of this cross loves the new skill, the new instrument, the new method — but Gate 16's enthusiasm is a Throat output, and outputs only flow correctly when the body has been asked first. Initiating enthusiasm from the head produces the burned-out hobbyist who has tried everything and finished nothing.

The shadow of this cross is the manic experimenter — the person who picks up the violin, the language, the system, the certification, and drops each one before Gate 9 has had time to focus on a single detail long enough to bring the skill into form. Gate 16 alone is willingness; only Gate 9 lets willingness become mastery. The Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 must be patient with the focus piece — and patience comes from the bargain underneath. When Design Sun Gate 37 and Design Earth Gate 40 are honored — when the person rests in their community, lets the friendships hold them, and withdraws when they need to recover — the surface enthusiasm of Gate 16 stays available for the long arc of refinement.

The gift, when lived in alignment, is the civilizing of the plan. Cross of Planning 2 people give the community something beautiful to gather around: the song, the building, the dish, the practice. They are the artisans of the tribal bargain. Ra was clear that this 400-year cycle of the Cross of Planning has produced everything we recognize as modern civilization, including its arts — and the Right Angle Cross of Planning 2 person is, in a real sense, one of the carriers of that artistic skill into matter. Their job is to enjoy the experiment, master the focus, and let the bargain underneath give the work a community to serve.
