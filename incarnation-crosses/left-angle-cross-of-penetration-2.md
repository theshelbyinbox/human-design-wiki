# Left Angle Cross of Penetration 2

The Left Angle Cross of Penetration 2 — known in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Cycles 1 — is the transpersonal expression of the four-armed Cross of Penetration in the Quarter of Civilization. The same four gates — 51, 57, 53, 54 — are present, but the angle is Left, and the carrier's life is woven into a karmic field of cycles rather than into a personal arc of beginnings.

Ra spoke of the Cross of Cycles with particular care, noting that when you come from one cycle to the next, the Cross of Cycles carrier is the one whose karmic life is the wheel itself — the configuration of someone whose participation in civilization is structured around the cycles that begin, develop, and complete. Their penetration is the cyclic pressure that keeps civilization moving from one phase to the next.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 53 — Development / Beginnings
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 54 — The Marrying Maiden / Ambition
- **Design Sun:** Gate 51 — Shock / The Arousing
- **Design Earth:** Gate 57 — The Gentle / Intuition
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose of Form)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The gate layout matches the Right Angle Penetration 2, but the angle changes the relationship of the carrier to the configuration. The Left Angle Cycles carrier is part of a karmic field of cyclic transformation.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 53 (Personality Sun) — Development, Beginnings

The 53 in the Left Angle is the karmic pressure to begin. The Cycles 1 carrier consciously initiates the cycles their karmic field requires. They are not personal starters; they are the starters of the civilization-cycles that their network is part of.

### Gate 54 (Personality Earth) — The Marrying Maiden, Ambition

Gate 54 grounds the beginnings in upward ambition. The Cycles carrier's beginnings are not aimless; they are aimed at the recognition and transformation that completes the cycle.

### Gate 51 (Design Sun) — Shock, The Arousing

The unconscious heart-line ego. The body's foundation is the shock-surviving ego. The cycle-starter's body is built to survive the shocks that beginning generates.

### Gate 57 (Design Earth) — The Gentle, Intuition

The unconscious intuition. The vehicle reads the karmic field with splenic awareness — knowing when the cycle is correct to begin.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. The Left Angle Cycles 1 contributes to form by being the cyclic engine of civilization. Form is not static; it moves through cycles, and the Cycles carrier is the karmic configuration whose life is the engine of the movement.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The Cycles 1 carrier does not own their beginnings; they participate in the cyclic movement of a karmic field that uses them as its starter. Their penetration into civilization is the start of the next cycle for the network they belong to.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who has internalized the criticism of their starting and become someone who does not begin anything. The cyclic pressure remains in the body, but the conscious life has been organized around suppression of the pressure. The result is chronic frustration paired with chronic inability to commit.

The correct expression is the opposite: the carrier begins. They start the cycles their karmic field is ready for. The starts may not always become finishes — that work belongs to other configurations — but the starts are the carrier's task, and the network depends on them.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left Angle Crosses manifest through 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3.

- **4/6** carries the externalizing Cycles carrier whose beginnings happen through the network
- **5/1** carries the universalizing Cycles carrier with a foundational study underneath
- **5/2** carries the heretical hermit Cycles carrier — natural withdrawal meets called initiation
- **6/2** carries the matured hermit Cycles carrier across three life phases
- **6/3** carries the role-model experimenter Cycles carrier whose trial-and-error becomes the wisdom of an elder

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation. The 53's root pressure is constant; the body knows when the beginning is the karmic one and not just personal restlessness. The mind cannot reliably distinguish the two.

The signature of the cross lived correctly is a life of beginnings, each one given to the karmic field that was ready to develop it. The signature of the not-self is the carrier whose body is full of cyclic pressure but whose life is empty of beginnings. The work is to let the cycle start.

When the carrier honors the design, they become what civilization needs: the cyclic engine whose starts keep form moving. Ra spoke of the Cross of Cycles as the configuration that comes from one cycle to the next — and the carrier's life is the bridge between them. Lived correctly, Left Angle Penetration 2 is the cross of the cycle-bringer.

## The Cycles of Civilization

Civilization moves in cycles, not in lines. Every form has a beginning, a development, a peak, a decline, and a beginning of something else. The Left Angle Cycles carrier is the karmic configuration whose life is structurally aligned with the cyclic nature of form itself. They feel cycles. Their body senses when the existing cycle is exhausted and the new one is ready to begin. They are not always conscious of this sensing; the 53 is a root pressure gate that operates below the level of mental awareness.

What the carrier notices is the restlessness — the pressure to start something new, to break ground, to leave the existing cycle behind. The not-self carrier reads this restlessness as a personal flaw. The correct carrier reads it as the karmic signal that the field they belong to is ready for its next cycle to begin. The work is to wait for [[Inner Authority]] to confirm the timing and then start.
