# Left Angle Cross of Explanation 1

The Left Angle Cross of Explanation 1 carries the same four gates as the right-angle variant — Gates 49, 4, 23 and 43 — with the Personality Sun in Gate 49, the gate of Principles and Revolution. The change of angle from right to left transforms this from a personal-destiny cross into a transpersonal karmic placement. The carrier's life is no longer their own to live in private; their revolutionary principles are mechanically woven into the lives of others by design.

This is one of the more demanding crosses to live correctly because Gate 49 is a Solar Plexus gate. The emotional verdicts the carrier delivers about what is principled and what is not do not stay private. They radiate into the communities the carrier is karmically placed inside, and those communities reorganise themselves around the verdicts whether they consciously agree with them or not.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 49 — Principles / Revolution
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 4 — Youthful Folly / Formulization
- **Design Sun:** Gate 23 — Splitting Apart
- **Design Earth:** Gate 43 — Breakthrough / Insight
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone, Purpose of Mind)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The gate composition is identical to the right-angle Explanation 1. The Sun crosses the wheel at the angle that places this life into karmic service rather than personal destiny.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 49 (Personality Sun) — Principles / Revolution

The conscious purpose is the emotional verdict on what is principled. In transpersonal life this verdict is broadcast. Communities the carrier enters feel the principles the carrier holds, and the carrier becomes, often without consent, the voice that names what the group can no longer accept.

Ra Uru Hu's framing applies in heightened form: this is the gate of the butcher and the sacrificed, the gate that decides who eats and who belongs. The left-angle carrier of Explanation 1 makes these verdicts on behalf of communities they are entangled with — and the verdicts have weight.

### Gate 4 (Personality Earth) — Youthful Folly / Formulization

Grounds the revolutionary verdicts in mental possibility. The carrier offers explanations alongside the verdicts — formulas for why the principle is necessary, hypotheses for what the new agreement should look like.

### Gate 23 (Design Sun) — Splitting Apart

The unconscious voice. Beneath the conscious work of principles and formulas, the body carries a mutative throat gate that translates individual knowing into language. The carrier's revolutionary articulations land with the precision of structured individual voice rather than generic moral pronouncement.

### Gate 43 (Design Earth) — Breakthrough / Insight

The unconscious source of inner knowing. The principles the carrier carries are not derived from outside argument; beneath the surface, a wordless insight is producing the certainty.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the realm of mind. The Cross of Explanation contributes the explanations — the mental articulations of principles that the bonded life will be built on.

The left-angle variant places this work into transpersonal service. The carrier does not simply articulate principles for their own life; they articulate them, by karmic placement, for the lives of others. The mental initiation of every community they enter receives the carrier's revolutionary explanations whether the community asked for them or not.

## The Angle Expression

The Left Angle is transpersonal karma. For Explanation 1 this means the revolutionary principles are not the carrier's private moral conviction. They are karmic placements. The communities the carrier ends up reforming, breaking with, or refounding were always going to receive that work from this carrier.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses the karmic placement — who tries to live a small private life and finds themselves constantly drawn back into communities that need their principled verdict. The mind says "I do not want to be the one to name this"; the mechanics say "this is the cross, this is the work." The correct expression is surrender. The carrier honours the wave, waits for clarity, and then articulates the principle when the body has cleared it.

The transpersonal nature of the cross also means that the carrier's principles often produce revolutions the carrier does not get to control. The bond breaks; the community reforms; the carrier moves on. The principle did its work; the outcome belongs to the karmic field, not to the carrier.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left-angle profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, 6/3.

- **4/6** is the Opportunist whose revolutionary principles land through close-network bonds in the first life phase, through retreat in the second, through public role-modelling in the third
- **5/1** is the Heretic whose principles are projected onto by strangers as universal moral solutions
- **5/2** is the Heretic-Hermit whose principles are broadcast despite the carrier's desire for retreat
- **6/2** is the Role Model-Hermit whose principles mature with age into elder wisdom about what can and cannot be agreed to
- **6/3** is the Role Model-Martyr whose principles are forged through visible bond-breakings that become wisdom

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are essential, and [[Emotional Authority]] is particularly relevant because Gate 49 sits in the Solar Plexus. The wave must be honoured. Principles articulated at the peak of the wave produce premature revolutions; principles articulated at the bottom produce reactive bond-breakings. Only the principles that survive a full wave are stable enough to broadcast karmically.

The not-self signature is the carrier worn down by repeated false revolutions — communities they broke with prematurely, bonds they severed before the wave had cleared, principles they announced and then could not actually live by. The signature lived well is the carrier whose principles, when finally delivered, produce revolutions that actually renew — bonds that needed to break and could not have broken any other way.

Ra Uru Hu's note on Gate 49 carries particular weight here: "All of our revolutions, all of our principles have been conditioned ignorantly by the nature of this wave. The awareness potential is food discrimination." — Ra Uru Hu. The left-angle carrier brings that awareness into transpersonal service.
