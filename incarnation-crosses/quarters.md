# Quarters

In [[Human Design System]], the 64 gates of the Rave Mandala are organized into four Quarters of 16 gates each. Each Quarter carries a distinct thematic realm that shapes the general purpose of any [[Incarnation Cross]] that falls within it. The Personality Sun's gate position determines which Quarter a person's cross belongs to.

## Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone)

Theme: Purpose fulfilled through Mind.
Mystical theme: The Witness Returns.

This Quarter begins with Gate 13 (The Fellowship of Man) and contains gates oriented toward mental awareness, the collection of human experience, and the development of conscious understanding. Ra Uru Hu described Alcyone as "the quarter of mind" -- it is this initiation of mind that makes human beings uniquely capable of complex understanding and articulation.

Key gates include: 13, 49, 30, 55, 37, 63, 22, 36, 25, 17, 21, 51, 42, 3, 27, 24.

People born into this Quarter carry a purpose that involves the development, transmission, or awakening of awareness and understanding.

## Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe)

Theme: Purpose fulfilled through Form.
Mystical theme: From Womb to Room.

This Quarter contains every gate of the [[Throat Center]], making it the Quarter of manifestation. The pressure in this realm is toward getting things done, building things, making things concrete. Ra described it as "the quarter of manifestation -- this is all about getting things done, building things, and making things."

The Quarter of Civilization is oriented toward material expression: taking what has been understood (Initiation) and giving it form.

## Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter)

Theme: Purpose fulfilled through Bonding.
Mystical theme: Measure for Measure.

This Quarter is organized around intimacy, bonding, relationship, and the drive for connection at the genetic level. Ra described it as the realm of "this need for intimacy and of having someone to bond with, as well as to find security in that bonding."

People born into this Quarter are essential agents of human fertility and continuation -- carrying purposes organized around connection, community, and the sustaining of human relationships.

## Quarter of Mutation (Realm of the Godhead)

Theme: Purpose fulfilled through Transformation.
Mystical theme: Accepting Death.

This Quarter carries mutation -- the gates through which genuine evolutionary change moves into the collective. Ra noted that "of this Quarter of Mutation all mutation comes into the world. The nature of mutation for us living in a vast program is that the program initiates the mutation, not us."

People born into this Quarter carry purposes related to transformation, change, and the introduction of new evolutionary patterns into the collective field.

## Quarters and Incarnation Cross Reading

A cross's Quarter gives the broad thematic direction. The specific gate and line give more detail. A cross in the Quarter of Initiation carries a purpose organized around mind; a cross in the Quarter of Mutation carries a purpose organized around transformation. Within each Quarter, many different specific crosses express the Quarter's theme in different ways.
