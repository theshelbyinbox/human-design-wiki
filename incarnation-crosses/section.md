# Incarnation Cross

The Incarnation Cross is the four-gate signature of your specific incarnation — the four conscious and unconscious Sun and Earth activations that together describe the *purpose* you are here to live out. There are 192 incarnation crosses in total (64 themes × 3 angle variants), and each carries a distinct theme of life work.

Unlike type, authority, and profile — which describe *how* you operate — the Incarnation Cross describes *what* your specific life is themed around. It is the impersonal purpose: the role you play in the larger field, not a goal to chase or a personality to perform.

The Cross is also categorized by *Quarter* — the season of the solar year in which the Personality Sun falls — which gives a broader purpose theme to the cross.
