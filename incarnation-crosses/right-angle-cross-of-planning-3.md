# Right Angle Cross of Planning 3

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 3 places [[Gate 40]] — Deliverance — in the Personality Sun position and [[Gate 37]] — The Family — in the Personality Earth. In this variant, the leading note of the cross is not the bargain itself but the will that delivers the bargain: the alone-energy, the "I have done enough, leave me," the singular stomach that feeds the family but needs to walk away to recover. The Design Sun in [[Gate 9]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 16]] provide the focused, enthusiastic skill the deliverer can draw on. Where Planning 1 leads with the bargain and Planning 2 leads with the experiment, Planning 3 leads with the lone provider whose deliverance from the family is also the only way the family survives.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Design Sun:** Gate 9 — The Taming Power of the Small (Focus)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose: Bonding)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — personal destiny
- **Axis A (Personality):** 37 ↔ 40
- **Axis B (Design):** 9 ↔ 16

## The Four Gates

### Gate 40 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 40]] is the stomach, the lone gate of deliverance in the Will (Heart) Center. It is the gate that says "I'll do it, but then leave me alone." As the lead of the Cross of Planning 3, Gate 40 places aloneness at the center of the cross. The deliverance is not a withdrawal from life; it is the recovery cycle that lets the will keep showing up.

### Gate 37 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain, the gate of friendship. Beneath the lone deliverer, Gate 37 grounds the cross in the family it is delivering to. Without Gate 37, Gate 40 becomes mere isolation; with it, the aloneness is purposeful — it is the breath the provider takes between rounds of providing.

### Gate 9 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral focus gate, the engineering attention that lets life-force concentrate on small detail. In the design body of this cross, Gate 9 supplies the deliverer with the granular concentration that turns will into work. Gate 40 delivers; Gate 9 makes the deliverable.

### Gate 16 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat enthusiasm gate, the experimental voice. As Design Earth, Gate 16 supplies the willingness underneath the work — the artistic try-again energy that keeps the deliverance from becoming grim. The 9/16 axis in the design is the [[Channel 9/16]] of Identification, talent meeting concentration.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the Quarter of Bonding, governed by the Realm of Jupiter. Its purpose is the deep work of relationship — the polarities, the marriages, the bonded pairs and groups that form the social fabric. The Cross of Planning 3, sitting in this quarter, makes the lone deliverer's aloneness part of the bonding cycle. The Right Angle Cross of Planning 3 person needs to leave in order to come back, needs the recovery in order to be present in the bond. Ra named this cross's theme as the will to be alone — and in the bonding quarter, that will is paradoxically what makes the bond sustainable.

## The Angle Expression

The Right Angle is personal destiny: the cross is lived for the self, by the self. A Right Angle Cross of Planning 3 person is not here to teach the world about deliverance; they are here to deliver — to feel from the inside what the lone provider experiences, what the cost of the bargain is, what the recovery cycle requires. The destiny is intimate to the body.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle Cross of Planning 3 manifests in the six Right Angle profiles:
[[1/3]] | [[1/4]] | [[2/4]] | [[2/5]] | [[3/5]] | [[3/6]]

The 1/3 delivers for the family, then collides with the deliverance — discovering through trial and error which deliverances were honest. The 1/4 delivers within the network, becoming the lone provider to friends. The 2/4 is the natural deliverer whose retreats are part of how the network bonds around them. The 2/5 is projected onto as the savior, expected to deliver, and learns the cost of being recruited as the lone fixer. The 3/5 throws themselves into the deliverance and bumps against the limits of will. The 3/6 spends thirty years as the burdened deliverer before climbing onto the roof to discover what alone really means.

## Living This Cross

For the Right Angle Cross of Planning 3, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only way to know which deliverance is yours. Gate 40 is willpower, and willpower is fuel — but fuel only works when it is aimed by the body, not by the mind's idea of who needs to be fed. The shadow of this cross is the over-deliverer: the person who keeps providing past the point of recovery because the bargain feels owed, because the family asked, because saying no feels like abandonment. Eventually the stomach gives out. The deliverer's body crashes. And the bargain that was being protected dissolves anyway, because the provider was never living their own design.

Gate 40 healed is the bargain that recovers. Ra emphasized that Gate 40's authentic expression is "alone" — the genuine willingness to walk away, eat by yourself, sleep alone, work alone — and this aloneness is what makes the return into the bargain real. A Cross of Planning 3 person who allows themselves the alone time finds that Gate 37, the friendship below, holds steady. The family is not abandoned by the deliverer's retreat; the family is preserved by it.

The gift, when lived in alignment, is durable provision. These are the people whose bargains hold over decades because the deliverer learned to recover. They are the lone elders, the matriarchs and patriarchs who could be relied on for fifty years because they had Gate 40 honored — they could say no, take their solitude, and come back with fresh will. The Cross of Planning 3 person is not the planner-in-chief; they are the planner-in-residence, the will that quietly keeps the plan alive by knowing when to walk away from it.
