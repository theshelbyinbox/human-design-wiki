# Master List of the 64 Incarnation Crosses (Right Angle)

> Canonical reference for the 64 Right Angle Incarnation Crosses by lead (Personality Sun) gate, sourced from Ra Uru Hu's *Incarnation Crosses by Profile* (IHDS, Jovian Archive 2008).
>
> **Structure:** There are **16 unique Right Angle Cross names**, each manifesting in **4 quarters** of the wheel — yielding 64 distinct crosses. Each named cross uses the **same four gates** across its four quarter variants; the variants rotate which gate sits in the Personality Sun (lead) position. The four gates of any cross always form **two wheel-opposite axes** (the Personality Sun-Earth axis and the Design Sun-Earth axis).
>
> **The Vessel of Love** (gates 10/15/25/46) sits at the midpoint of every quarter — the heart of the wheel structure Ra describes.
>
> **Source files:**
> - `Incarnation Crosses (Ra Book) Quarter 1.md` (Q1: gates 13→24)
> - `Incarnation Crosses (Ra Book) Quarter 2.md` (Q2: gates 2→33)
> - `Incarnation Crosses (Ra Book) Quarter 3.md` (Q3: gates 7→44)
> - `Incarnation Crosses (Ra Book) Quarter 4.md` (Q4: gates 1, 43, 14 only — Q4 source file truncated; remaining Q4 gates verified from the combined `Incarnation Crosses by Profile- Ra Uru Hu.md` master file)

## The Four Quarters

| Quarter | Realm | Purpose | Theme | Opening Gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Realm of Alcyone | **Mind** | Quarter of Initiation | 13 (Fellowship of Man) |
| 2 | Realm of Duhbe | **Form** | Quarter of Civilization | 2 (The Receptive) |
| 3 | Realm of Jupiter | **Bonding** | Quarter of Duality | 7 (The Army) |
| 4 | Realm of (Mutation) | **Transformation** | Quarter of Mutation | 1 (The Creative) |

Each quarter is anchored by a Sphinx gate at its opening, holds a Vessel of Love at its midpoint, and closes with a gate from the Cross of the Four Ways.

## Verification Notes

- **64 crosses identified** (16 names × 4 quarter variants), confirmed from gate-section headers in all four Quarter files plus the consolidated master file.
- **Quarter 4 source file is truncated** (only gates 1, 43, 14 present in the standalone Q4 file). The remaining 13 Q4 crosses were verified from the consolidated `Incarnation Crosses by Profile- Ra Uru Hu.md` (combined TOC + chapter headers).
- **Spelling ambiguities flagged:**
  - "Maia" appears as a spelling variant in Q1; canonical spelling per Q2, Q3, Q4 and Index is **"Maya"**.
  - "Right Angel" (typo for Angle) appears in the Q4 entry for gate 26 (Rulership 4) in the consolidated master file.
- **Personality vs Design axis assignment:** Confirmed for Sphinx 1 (P=13/7, D=1/2) and Sphinx 2 (P=2/1, D=13/7) from direct source reading. The pattern holds: the lead gate's wheel-opposite axis is the Personality axis; the other axis is the Design axis. The lead (Personality Sun) gate is always the gate that opens the section in the source.
- **Design Sun vs Design Earth allocation within the Design axis** is not explicitly stated for every cross in the headers; in the canonical wheel calculation, Design Sun sits ~88° (≈15–16 hexagrams) earlier in zodiacal order than Personality Sun. For the article-writing phase, verify Design Sun/Earth direction against an authoritative HD wheel reference per cross.
- **Themes** are one-sentence distillations drawn from the chapter introductions and Ra's narrative framing for each cross; they are interpretive summaries, not direct quotes. Refine them against the source body when drafting full articles.
- **Vessel of Love midpoint structure verified** — each quarter contains a Vessel of Love cross at its midpoint (positions: Q1 gate 25, Q2 gate 15, Q3 gate 46, Q4 gate 10).
