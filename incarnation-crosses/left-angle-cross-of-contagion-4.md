# Left Angle Cross of Contagion 4

The Left Angle Cross of Contagion 4 is the transpersonal expression of the Contagion gate set in the Quarter of Mutation. In the closing quarter of the wheel, the Left Angle 2 variant of Contagion is the Left Angle Cross of Empowerment — the cross whose carriers are here to use their accumulated power skills to empower mutation in others.

The one-line theme: empowerment through transmission of power skills. The carrier is here to give others the resources, knowledge, and material substrate they need to mutate.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 8 — Holding Together
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 2 variant of Contagion 4 keeps the Personality axis 14–8 of the Right Angle but rotates the Design axis from the abstract pair (29–30) to the individual pair (55–59). The body is no longer fuelled by the abstract circuit's collective desire-engine; it is fuelled by individual mutating spirit and the genetic role gate of intimacy. The carrier's empowerment work is therefore not driven by what the collective experientially wants — it is driven by what individual mutating spirit demands.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 14 (Personality Sun) — Power Skills

The sacral fuel that retains and channels power. In the Left Angle, the carrier leads consciously with their accumulated power skills, but the leading is transpersonal — they are here to use their power to empower others, not to amass for themselves alone.

### Gate 8 (Personality Earth) — Holding Together

The throat voice of unique contribution. The 8 ensures the carrier's empowerment work is voiced — they speak the empowerment, teach the empowerment, model the empowerment. Their voice is the medium through which the power skills are transmitted.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The unconscious engine is the moody emotional pulse of individual mutating spirit. The body is animated by the wave of the 55. When spirit is present, the empowerment work flows; when spirit is absent, the work feels mechanical. The 55 keeps the Left Angle Empowerment carrier honest — they cannot fake the empowerment.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The body's intimate biology. The 59 ensures the empowerment work happens through intimate transmission — through mentorship, partnership, close working relationships, not through mass broadcast. The empowerment is body-to-body in a sense, even when mediated by voice.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Within this quarter, the Left Angle Empowerment serves a specific function: the transmission of accumulated power skills to the people who will carry the mutation forward. The mutation is not the carrier's own — they are not here to become the new vessel; they are here to empower the new vessel by giving it what they have accumulated.

This is the cross of the mentor, the funder, the patron, the teacher whose role is to hand the resources of mutation to the people positioned to carry it. The carrier's own mutation may have happened earlier in life or may not happen at all; their transpersonal function is to ensure that what they have can be passed to who needs it.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is shaped by what others need from the carrier. For Empowerment 4, the transpersonal pull is the people who need the carrier's accumulated power skills to mutate themselves.

Ra emphasised that this cross is one of the natural seats of mentorship and patronage. The carrier finds themselves drawn to younger or less-resourced people whose mutation is visible. The work of the life becomes investing in those people — financially, energetically, with attention and voice — and trusting that what is transmitted will be used.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who hoards their power, refusing to transmit, treating accumulation as the end rather than the substrate of empowerment. The not-self Empowerment 4 becomes the wealthy isolated figure whose resources never reach the mutation they were accumulated to serve. The correct expression is generous transmission — keys handed to those whose hands are open.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra discussed the 5/1 in this configuration as the heretic who teaches the universal lesson of power. The 5/2 holds the empowerment privately. The 6/2 Empowerment, when awake, becomes a deeply present mentor whose mere existence in the field of a younger person changes that person's possibilities. The 6/3 is the bridge to the closing right angle of the wheel.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept the transpersonal pull toward empowerment. The carrier of this Left Angle cannot privatise their power skills indefinitely — the design demands transmission.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] either hoards (refusing to transmit) or scatters (transmitting indiscriminately, exhausting themselves on recipients who do not respond to the transmission). Living from [[Signature]] means letting the body respond to which transmissions are correct — which mentees, partners, projects, and recipients are the right ones for the empowerment work at this stage of the life.

The deepest practice for this cross is letting the recipient's response guide the transmission. The carrier of Empowerment 4 cannot empower someone who is not in receptive position. They can only transmit to those whose bodies say yes. When the body of the receiver responds, the empowerment lands; when the body does not respond, no amount of pushing will land it. The carrier learns over time to wait for the visible response and then to transmit fully, trusting that what is given will fund the mutation it is meant to fund.

When delivered correctly, this cross produces some of the most generative elder figures in any community — the people whose long history of accumulated power skills becomes the heritable resource for the next mutation.
