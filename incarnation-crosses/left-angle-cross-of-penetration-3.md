# Left Angle Cross of Penetration 3

The Left Angle Cross of Penetration 3 — known in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of the Clarion 2 — is the transpersonal expression of the four-armed Cross of Penetration in the Quarter of Bonding. The same four gates — 51, 57, 53, 54 — are present, but the angle is Left, and the carrier's life is the announcing function of a karmic field of bonded relationship.

This is the second of the two Clarion crosses on the wheel. Where the Left Angle Clarion 1 announces in the Quarter of Initiation, the Left Angle Clarion 2 announces in the Quarter of Bonding. The penetration here is the gentle intuitive transmission that pierces the conditioning of intimate relationship — the bonded Clarion whose substance changes the people they are karmically tied to.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 57 — The Gentle / Intuition
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 51 — Shock / The Arousing
- **Design Sun:** Gate 53 — Development / Beginnings
- **Design Earth:** Gate 54 — The Marrying Maiden / Ambition
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The Personality axis 57↔51 carries the conscious intuitive transmission grounded in the heart-line ego. The Design axis 53↔54 carries the unconscious vehicle of starting cycles and tribal ambition.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 57 (Personality Sun) — The Gentle, Intuition

The 57 in the Left Angle is the gentle penetrating awareness as a karmic transmission. The Clarion 2 carrier consciously reads the bonded field and delivers the intuitive substance that the field requires. The gentle breeze that goes deep is the working tool of the cross.

### Gate 51 (Personality Earth) — Shock, The Arousing

Gate 51 grounds the gentle in the heart-line ego. The Clarion's intuition is given courage by the ego that survives shock. Without the 51, the 57 might read and never speak; with the 51, the gentle awareness is delivered.

### Gate 53 (Design Sun) — Development, Beginnings

The unconscious pressure to begin. The body's foundation is the starting engine. The intuitive transmission often arrives at the beginning of a karmic cycle.

### Gate 54 (Design Earth) — The Marrying Maiden, Ambition

The unconscious upward ambition. The vehicle's foundation is the desire to rise. The Clarion's transmission carries upward energy beneath the conscious gentleness.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Bonding is the realm of duality. The Left Angle Clarion 2 contributes to bonding by being the announcing voice of the karmic intimate field. The carrier's life is the delivery of the intuitive transmission that the bonded relationships of their karmic field require in order to function correctly.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The Clarion 2 carrier does not own the transmission; they deliver it. Ra was clear that the Left Angle Penetration brings substance, not style. The Clarion 2 carrier's intuitive readings are not lifestyle suggestions; they are the substance that the bonded field actually needs.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who has overridden the gentle splenic voice with the mind and now tries to manufacture intuitive readings through mental analysis. The transmission becomes performance. The bonded others sense that the substance is not real, and the relationships suffer.

The correct expression is the opposite: the carrier trusts the gentle, delivers what is given, and allows the karmic field of bonded relationship to receive what the splenic awareness has read in the now.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left Angle Crosses manifest through 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3.

- **4/6** carries the externalizing Clarion whose transmission flows through the bonded network
- **5/1** carries the universalizing Clarion with foundational study underneath the transmission
- **5/2** carries the heretical hermit Clarion — natural withdrawal meets called transmission
- **6/2** carries the matured hermit Clarion across three life phases
- **6/3** carries the role-model experimenter Clarion whose trial-and-error becomes the wisdom of an elder

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation. The 57 speaks in the now, once. The mind that argues with the splenic voice loses access to the awareness it was given. For the Clarion 2 carrier, trusting the gentle is the central spiritual discipline of the cross.

The signature of the cross lived correctly is bonded relationships that operate on real awareness. The signature of the not-self is the carrier whose intuitive transmissions have been overridden by the mind and whose bonded field has been managed through mental analysis with predictable confusion. The work is to let the gentle penetrate.

When the carrier honors the design, they become what bonding requires: the announcing intuitive voice of the karmic intimate field. The 57 reads the present. The 51 delivers what is read. The 53 begins what the reading initiates. The 54 lifts the bonded field upward through the transmission. Lived correctly, Left Angle Penetration 3 is the cross of the intimate Clarion — the one whose gentle penetration changes the bonded relationships of their karmic life.

## The Substance of Bonded Initiation

Ra Uru Hu drew the distinction between Right Angle style and Left Angle substance with particular force in the Penetration cross. The Right Angle Penetration 3 carrier penetrates with intuitive style — a flavor, a quality, a moment of recognition that leaves an impression. The Left Angle Clarion 2 carrier delivers the substance of the intuitive transmission itself. The bonded other receives not a hint but the real thing.

This makes the Left Angle Clarion 2 a configuration of considerable karmic weight. The intimate field the carrier belongs to is structurally dependent on the carrier's transmission being real. When the transmission is real, the field flourishes. When the transmission is suppressed or manufactured, the field suffers. The carrier's responsibility is therefore to wait for the splenic voice and deliver only what it actually says.
