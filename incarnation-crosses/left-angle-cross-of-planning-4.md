# Left Angle Cross of Identification 2

The Left Angle Cross of Identification 2 is the transpersonal Q4 variant of the Cross of Planning. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 9]] (Focus) and Personality Earth in [[Gate 16]] (Enthusiasm), the cross leads with focus as the identifier — and lives that focus on behalf of others, in the mutation quarter where the larger Planning era is ending. The Design Sun in [[Gate 40]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 37]] place the lone will and the bargain beneath the surface as design context. Where Identification 1 leads with enthusiasm being recognized, Identification 2 leads with focus being recognized — the person known not for what they are willing to try but for what they have actually concentrated on.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 9 — The Taming Power of the Small (Focus)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Design Sun:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose: Transformation)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — transpersonal karma
- **Axis A (Personality):** 9 ↔ 16
- **Axis B (Design):** 37 ↔ 40

## The Four Gates

### Gate 9 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral focus, the engineering capacity for sustained attention. As the lead of the Left Angle Cross of Identification 2, Gate 9 makes focused work itself the public identifier. People recognize this person by what they have patiently concentrated on.

### Gate 16 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat enthusiasm, the willingness to try. Beneath the focus, Gate 16 supplies the visible practiced art — the artistic surface that the deep concentration is converted into.

### Gate 40 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 40]] is the will of deliverance. In the design body, Gate 40 supplies the lone strength the focused person needs to keep the work going past the point where others would quit.

### Gate 37 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain. Beneath the surface, Gate 37 grounds the focused life in a community that can receive what the focus produces.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the Quarter of Transformation. Its purpose is breakthrough — ending one cycle and seeding the next. The Left Angle Cross of Identification 2, sitting in this quarter, makes the identification a mutative act. The carrier is recognized for a concentration that bridges eras. As the 400-year Planning cycle closes, Identification 2 people are the focused specialists whose work survives the transition because the discipline itself is portable.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle crosses are transpersonal. The Identification 2 person is identified not for personal reasons but because the community needs the focus they have done. The karma is collective — the focused work is being delivered to others, often to a new collective that did not even exist when the focus began.

## Living This Cross

For the Left Angle Cross of Identification 2, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] keep the focus pointed at what is actually theirs to concentrate on. The shadow of this cross is the focused worker who concentrates obsessively on something the body never asked them to do, because the identification it produces has become more important than the truth of the work. The mind drives Gate 9 into a kind of intellectual capture, and years of focus go into something that does not feed the body.

The healthy expression is the deeply focused contributor whose work outlives the moment because the focus was sound. The Cross of Identification 2 person, in correctness, is the specialist whose body led them to their subject. The community recognizes them because the work is real, not because the work was performed for recognition.

Ra spoke about the closing of the Planning cycle as a passage rather than a loss. The Left Angle Cross of Identification 2 person is particularly suited to this passage, because focus is one of the things that survives the mutation. New cycles need patient attention. The Identification 2 person carries that attention forward, often without knowing they are doing so. They simply concentrate. They are simply recognized for the concentration. And the new community that forms in the next era will identify them as one of the bridges.

The gift, lived in alignment, is the gift of inheritable discipline. Identification 2 people leave behind work that the next generation can pick up. The bargain underneath (Gate 37) is the community that receives the inheritance; the deliverance underneath (Gate 40) is the lone strength that let the work be completed. When all four gates are honored, the Identification 2 person delivers the focused contribution and then withdraws — and the community that identifies them continues to find use for the work long after the person is gone.

This cross asks its carriers to be patient with being identified. Identification 2 can feel slow. The recognition is gradual. The body holds the focus for years before the community sees what is being concentrated on. The mind wants to be identified faster, more loudly, with more clarity — and that pressure leads to the shadow. Trusting the slow build of authentic identification is the discipline.
