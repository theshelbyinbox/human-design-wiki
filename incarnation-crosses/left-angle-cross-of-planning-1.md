# Left Angle Cross of Migration 1

The Left Angle Cross of Migration 1 is the transpersonal variant of the Cross of Planning anchored in the Quarter of Initiation, carrying the same four gates ([[Gate 37]], [[Gate 40]], [[Gate 9]], [[Gate 16]]) but reorganized into a karmic destiny that is lived for and with others rather than for the self. The lead gate, Personality Sun in Gate 37, still names the bargain — but the bargain in this Left Angle expression is not a personal household. It is a migration: the leaving of one community to find or build another. Ra named this cross Migration because its theme is the breaking of the bargain in one place and the re-establishment of it somewhere new. Left Angle people are not here to live for their own family. They are here to move, to relocate, to carry the bargain across borders and into populations that have not yet learned how to live it.

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Design Sun:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm

- **Angle:** Left Angle — transpersonal karma, life lived with and for others
- **Axis A (Personality):** 37 ↔ 40
- **Axis B (Design):** 9 ↔ 16

## The Four Gates

### Gate 37 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain — the gate of friendship and family touch. In the Left Angle Cross of Migration 1, Gate 37 is the bargain that is being moved. The person is here to leave one bargain and find or make another. The mouth of the tribe is portable.

### Gate 40 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 40]] is the stomach, the will of deliverance. Beneath the leading bargain, Gate 40 supplies the lone strength that makes migration possible. To leave a community, one must be capable of being alone — and Gate 40 is the engine of that capacity.

### Gate 16 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat gate of enthusiasm, the experimental willingness to try. In the design body, Gate 16 supplies the skill and the artistic optimism that makes the migrant viable in the new place. The new bargain must be tried into existence.

### Gate 9 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral focus, the engineering attention. Beneath the surface, Gate 9 provides the patient concentration the migrant needs to make a life in unfamiliar territory.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation is the Quarter of Mind, governed by the Realm of Alcyone. It is where new direction is mentally initiated. The Left Angle Cross of Migration 1, sitting in this quarter, frames migration as a mental initiation — the moving is a thought-form that opens new community. The person migrates not because the body cannot live where it is, but because the mind has registered that the bargain in the original place will not hold.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle crosses are transpersonal. The life is not a personal karma lived for the self; it is a karma lived with others. The Left Angle Cross of Migration 1 person is not here to settle into one family; they are here to keep moving, to disturb the bargain wherever they find it, to ask of a community: is this bargain still real? Ra described Migration in part by referring to the founding of America by religious migrants who could not fit their bargain in their place of origin. Migration people are not at home in the original bargain. They are at home in the moving of bargains.

The 5/1 of this cross is the practical migrant — the one called on by others to bring a workable bargain to a new place, the missionary or the foundational settler. The 5/2 is the projected leader of migration, recruited to relocate a community whether they planned to or not. The 6/2 is the role-model migrant, the natural-born hermit-in-motion who eventually exemplifies how to move from one bargain to another. The 6/3 spends thirty years migrating through trial and error, then ascends to the roof from which they observe the migration patterns of others. The 4/6 holds the network through the migration, carrying the friendship while the move is happening.

## Living This Cross

For the Left Angle Cross of Migration 1, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are how the moving stays honest. The shadow of this cross is the perpetual migrant — the person who never settles because the body has been outrun by the mind's restlessness. Real migration is body-led. The body responds, the move happens, and a new bargain forms in the new place. Mind-led migration is dislocation; body-led migration is the founding of new community.

Ra spoke about this cross with particular tenderness toward the leaving and the coming-to. He noted that the breakdown of institutions toward the end of the Planning cycle is itself a migration — civilization is moving from one bargain to another, and the Migration 1 person is, in a real sense, the human carrier of that institutional shift. They are not destroying the bargain; they are relocating it. They are the people who can step out of one community and step into another without losing the thread of what a bargain is.

The gift, when lived in alignment, is the renewal of community. Migration 1 people refresh the bargain by moving it. They reveal what the bargain is by removing it from the original place and trying it somewhere else. Without them, the bargain calcifies — the community becomes the institution becomes the dead weight that cannot adapt. With them, the bargain stays alive, because someone is always asking whether it is still real in this new place.

This cross also asks its carriers to grieve. To migrate is to leave. The Left Angle Cross of Migration 1 person carries the loss of the original family forward into the new bargain — and that loss is not pathology; it is the cost of the assignment. Ra noted that Left Angles often feel out of place in their original families and this is by design. They are not here to belong to the family of origin. They are here to find or make the family of destination.
