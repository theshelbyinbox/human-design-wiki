# Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 3

The Left Angle Cross of the Vessel of Love 3 is named in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Healing 2. The four Vessel of Love gates — 46, 25, 15, 10 — rotate into a Left Angle configuration in the Quarter of Duality, producing a transpersonal life whose karmic work is somatic healing. Where the Right Angle of this cross is the personal journey of body-love, the Left Angle is the carrier's body as a healing medium for others. The Healing 2 carrier is here to be present in their flesh in a way that lets the bodies of the people around them remember how to come back into balance.

This is the second Healing cross in Ra's teaching. The first, in Quarter 1, healed innocence at the mental level. The second, in Quarter 3, heals the body at the somatic level. Both are transpersonal expressions of universal love, but the Q3 variation is grounded in flesh.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 46 — Pushing Upward / Determination of the Body
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 25 — Innocence
- **Design Sun:** Gate 15 — Modesty
- **Design Earth:** Gate 10 — Treading
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose of Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The same four gates as the Right Angle Vessel of Love 3 but lived through a transpersonal angle. The carrier's life will not make sense as a personal story. It makes sense as the bodies that recovered because the carrier was near them.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 46 (Personality Sun) — Pushing Upward

The body as the conscious instrument of healing. For the Healing 2 carrier, the 46 is the physical form through which the universal love of the Vessel flows outward into the bodies of others. Touch, presence, proximity — these become the carrier's working tools, often without their conscious knowledge. The body of the carrier is the healing field.

### Gate 25 (Personality Earth) — Innocence

Universal love as the conscious ground of the healing. The 25 ensures that the somatic healing is not mixed with the carrier's personal needs. The Healing 2 heals because love moves through them, not because they need to be needed. The 25 protects the work from becoming codependent.

### Gate 15 (Design Sun) — Modesty

The wide aura beneath the conscious work. The 15 is what allows the carrier's healing field to include diverse bodies — diverse rhythms, diverse damages, diverse cultures of the flesh. The carrier does not specialise in one kind of body. Their aura is wide enough to include any.

### Gate 10 (Design Earth) — Treading

Behavior of the self as the underlying medium. The carrier's healing is delivered through their own conduct in their own body. They do not need to perform healer. They need to behave correctly with their own flesh, and the people around them register that conduct as a permission to recover.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the realm of bonding. Its purpose is the meeting of self and other. The Healing 2 carrier contributes to that bonding work by being the body that other bodies can safely bond with. Their flesh is the safe meeting-point. People come to the carrier — often through intimacy, friendship, or proximity — and find that their own body, in the carrier's presence, remembers how to be at home.

Ra was clear that Quarter 3 is the most relational of the four quarters. The Healing 2 lives that relationality as the central feature of life. The carrier's bonded relationships are the medium of the work.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The life is not the carrier's own. The work is publicising — making available to the collective what the Right Angles have lived through. The Healing 2 publicises somatic healing: the carrier walks among the wounded bodies and lets the bodies remember themselves.

The not-self distortion of this cross is the carrier who tries to be a professional healer in the way the mind can manage — credentialing themselves, marketing themselves, setting up the practice the way the homogenised culture wants healers to be set up. The body's design is different. The healing happens through ordinary intimacy. The carrier's lovers, friends, family, and acquaintances are the ones who heal. The work cannot be turned into a transaction without losing its medicine.

Ra called Left Angles karmic lives. The Healing 2 carrier will meet, again and again, the wounded bodies that match their own healing field. Each encounter is karmic. Each bonding is preordained. The work is to be present in the body, available to the intimacy, and undefended against the love that wants to move through.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle of this cross is carried by five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2 and 6/3.

- **4/6** is the externalising friend whose healing flows through the friendship network — friends and the friends of friends are the bodies that recover
- **5/1** is the heretic with foundation, the universalising body-healer whose interventions actually work because they are grounded in their own 1st line study of the body
- **5/2** is the heretic hermit, projected upon as a saviour of the body, most effective when allowed to remain selective about whom they let close
- **6/2** is the role model emerging from the rooftop, holistic body-healing as a life pattern — the elder whose presence shifts bodies without any intervention at all
- **6/3** is the role model who moved through trial and error in their own body and is now demonstrating that recovery is possible

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are non-negotiable. A Left Angle carrier who lives by mind quickly becomes a missionary for body-wisdom that has nothing to do with the actual healing field. The work is to be the body, in the room, with the people whose bodies are calling, and to let the field do what the field does.

The signature of this cross lived well is bodies around the carrier that feel safer, more themselves, more at home in their own flesh. The not-self signature is exhaustion — the sense that the carrier is collecting other people's somatic distress and absorbing it into their own body. The recovery is to come back to [[Strategy]] and let the design's natural protections do their work.

When the carrier honours this cross, they become what Ra implied was one of the great gentle healers of the wheel — the one whose work is mostly invisible because it happens through ordinary intimacy. They do not need to advertise. The right bodies find them.

The carrier should be warned that this cross is particularly vulnerable to the spiritual ego. The mind notices what is happening — bodies recovering, intimacies opening, lives shifting in the carrier's presence — and wants to take credit. The correction is to remember that the body is the instrument and the love is the agent. The carrier is the host, not the source.

This is the cross of the somatic healer, the embodied lover, the body whose presence is medicine. Lived correctly, it is a quietly intimate life. Lived incorrectly, it is an exhausted performance of healership. The difference is whether the carrier remains in their own body or tries to live in everyone else's.
