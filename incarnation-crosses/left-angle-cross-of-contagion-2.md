# Left Angle Cross of Contagion 2

The Left Angle Cross of Contagion 2 is the transpersonal expression of the Contagion gate set in the Quarter of Civilization. Ra Uru Hu named this configuration the Left Angle Cross of Uncertainty 1 — a name that points directly at its mechanics. Where the Right Angle Contagion 2 carrier contributes form to civilisation, the Left Angle Uncertainty carrier serves the larger principle that form-building is inherently uncertain. They are here to commit humanity to projects whose outcome cannot be known.

The one-line theme: uncertainty as the engine of civilisation. The carrier is here to mobilise others into long-term form-building whose results cannot be predicted but whose process binds humanity together.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 8 — Holding Together (Contribution)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 14 — Possession in Great Measure
- **Design Sun:** Gate 55 — Spirit / Abundance
- **Design Earth:** Gate 59 — Dispersion
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Left Angle 1 of Contagion 2 keeps the Personality axis 8–14 of the Right Angle but rotates the Design axis to 55–59, replacing the abstract emotional fire (30–29) with the individual emotional pulse (55–59). This is a crucial mechanical shift. The carrier's body is no longer fuelled by the collective experiential desire-fire; it is fuelled by individual mutative spirit. The contribution they make to civilisation is therefore not driven by what people want — it is driven by what spirit demands.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 8 (Personality Sun) — Holding Together

The throat voice of unique contribution. In the Left Angle, the carrier's conscious purpose is still to contribute — but the contribution is transpersonal. They are not building for themselves; they are building for the spirit of humanity. Ra spoke of monuments, pyramids, scientific projects, public works — things built by populations whose individual lives end long before the project is finished.

### Gate 14 (Personality Earth) — Power Skills

The sacral fuel that sustains the long project. The carrier's body is equipped to work for years, decades, lifetimes on what others would abandon. The 14's lifelong-development quality makes the Left Angle Uncertainty carrier the natural builder of slow-rising monuments.

### Gate 55 (Design Sun) — Spirit / Abundance

The unconscious engine of mutative spirit. Beneath the voice and the power, the body is animated by the moodiness of the 55 — the uncertainty about whether spirit is present, the longing for fullness, the creative pulse. This is what makes the Left Angle Uncertainty carrier a heretic: they cannot build to the tribal plan because the tribal plan does not address the spirit they feel.

### Gate 59 (Design Earth) — Dispersion

The body's intimate biology. The 59 grounds the work in the bonded relationships through which the long project is built. Pyramids are not built by individuals; they are built by communities bound together by sex, marriage, kinship, friendship. The 59 ensures the Left Angle Uncertainty carrier can form and hold the bonds needed for the lifelong project.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the realm of form. The form-building work of civilisation requires two distinct forces: the contagion that makes form irresistible (Right Angle Contagion 2) and the uncertainty that mobilises form-building despite its unpredictability (Left Angle Uncertainty 1). Ra emphasised that humanity loves uncertainty — loves the great projects whose outcome is unknown — because uncertainty bonds the participants together in a way that certainty cannot. The cathedral that takes three generations to build creates a tribal spirit that no completed work can match.

The Left Angle Uncertainty carrier is the cross of those great projects. They are the architects and visionaries who can sell humanity on the moonshot, the pyramid, the cathedral, the scientific endeavour whose results no one can guarantee.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is transpersonal karma. The life is for others. For Uncertainty 1, the transpersonal pull is the mobilising of long-term form-building. The carrier is here to commit communities to projects that cannot be guaranteed. Ra used the example of the moon landing, the Barcelona Olympics, the great pyramids — projects whose initiation required someone to convince a community that the uncertainty was worth carrying.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who refuses the long project, who insists on immediate results, who treats uncertainty as failure. The same configuration that makes the awake Uncertainty 1 a builder of monuments makes the asleep Uncertainty 1 a chronic non-finisher, abandoning each project as soon as the outcome becomes uncertain.

Ra also pointed out that the 6th line carriers of this cross often disengage from the project itself and live in the now. They build, but they refuse to attach to the result. This is the awake 6 expression of Uncertainty: do the work, hold the spirit, refuse to be entranced by what may or may not come.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Uncertainty 1 manifests in five profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. Ra described the 5/1 Uncertainty as the heretic who sells the universal project — the visionary who can mobilise a population. The 5/2 carries a private vision that rarely concretises into the form it dreams of. The 6/2 Uncertainty, when awake, embodies "shut up and lift the stone" — present in the work without attachment to its outcome. The 6/3 is the bridge to the next Right Angle of the wheel.

## Living This Cross

To live this cross correctly is to honour [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] and to accept that the work is long. The carrier of Uncertainty 1 cannot get a quick result; the work of the life requires years to develop. Living from [[Strategy]] means waiting for the correct invitations, recognitions, or responses before committing to the long project — and once committed, sustaining the commitment without demanding premature certainty.

A carrier living from [[Not-Self]] either chases short-term wins (the wrong work for the design) or commits to long projects that the body did not respond to (the right kind of work but the wrong specific project). Living from [[Signature]] means letting the body select which long project is correct, then giving it the years it requires.

The deepest practice for this cross is treating uncertainty as the design rather than as the obstacle. The Left Angle Uncertainty carrier does not get to know whether the cathedral will be finished. They get to know whether to lay the stone today. The lifelong work is to lay the stones the body responds to laying, and to leave the outcome to the longer time-scale the work belongs to.
