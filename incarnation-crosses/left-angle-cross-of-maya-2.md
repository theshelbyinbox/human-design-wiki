# Left Angle Cross of Maya 2

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 2 — known in Ra Uru Hu's source teaching as the Left Angle Cross of Obscuration 1 — is the transpersonal-karmic variant of the Maya cross in Quarter 2. The Personality Sun sits in [[Gate 62]] (Preponderance of the Small) at the higher-trigram lines (5 or 6), which shifts the cross out of personal destiny and into a life oriented toward others. In Quarter 2 the cross expresses itself through Form — the precise naming of details that builds civilization — and in the Left Angle this naming-work becomes transpersonal. The carrier is here to meet specific people whose lives need the carrier's precision and whose own lives, in turn, become part of the carrier's purpose.

## Mechanics

- Personality Sun: [[Gate 62]] (Preponderance of the Small / Detail), Lines 5 or 6
- Personality Earth: [[Gate 61]] (Inner Truth), Lines 5 or 6
- Design Sun: [[Gate 42]] or [[Gate 32]], Lines 2 or 3
- Design Earth: the wheel-opposite of the Design Sun
- Personality Axis: 62 / 61
- Design Axis: 42 / 32
- Quarter: Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe)
- Realm Purpose: Form
- Mystical Theme: From Womb to Room
- Angle Expression: Left Angle — transpersonal karma

## The Four Gates

### Gate 62 (Personality Sun)

[[Gate 62]] in the Left Angle is the detail-naming faculty turned outward. At line 5 the gate carries Metamorphosis — the projection field that makes the carrier's precise communication sought in moments of collective confusion. At line 6 the gate carries Self-Discipline — the mature elder whose precision has become economical, who has learned which details are worth attention and which are not. In both higher lines the carrier's naming-work is karmically routed toward specific others.

The name "Obscuration" given to the Left Angle of this cross signals something subtle: the Maya 2 Left Angle carrier sees the detail others miss, which means they are constantly aware of what is being obscured by the agreed-upon names. The carrier names the obscure thing; or, just as often, the carrier sees how the named thing is itself a piece of obscuration, hiding what the name was supposed to reveal.

### Gate 61 (Personality Earth)

[[Gate 61]] at line 5 is Influence — the practical projection of mystic mental work. At line 6 it is the Bodhisattva of Truth, the mature withdrawn expression. The Earth grounds the Sun, so the carrier's outward-facing detail-work is rooted in a transpersonal mysticism — a quiet mandate to see through the names to the mystery they sit inside.

### Gate 42 (Design)

[[Gate 42]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the sacral pulse of cycle-completion into the body. Line 2 carries Identification — the natural pull toward specific cycles that have chosen the carrier. Line 3 carries Self-Service — the experimental willingness to walk away from cycles that have revealed themselves to be wrong. The design 42 makes sure the body has the sustained life-force to stay inside the transpersonal work long enough for it to mature.

### Gate 32 (Design)

[[Gate 32]] in the design imprint at lines 2 or 3 brings the splenic continuity-judgment into the body. The carrier's vehicle has a built-in instinct about which encounters carry long-term value. In Left Angle Maya 2, this instinct filters the karmic encounters: which people are this carrier really here to meet, and which are passing through with no karmic load attached.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization channels purpose through Form. The Maya 2 cross is the cross of the namer; the Left Angle variant routes the naming-work into transpersonal karma. The mystical theme of Quarter 2 is From Womb to Room — the movement from hidden potential to materially expressed life. In the Left Angle Maya 2, this movement happens through specific encounters: the carrier names what needs to be named for the people their karma routes them toward, and those people's lives are concretized by the precise naming.

The shadow expression of this in the Left Angle is Obscuration — the carrier whose precision is conscripted to obscure rather than reveal, who names things in ways that hide what they were supposed to make visible. The work of the cross is to keep the precise naming honest.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal karma. The Maya 2 Left Angle person is not here primarily for themselves. They are here to meet specific others, complete specific karmic exchanges through the field of precise naming, and have their own life shaped, in turn, by those encounters. Ra Uru Hu was clear that Left Angle life cannot be planned in advance; it unfolds through the relationships and encounters that the karmic field arranges.

This makes [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] especially important for the Left Angle carrier. The mind does not know who the karmic-encounters will be; only the body's response and authority can recognize the right ones as they arrive.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Left Angle Cross of Maya 2 manifests in the transpersonal profiles: 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3. The 4/6 brings opportunity-based connection that matures through three life-phases. The 5/1 brings projected universal teaching grounded in deep foundational study — the heretic-investigator. The 5/2 brings projected universal influence offered from a hermit base. The 6/2 brings role-model life lived in alternation between hermit privacy and public example. The 6/3 brings the experimental first life-half that distills into role-model wisdom in the third phase.

## Living This Cross

The Left Angle Maya 2 life is organized around encounters in which precise naming changes something. The carrier walks into a room and notices the specific detail no one else has named. Speaking that detail at the right moment to the right person — invited, requested, in correct timing — can reorganize an entire situation. Speaking it uninvited, or to the wrong audience, produces the Obscuration shadow: the detail becomes a way of hiding rather than revealing.

The 62 line 5 fifth-line projection makes the carrier particularly vulnerable to being conscripted. Others will project onto the Maya 2 Left Angle person the role of "the one who has the answer" and demand precision on schedules that are not the carrier's own. The work is to honor the [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] that filter which projections to respond to and which to let pass.

The 61 Earth keeps the carrier humble about the larger pattern. The detail is for the karmic encounter, but the mystery is always larger than the names. Left Angle Maya 2 carriers who forget this become the brittle precise teacher whose work has become a brand; the ones who remember it become trusted elders whose precise naming has weight because it is offered with respect for what the names cannot reach.

The deeper teaching is that civilization is built out of meaningful encounters in which precise naming reorganizes a piece of reality. The Maya 2 Left Angle carrier is one of the karmic agents of this process. Their life is not their own in the Right Angle sense; the encounters are the life, and the work is to be present for them.

In family, work, and community, the Left Angle Maya 2 person is often experienced as the one who can finally name what no one else has been able to articulate. The relief their precision produces in others is itself part of the karmic exchange — the encounter that needed to happen, the name that needed to be spoken, the obscured thing finally made plain. Their own growth, in turn, is shaped by the people their precision was offered to.
