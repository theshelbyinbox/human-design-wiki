# Right Angle Cross of the Unexpected 4

The Right Angle Cross of the Unexpected 4 is the cross of the starting code. The Personality Sun sits in Gate 41, the gate of Decrease and Contraction, and the carrier opens the Quarter of Mutation with a life shaped around the seeding of new experiential direction. Every January the Rave New Year begins on Gate 41. This cross is the human carrier of that seasonal contraction — the body through whom the next round of human feeling is initiated.

Ra Uru Hu described the 41 as the single initiating codon in the genetic structure, the gate from which the entire abstract experiential cycle is born. The Unexpected 4 carrier holds that initiation as a personal destiny. Their life begins something. They will not always know what they are beginning, and the something they begin will rarely be what they planned to begin. That is the unexpected dimension of the cross.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 41 — Decrease / Contraction
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 31 — Influence
- **Design Sun:** Gate 28 — Preponderance of the Great
- **Design Earth:** Gate 27 — Nourishment / Caring
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Right Angle — Personal Destiny

The Personality axis 41–31 is the consciousness: the contracted starting pressure paired with the projected leadership voice. The Design axis 28–27 is the body's vehicle: the splenic Game Player tethered to the tribal caring gate. The cross sits at the seed of the mutation quarter — the place where the wheel restarts.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 41 (Personality Sun) — Decrease

The conscious purpose. Gate 41 is the contracted root pressure from which all human experience begins. It is fantasy under pressure — the longing for the next experience, distilled into a single point of focus. As the lead of this cross it gives the carrier a life-long position at the starting line of the abstract cycle. The carrier's mind is generating the next round of human feeling whether anyone has asked for it or not.

### Gate 31 (Personality Earth) — Influence

The ground the seed pressure stands on. Gate 31 is the projected voice of leadership. As the Earth of this cross it grounds the seed in a public voice. The contraction of the 41 becomes the words of the 31, and the words become the influence by which the new direction spreads. Without the 31 the 41 stays private; with it the seed is announced.

### Gate 28 (Design Sun) — The Game Player

The unconscious splenic engine. Gate 28 is the awareness that purpose is worth dying for. The body of this carrier is built around the willingness to risk for the seed it carries. The 28 is what gives the 41 the courage to actually initiate, even when the carrier has no idea what will be initiated.

### Gate 27 (Design Earth) — Nourishment

The unconscious tribal ground. Gate 27 is the sacral gate of caring. Beneath the conscious starting code, the body is built to feed what it begins. The 27 ensures that the seed has something to grow on — that the new direction has the material support to actually mature.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. Its purpose is to break the patterns of what was and to seed the patterns of what will be. Gate 41 opens this quarter at the level of feeling — the contracted starting pressure that ignites the next round. The Unexpected 4 carrier contributes to the mutation quarter by being the body through which the new feeling enters the species. Whether the mutation will be small or planetary, the cross holds the position of seed.

## The Angle Expression

A Right Angle Cross is a personal destiny. The Unexpected 4 carrier is not here to seed someone else's mutation; they are here to live their own life under the condition that the life itself keeps initiating new directions. Ra was emphatic about the relationship between Gate 41 and mutation: the gate is the starting code, and the starting code does not know what it starts. The carrier's job is not to plan the mutation but to live the contraction honestly.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who tries to direct the mutation, who attaches to a particular outcome for the seed, or who refuses the starting pressure because it is uncomfortable. The correct expression carries the contraction as a sacred pressure, lets the 31 voice it when the voice is genuinely called for, and trusts that the unexpected is the mechanism by which the seed finds its ground.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The Right Angle is carried by the six classical right-angle profiles: 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, and 3/6.

- **1/3** the investigator-seeder who tests new directions through trial and error
- **1/4** the investigator-seeder whose initiations are carried into a stable network of friends
- **2/4** the natural hermit through whom new patterns enter via known relationships
- **2/5** the called hermit-seeder projected onto by the collective as the source of the next direction
- **3/5** the trial-and-error mutator whose seeds emerge from the wreckage of past attempts
- **3/6** the role-model seeder passing through three life phases of increasingly clear initiation

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only reliable navigation system. A Generator with this cross responds with the sacral to which seeds are theirs to plant; a Projector waits for the invitation before voicing the seed; a Manifestor informs before initiating the new direction; a Reflector lets a lunar cycle reveal whether the seed is correct in this environment.

The signature of the cross lived well is a life that keeps beginning. The not-self signature is the frustration of the carrier who tried to stabilise the seed pressure and ended up sitting on a contraction that could not be released. The work is to let the 41 contract, let the 31 voice when voicing is correct, let the 28 risk what risk is required, and let the 27 feed what the seed becomes.

When the carrier honours the design, they become the person around whom the next chapter starts — not because they planned it, but because the unexpected itself selected them as the body in which the starting code would live this round.
