# Left Angle Cross of Separation 1

The Left Angle Cross of Separation 1 is the transpersonal Q2 variant of the Cross of Consciousness. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 35]] (Progress) and Personality Earth in [[Gate 5]] (Waiting), this Left Angle cross places hunger and rhythm into a karmic role lived for others — but the role is one of separation. Ra Uru Hu named this cross "Separation" because the carrier's hunger for progress repeatedly moves them away from what they have done before. They are the ones who keep stepping out — out of relationships, out of contexts, out of completed experiences — and the separation is the gift to the collective.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 35 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 35]] is the Throat gate of hunger for experience. As the lead of the Left Angle Cross of Separation 1, Gate 35 makes the appetite a transpersonal force — the carrier's hunger separates them from settled situations because the next experience is calling.

### Gate 5 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 5]] is the Sacral gate of fixed rhythms. Beneath the surface, Gate 5 supplies the cyclical patience that gives the separations their timing — they do not happen randomly but in rhythm.

### Gate 63 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 63]] is the Head doubt. In the design body, Gate 63 supplies the questioning that justifies each separation — the inquiry that says "this is no longer what I am here to do."

### Gate 64 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 64]] is the Head confusion. Beneath the surface, Gate 64 carries the unfinished material from the separations — the relationships left, the experiences uncompleted, the conversations cut short.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the Quarter of Form. The Left Angle Cross of Separation 1, sitting in this quarter, makes separation a civilizational act. Civilization grows in part because of carriers who keep separating from the old form to seek the new one. The Separation 1 person is not a quitter; they are the natural progress-seeker whose departures keep civilization in motion. Without them, form calcifies; with them, the culture keeps producing new edges.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle crosses are transpersonal. The Separation 1 carrier lives the separations on behalf of others. Their leavings are not private failures of commitment; they are collective demonstrations that the field is still moving. Often the people they leave behind eventually understand why the carrier had to go — and the separation, in retrospect, becomes part of how the community grows.

## Living This Cross

For the Left Angle Cross of Separation 1, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] determine which separations are honest and which are mind-driven flight. The shadow is the carrier who leaves at the first discomfort, who confuses Gate 35's hunger with the mind's restlessness. Real separations are body-led: the body has finished with a context, and the next hunger is genuine. Mind-led separations are the perpetual quitter who never lets anything mature.

The healthy expression is the carrier who separates in rhythm. Gate 5 underneath gives the leavings their timing — the body knows when the cycle has completed. The Separation 1 person, in correctness, leaves cleanly. There is grief, often profound, but no thrashing. The carrier knows the leaving is theirs and the next chapter is calling.

Ra spoke of this cross with reference to the difficulty of intimacy for Left Angle Consciousness carriers — the same hunger that makes them progress also makes them leave the people they love. This is not pathology; it is the design. The carrier's life is not a settled life. It is a series of arcs, each one beginning, ripening, ending, and the next one starting. Communities that try to hold the carrier in one place will find themselves grieving; communities that let the carrier go and welcome them back will find themselves enriched by what the carrier brought from elsewhere.

The gift, lived in alignment, is the integration of varied experience. Separation 1 people return with stories, with skills, with insights from contexts they alone visited. They are the natural bridges between worlds. The civilizations they leave behind benefit from what they bring back, even when the leaving itself was painful.

The danger is bitterness on both sides. The carrier can become hardened by the repeated leavings — the body becomes used to not staying. The community can become resentful of the carrier's departures. The remedy is honesty: leaving when the body says so, returning when the body says so, and refusing to apologize for either. Strategy and Authority make this honesty livable.

The Separation 1 carrier also lives at an interesting tension with the Quarter of Civilization. The quarter wants form, settledness, established culture — and the Separation carrier keeps leaving the form behind to seek the next experience. Their departures are part of how the quarter civilizes itself, because each separation reveals what was actually civilization and what was merely habit. The carrier's leaving tests the bonds. The bonds that survive the leaving are the real ones. This is a service the community may not recognize until decades later, when they see which bonds held and which ones were always going to break.
