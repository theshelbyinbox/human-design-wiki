# Left Angle Cross of Migration 2

The Left Angle Cross of Migration 2 is the transpersonal Q3 variant of the Cross of Planning. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 40]] (Deliverance) and the Personality Earth in [[Gate 37]] (Family), this Left Angle cross is migration led by the lone provider — the person whose will to leave is also the will that delivers the bargain to a new place. The Design Sun in [[Gate 9]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 16]] supply the disciplined enthusiasm needed to make the migration land. Where Migration 1 leads with the bargain being relocated, Migration 2 leads with the deliverer being relocated. Ra called this the migration of rigidity — the willful, sometimes stubborn move that breaks one community's hold so a new one can form.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Design Sun:** Gate 9 — The Taming Power of the Small (Focus)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Duality (Realm of Jupiter, Purpose: Bonding)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — transpersonal karma
- **Axis A (Personality):** 37 ↔ 40
- **Axis B (Design):** 9 ↔ 16

## The Four Gates

### Gate 40 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 40]] is the stomach, the lone will of deliverance. As the lead of the Left Angle Cross of Migration 2, Gate 40 places the lone provider's "I'll do it but leave me alone" at the center of a transpersonal migration. The deliverer leaves. The deliverer arrives somewhere new. The will is what carries the migration.

### Gate 37 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain. Beneath the lone deliverer, Gate 37 grounds the cross in the bargain being relocated. The deliverer is not migrating for themselves alone; they are carrying a bargain to a new place where it can be re-established.

### Gate 9 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral focus, the engineering attention. In the design body, Gate 9 supplies the disciplined concentration the migrant deliverer needs to actually establish the new bargain on the other side of the move.

### Gate 16 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat enthusiasm, the experimental willingness. Below the surface, Gate 16 keeps the migration from becoming grim — there is artistry, repetition, a willingness to try again in the new place.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Duality is the Quarter of Bonding, governed by the Realm of Jupiter. Its purpose is the deep work of relationship. The Left Angle Cross of Migration 2, sitting in this quarter, makes migration a bonding act. The carrier of this cross moves not to escape relationship but to enter new bonds. The deliverer arrives in a new place and the bargain re-forms, the family re-forms, the relationship re-forms. Migration in this quarter is generative, not isolating.

Ra spoke about this cross with reference to rigidity — the carrier of Migration 2 can be unbending in their will to leave or to land. That rigidity is part of the design. It is what makes the migration succeed. A flexible migrant gets reabsorbed into the place they were trying to leave; a rigid migrant breaks through and establishes the new bond.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle crosses are transpersonal. The life is not lived for the self alone. A Left Angle Cross of Migration 2 person carries the bargain on behalf of a community — the one they left, the one they will form, the one they will help establish. The karma is collective, not personal.

## Living This Cross

For the Left Angle Cross of Migration 2, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are how the migration stays a living act and not a compulsion. The shadow of this cross is the migrant who keeps leaving because the mind cannot bear to settle. Real Migration 2 is body-led: the body responds, the move happens, the new bargain forms. Mind-led migration is exile — the perpetual wanderer who never lets the new bargain take root because Gate 40's aloneness has become a strategy rather than a recovery.

The healthy expression is the lone deliverer who can hold the alone time and also let the new community in. Gate 40 needs Gate 37 — the deliverance needs the bargain to deliver to. Without the bargain, Gate 40 collapses into isolation. Without Gate 40, Gate 37 collapses into co-dependence. The Cross of Migration 2 person, in correctness, is the deliverer who can leave and arrive — who can be alone and be in the bond — and the migration completes because both gates are honored.

The gift, lived in alignment, is the renewal of community through movement. Migration 2 people are the founders, the settlers, the establishers of new bargains. They carry the will to make a new place real. Without them, the bargain of the old place would never have anywhere to go; with them, the bargain finds new ground.

This cross also carries the loss of the original tribe. The Migration 2 person leaves something behind, and the leaving is not optional. The will of Gate 40 makes the leaving feel necessary, even when the mind grieves it. The healing is to allow the grief without letting it pull the person back to a bargain that was never going to hold for them. The new bargain is the assignment. The old bargain was the launchpad.
