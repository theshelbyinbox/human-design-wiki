# Left Angle Cross of Confrontation 2

The Left Angle Cross of Confrontation 2 is the transpersonal extension of the Rulership 4 gate set in the Quarter of Mutation. The Personality Sun in Gate 45 makes the kingly voice the conscious instrument, but with the Design axis rotated to Gates 25 and 46 (the Vessel of Love axis), the entire cross is reorganized around the confrontation that arises when material rulership meets universal love and embodied determination.

Where Confrontation 1 in the Quarter of Civilization was built to challenge unjust rulers from outside, Confrontation 2 in the Quarter of Mutation is built to confront from inside the system of mutation itself. The carrier is not simply attacking power; they are confronting the resistance to the mutation that the quarter requires. This is the cross of the mutative confronter — the being whose voice challenges the established tribal order to allow what must come next.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 45 (Gathering Together / The King)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 26 (The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 25 (Innocence / The Spirit of the Self)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 46 (Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose: Transformation)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Personality axis is the tribal axis 45/26 — the king and the salesman. The Design axis is the Vessel of Love axis 25/46. The conscious mind operates as the kingly voice grounded in egoic salesmanship; the unconscious carries universal love and embodied determination.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 45 (Personality Sun) — Gathering Together / The King

[[Gate 45]] is the conscious lead. The Confrontation 2 being identifies as a kingly voice — one who speaks for the tribe, who gathers the tribe's attention, who claims the right to direct what comes next. On a Left Angle cross, this kingly voice is turned transpersonally. The carrier is not gathering for themselves; they are gathering on behalf of the mutation that must come.

### Gate 26 (Personality Earth) — The Taming Power of the Great / The Egoist

[[Gate 26]] grounds the conscious mind in the egoic salesmanship. The Confrontation 2 being knows how to package challenge in language the tribe can accept. The 26 is what makes the confrontation effective; without it, the kingly voice would simply alienate the audience.

### Gate 25 (Design Sun) — Innocence / The Spirit of the Self

[[Gate 25]] is the unconscious driver. The being carries universal love at the level of the design. The confrontation arises from love rather than from grievance — Ra was clear that this is the key distinction between confronters who serve mutation and confronters who burn out as bitter critics.

### Gate 46 (Design Earth) — Pushing Upward / Determination of the Self

[[Gate 46]] grounds the unconscious in the love of the body. The Confrontation 2 being inhabits their body fully and confronts from the embodied position of having lived what they speak about.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation places this cross in the realm where Transformation is the purpose. The Confrontation 2 being takes the kingly voice of the tribe and turns it toward the mutation that the tribe resists. They confront not as enemies of the tribe but as members of the tribe who are structurally built to advocate for the change the tribe needs.

This produces a particular kind of confrontation that is different from the Confrontation 1 of the Quarter of Civilization. Confrontation 1 challenges established power from outside or below; Confrontation 2 challenges established patterns from within, using the very kingly voice that the tribe is conditioned to obey.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle Crosses carry Transpersonal Karma. The Confrontation 2 being is here to confront on behalf of mutation, not for personal advancement. The karma flows through the carrier as a transpersonal current — the confrontation is the work, and the work serves the collective transformation that the Quarter of Mutation is committed to delivering.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The five Left Angle profiles produce five distinct flavors of Confrontation 2.

The 4/6 confronts through their network of friends and allies — building the case for mutation within a defined circle and watching the influence spread. The 5/1 is the public confronter who broadcasts the challenge to the universal field. The 5/2 knows the mutation that must come and confronts only when self-motivated; their rare confrontations carry the weight of long deliberation. The 6/2 is the experiential confronter who speaks with the authority of having lived through the patterns they confront. The 6/3 carries the bridge between confrontation and surrender, oscillating between escalation and retreat.

## Living This Cross

For the Confrontation 2 being, the work is to confront with the love of Gate 25 leading. The conscious 45 wants to speak, the 26 wants to sell, but if these conscious motors operate without the unconscious 25/46 grounding, the confrontation becomes either grandiose or manipulative.

The shadow path is the carrier who uses the kingly voice for personal power, who packages confrontation as a way to dominate rather than to serve. Ra was clear that Left Angle crosses cannot be absolute in their rulership claims — they are structurally transpersonal, and any attempt to make the work personal collapses the cross.

The medicine is to honor Strategy and Inner Authority, and to let the universal love of Gate 25 guide the kingly voice of Gate 45. The Confrontation 2 being who speaks from love about what the tribe needs, who packages the mutation in language the tribe can accept, and who embodies what they speak about — this carrier becomes one of the most effective mutative voices on the wheel.

The 5/1 with this cross can become a reformer whose voice carries genuine authority because it is grounded in love. The 6/2 with this cross becomes the elder whose mere presence in a community shifts what is possible. Each profile produces a distinct expression of the same structural orientation: confrontation in service of the mutation the tribe is ready for but resisting.

The cross also requires the carrier to understand that confrontation is not the only mode of transmission. The 45 can also be used to gather and celebrate, the 26 can be used to teach, the 25 can be used to love unconditionally, the 46 can be used to inhabit the body fully. The integrated carrier oscillates between confrontation and contribution, using the cross's full range.

When the cross is lived well, the Confrontation 2 being becomes a fixed point of mutative leadership. They speak with the voice of the tribe, they package challenge in language the tribe can receive, and they ground the entire operation in the universal love that makes the confrontation medicinal rather than destructive. This is the cross of the loving reformer, the kingly voice that confronts not to destroy but to transform.
