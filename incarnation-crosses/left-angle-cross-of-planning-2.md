# Left Angle Cross of Identification 1

The Left Angle Cross of Identification 1 is the transpersonal Q2 variant of the Cross of Planning. With the Personality Sun in [[Gate 16]] (Enthusiasm) and Personality Earth in [[Gate 9]] (Focus), this Left Angle cross places identification — the [[Channel 9/16]] of talent — into a karmic role that is lived with and for others. Ra named this cross "Identification" because the carriers of it are recognized by what they are skilled at; they are identified, marked, located by the practiced enthusiasm they bring. The Design Sun in [[Gate 37]] and Design Earth in [[Gate 40]] hold the tribal bargain beneath, but in this Left Angle variant the bargain is in service of the skill being received and used by the collective. This is the artisan who civilizes by being identified.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 16 — Enthusiasm
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 9 — Focus (The Taming Power of the Small)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 37 — The Family / Friendship
- **Design Earth:** Gate 40 — Deliverance
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Civilization (Realm of Duhbe, Purpose: Form)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — transpersonal karma
- **Axis A (Personality):** 9 ↔ 16
- **Axis B (Design):** 37 ↔ 40

## The Four Gates

### Gate 16 (Personality Sun)
[[Gate 16]] is the Throat gate of enthusiasm, the experimental "I'll have a go" that lets skill enter form. As the lead of the Left Angle Cross of Identification 1, Gate 16 becomes the public-facing willingness — the surface through which others identify what this person is for.

### Gate 9 (Personality Earth)
[[Gate 9]] is the Sacral capacity to focus on small detail. Beneath the enthusiasm, Gate 9 is the practiced concentration that makes the identification real. People recognize this person because the focus has actually been done.

### Gate 37 (Design Sun)
[[Gate 37]] is the mouth of the bargain. In the design body, Gate 37 places the skill into a community context — the artisan is identified because there is a tribe that needed them.

### Gate 40 (Design Earth)
[[Gate 40]] is the will of deliverance. Beneath the surface, Gate 40 provides the alone-strength that lets the artisan recover between rounds of being identified and called upon.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Civilization is the Quarter of Form. Its purpose is to take initiations of mind and make them visible — to civilize the impulse. The Left Angle Cross of Identification 1, sitting in this quarter, makes the identification a civilizational act. The artisan is identified, and that identification gives the community a structure to gather around. Civilization is built in part by who gets identified and what they are identified as.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle crosses are transpersonal. The life is lived with and for others. A Left Angle Cross of Identification 1 person is not here primarily for their own talent; they are here to be identified by a community that needs that talent. The karma is shared. The skill belongs in some sense to the people who recognize it.

## Living This Cross

For the Left Angle Cross of Identification 1, [[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only honest way the identification can land. The shadow is the artisan who pursues skill for the sake of being identified — chasing recognition rather than letting the body lead. When the mind drives Gate 16's enthusiasm, the skill becomes performance, and the identification becomes a costume rather than a true mark.

The healthy expression is the artisan who let the body answer first and then practiced the focus long enough for the skill to become real. The community then identifies them not because they sought identification but because the work was done. This is the difference between a celebrity and a master. The Cross of Identification 1 person, when correct, is the master.

Ra noted that the Cross of Planning era has produced the artisan as a cultural figure — the recognized skilled person whose identification by the community gives them a place. The Left Angle Cross of Identification 1 person carries this archetype forward. They are the recognized craftsperson, the named scholar, the identified expert. Their karma is to be recognized, and to let that recognition serve the community rather than the ego.

The gift, lived in alignment, is the gift of contribution. Identification 1 people give the community something it can identify — a skill, a craft, a body of work — and that act of being identifiable is itself a service. It tells the community what is possible. It models what focus and enthusiasm together can produce. It civilizes by being seen.

The danger is the over-identification with the skill. When the person becomes their identification, the bargain underneath (Gate 37) and the deliverance underneath (Gate 40) can be lost. Then the artisan keeps performing past the point of sustainability because the identification has eaten the will. Healthy Identification 1 people return regularly to Gate 40's aloneness, drop the identification, and remember that the bargain comes from family — from rest, from intimacy, from the ones who know them outside the skill.
