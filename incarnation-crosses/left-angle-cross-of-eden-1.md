# Left Angle Cross of the Plane 1

The Left Angle Cross of the Plane 1 is the transpersonal extension of the Eden 1 gate set in the Quarter of Initiation. The Personality Sun in Gate 6 makes friction the conscious instrument, but with the Design axis rotated to the Vessel of Love axis 10/15, the entire cross is reorganized around witnessing the world as it actually is — without the consoling filters that most beings maintain.

Ra called this "the dharma cross" and described it as the configuration of the load-bearer who sees the plane in full. The Plane 1 being is not on the inside of the Eden experience — they have not personally fallen from grace in the way Eden 1 carriers have — but they are witnesses to the whole plane of human experience as it unfolds, and that witnessing is the work.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 6 (Conflict / Friction)
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 36 (Crisis / Darkening of the Light)
- **Design Sun:** Gate 15 (Modesty / Rhythm)
- **Design Earth:** Gate 10 (Treading / Behavior of the Self)
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Initiation (Realm of Alcyone, Purpose: Mind)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Karma

The Personality axis is the Solar Plexus emotional bonding axis 6/36 — friction and crisis. The Design axis is the Vessel of Love axis 15/10 — rhythm and behavior. The conscious mind leads with friction, grounded in crisis; the unconscious carries the love of humanity through rhythm and the love of the self through behavior.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 6 (Personality Sun) — Conflict / Friction

[[Gate 6]] is the Solar Plexus gate of friction, bonding, and intimacy. As the conscious lead of this Left Angle cross, Gate 6 places the entire cross at the apex of relational friction. The Plane 1 being lives the conflict of being-with-others as their conscious organizing principle.

### Gate 36 (Personality Earth) — Crisis / Darkening of the Light

[[Gate 36]] grounds the conscious mind in the experience of crisis. The Plane 1 being knows crisis intimately — not always as a personal experience but as a constant feature of the plane they are witnessing.

### Gate 15 (Design Sun) — Modesty / Rhythm

[[Gate 15]] is the unconscious G Center gate of the love of humanity through rhythm. The being carries the magnetic monopole's social face at the level of design — the rhythm that connects them to the human family in all its variations.

### Gate 10 (Design Earth) — Treading / Behavior of the Self

[[Gate 10]] grounds the unconscious in the love of the self through behavior. The Plane 1 being's behavior is the vehicle through which their witnessing of the plane is expressed.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Initiation places this cross at the closing of the first half of the quarter's eight gates. The Plane 1 being is built to witness what the rest of the quarter is producing — to see the mental codification, the rulership crises, the inexperience-to-experience movement, and to report on the plane of life that emerges from all this activity.

## The Angle Expression

Left Angle Crosses carry Transpersonal Karma. The Plane 1 being is here to witness on behalf of the collective — to see what most cannot see, to register the full weight of the plane, and to transmit that witnessing to others. The karma flows through the carrier as a transpersonal current.

Ra noted that Plane 1 beings can be very depressed because they see the full reality of human existence without the protective filters that most beings maintain. They see hunger, starvation, pain, conflict, death, disease — and they see it not as abstract knowledge but as the actual texture of the plane.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

The five Left Angle profiles produce five distinct flavors of Plane 1.

The 4/6 witnesses through their network — they see the plane as it manifests in their close circle. The 5/1 is the paranoid witness — they suffer from the universal projection that 5th line beings carry, compounded by the unfiltered view of the plane; they are often heretics about spirituality because they cannot bring themselves to believe in a benevolent universe that allows what they see. The 5/2 is the ultimate hermit — they see the plane and want nothing to do with it. The 6/2 is the experiential survivor — they live through the first thirty years of suffering, climb onto the roof, and accept what they have seen. The 6/3 is the bridge between the plane and the next initiation; they carry the gateway to Gate 25 and the possibility that mutative spirituality can transform the experience of the plane.

## Living This Cross

For the Plane 1 being, the work is to witness without being destroyed by what is seen. Ra was clear that this cross does not produce conventional spirituality. The carriers are heretics, atheists, doubters — not because they are spiritually deficient but because the plane they see does not match the consoling narratives that mainstream spirituality offers.

The shadow path is the carrier who becomes nihilistic, who concludes from the witnessing that nothing matters and that life is meaningless. The 5/1 in shadow becomes the angry atheist who attacks every spiritual claim; the 5/2 in shadow becomes the depressed recluse who refuses all engagement; the 6/2 in shadow becomes the cool ironist who has accepted too much.

The medicine is the bridge to Gate 25 that Ra emphasized. The Plane 1 being is structurally positioned at the closing of the first eight gates of the quarter, and the next gate is Gate 25 — the Vessel of Love, universal love, the priesthood. The bridge is the recognition that mutative individual spirituality might transform the experience of the plane even if it does not change the plane itself. This is not the consoling spirituality of mainstream religion; it is the harder spirituality of waking up to one's own design and living it correctly.

The 6/3 carries this bridge most explicitly. Ra noted that the 6/3 Plane 1 can have the deep breakthrough that says, "Wait a minute, there is something else, there is something beyond that." This is not return to belief but discovery of mutative individual mechanics — the recognition that one's own design has authority and that living that authority is what saves one from being destroyed by the plane.

When integrated, the Left Angle Cross of the Plane 1 produces a witness whose seeing serves the collective. The carrier reports on the plane in whatever medium suits them — writing, art, conversation, presence — and the report mutates the listener's capacity to see what is actually there. This is not pleasant work, but it is essential work, and the carrier who lives it well becomes one of the more important transpersonal voices on the wheel.
