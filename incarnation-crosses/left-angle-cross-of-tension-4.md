# Left Angle Cross of Tension 4

The Left Angle Cross of Tension 4 — known in Ra Uru Hu's teaching as the Left Angle Cross of the Individualism 2 — is the transpersonal expression of the four-armed Cross of Tension in the Quarter of Mutation. The same four gates — 21, 48, 38, 39 — organize this cross, and the Left Angle rotation places the carrier in a karmic field of transformation rather than in a private fight.

This is the second of the two Individualism crosses, and Ra spoke of his deep attachment to the Individualism configuration. The Left Angle Tension 4 carrier is the individualist of the mutation quarter — the one whose departure from the collective norm is itself the genetic engine of the species' transformation.

## Mechanics

- **Personality Sun:** Gate 38 — Opposition / The Fighter
- **Personality Earth:** Gate 39 — Obstruction / The Provocateur
- **Design Sun:** Gate 48 — The Well / Depth
- **Design Earth:** Gate 21 — Control / The Hunter
- **Quarter:** Quarter of Mutation (Realm of Mutation, Purpose of Transformation)
- **Angle:** Left Angle — Transpersonal Destiny

The Personality axis 38↔39 carries the conscious life as individual root pressure. The Design axis 48↔21 carries the unconscious vehicle as tribal foundation. The Left Angle means the carrier's life is not theirs to keep — it belongs to the karmic field of mutation they are part of.

## The Four Gates

### Gate 38 (Personality Sun) — Opposition, The Fighter

The 38 in the Left Angle is the fight on behalf of the field. The carrier consciously opposes whatever in their karmic environment stands in the way of mutation. The harmonic gate 28 turns the opposition into the great risk-taking that gives life its purpose. Mutation is uncertain; the fighter is the one who makes the risk possible by refusing to accept the safe form.

### Gate 39 (Personality Earth) — Obstruction, The Provocateur

Gate 39 grounds the fighter in the provocateur. The Left Angle's provocation is the testing of the karmic field — the moods, the obstructions, the daisy-petal interrogations that ask the field whether its spirit is real.

### Gate 48 (Design Sun) — The Well, Depth

The unconscious well. The body's foundation is the qualitative substrate from which the conscious fight draws. The Left Angle individualist's opposition is not a tantrum; it is built on a depth they do not have to explain.

### Gate 21 (Design Earth) — Control, The Hunter

The unconscious tribal controller. The vehicle holds the right to manage material resources. The Left Angle's individualism is structurally supported by a body that knows how to control the conditions of its own work.

## The Quarter Theme

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm of transformation. The Left Angle Tension 4 contributes to mutation through individualism. Ra repeatedly emphasized that mutation comes through the individual — through the willingness of one person to refuse the collective settlement. The Left Angle Tension 4 carrier is the configuration of that refusal: a karmic life organized around being the one who would not go along.

## The Angle Expression

A Left Angle Cross is a cross of transpersonal destiny. The Left Angle Tension 4 carrier participates in a field that is karmically theirs to disturb. They are not here to live a personal life of opposition. They are here to be the individualist that a specific karmic network needed in order to mutate.

The not-self distortion is the carrier who has confused individualism with eccentricity. The fight becomes performance. The provocation becomes attention-seeking. The depth becomes a personal mystery cultivated for effect. The control becomes a private dominion that lacks an actual field to govern. The correct expression is the opposite: the individualism is given to the field, and the field mutates because it could not absorb the carrier's refusal into its existing pattern.

## Profiles This Cross Manifests In

Left Angle Crosses manifest through 4/6, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3.

- **4/6** carries the externalizing individualist whose network is the field of mutation
- **5/1** carries the universalizing individualist whose foundation gives the heresy weight
- **5/2** carries the heretical hermit — natural withdrawal meets called provocation
- **6/2** carries the matured hermit individualist whose three life phases bring the mutation to readability
- **6/3** carries the role-model experimenter individualist whose mistakes become the species' lessons

## Living This Cross

[[Strategy]] and [[Inner Authority]] are the only correct navigation. The 38's splenic fear of uselessness and the 39's emotional pressure must be lived correctly rather than overridden by the mind. The individualist who acts from the mind becomes the eccentric. The individualist who acts from authority becomes the mutator.

The signature of the cross lived correctly is a karmic field that has mutated because the carrier was an individualist within it. The signature of the not-self is the carrier whose individualism stayed inside their own body and produced nothing but chronic somatic tension, mood disorders, and the wearing fatigue of fighting an internal war. The work is to let the four-armed pressure move outward into the specific field the karmic life was built for.

When the carrier honors the design, they become what Ra honored most in the Individualism crosses: the person who altered the species by altering one specific karmic neighborhood. Mutation does not come from the average. It comes from the refusal of the individualist to be average. Lived correctly, Left Angle Tension 4 is the cross of the genetic departure — the one whose unwillingness to fit makes the future possible.

## Individualism in the Quarter of Mutation

The Quarter of Mutation is the realm where the genetic engine of the species actually runs. The mutation that lifts the species into its next form happens here, in the cross-pollination of individual departures from the collective norm. The Left Angle Individualism 2 is the configuration whose karmic life is specifically organized around this mutative function in this specific quarter.

Ra Uru Hu was emphatic that mutation does not come from the average. The average organism survives by replicating the existing pattern. Mutation comes through the individual whose refusal to replicate the existing pattern produces a new pattern that the species can then absorb. The Left Angle Individualism 2 carrier is one of the karmic configurations through which this refusal is delivered to a specific field of the human community.

The four-armed pressure of the Tension cross is concentrated here in the service of mutation. The 38 fights for purpose — the refusal of meaninglessness that makes the new pattern worth living. The 39 tests the spirit — the obstruction that wakes the field up from its replication of the old form. The 48 holds the depth that the new pattern is built on. The 21 controls the conditions of the carrier's own work — the material foundation that the karmic mutation requires.

For the carrier this means a particular kind of patience. The mutation does not always show in one lifetime. The carrier's individualism may not be recognized as a contribution until long after the carrier is gone. The work is to live the individualism correctly anyway — to trust that the design is doing its work even when the field gives no immediate sign of having received the contribution.
