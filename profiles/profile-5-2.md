# Profile 5/2 - Heretic/Hermit

The 5/2 profile is a harmonic Left Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a fifth-line Personality (the Heretic) with a second-line Design (the Hermit). It carries the strong projection field of the fifth line with the solitude-seeking natural genius of the second line.

## The Heretic Personality (Line 5)

The fifth-line personality is highly projected upon. Others see a practical solution-bringer, a universalist, someone who can resolve their problems. The 5/2 person may find this projection confusing or burdensome, because their second-line design simply wants to develop naturally and be called out on its own terms.

## The Hermit Design (Line 2)

The second-line design carries natural abilities that the person may not be fully aware of. The Hermit needs solitude to develop and express what it carries. The calling-out mechanism of the second line -- where others recognize and invite the Hermit's gifts -- interacts with the fifth line's projection field in a complex way. The 5/2 may be called out because of projected expectations (fifth line) as well as genuine recognition (second line), and distinguishing between these is important.

## Left Angle: Transpersonal Karma

The 5/2 is Left Angle -- their life serves a transpersonal purpose through their encounters with and influence on others. The universalizing quality of the fifth line, grounded in the second line's natural gift, offers something genuinely useful to communities and relationships.

## Practical Patterns

The 5/2 may notice a rhythm: periods of retreat and natural development (Hermit), followed by periods of being called out into situations where their gifts are expected to resolve something (Heretic). Operating by Strategy and Authority helps distinguish which callings are genuine from which are projections that will not serve either party.

## Angle

Left Angle. Transpersonal karma.
