# Profile 4/6 - Opportunist/Role Model

The 4/6 profile is the final Right Angle profile and the other bookend of the Right Angle arc, combining a fourth-line Personality (the Opportunist) with a sixth-line Design (the Role Model). It is described as "the alpha and omega" of Right Angle profiles -- the 1/3 is the beginning (entirely lower trigram) and the 4/6 is the end (entirely upper trigram).

## The Opportunist Personality (Line 4)

The fourth-line personality is network-oriented. Opportunities come through existing relationships. The 4/6 person is consciously invested in their social network and knows that the right opportunities arrive through people they already know, not through strangers.

## The Role Model Design (Line 6)

The sixth-line design carries the tripartite life arc:

- **Phase 1 (birth to ~30)**: Lived as third line -- trial and error, experiential intensity, many encounters with what doesn't work. The 4/6 in their first 30 years has a social and experimental intensity: Opportunist personality building relationships while third-line design discovers through error.
- **Phase 2 (~30-50)**: On the roof. Observational. Integration. The social investment continues (fourth-line personality persists) but the sixth-line design is absorbing lessons rather than actively experimenting.
- **Phase 3 (50+)**: Return as embodied Role Model. The 4/6 now brings the wisdom of both phases into their network -- offering genuine guidance born from lived experience.

## The Network and the Role

The 4/6 is uniquely positioned to be a Role Model within their community. Because they are both network-oriented (fourth line) and wisdom-bearing (sixth line), they have the social access and the credibility that comes from having genuinely lived it. Their influence operates through existing relationships rather than broadcast.

Ra Uru Hu noted that the 4/6 is the only profile said to be destined for a soulmate -- someone genuinely suited to them, found through correct Strategy and Authority.

## Angle

Right Angle. Personal destiny. The entirety of upper trigram energy directed toward personal fulfillment through the social network.
