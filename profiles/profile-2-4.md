# Profile 2/4 - Hermit/Opportunist

The 2/4 profile is a harmonic Right Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a second-line Personality (the Hermit) with a fourth-line Design (the Opportunist). Like all harmonic profiles, it is relatively rare.

## The Hermit Personality (Line 2)

The second-line personality carries natural gifts and abilities that may be invisible to the person themselves. Line 2 people often do not recognize their own genius because it comes so naturally -- they cannot see why others don't also have it. They are frequently called out by life: recognized and invited by others precisely when they have retreated into their own world.

The Hermit needs solitude. They are recharged by time alone, engaged in their own interests and development. Social demands can feel depleting. The not-self pattern for the second line is responding to projections from others -- being pulled out of hermit mode before ready, or performing in ways others expect rather than honoring the natural rhythm of retreat and emergence.

## The Opportunist Design (Line 4)

The fourth-line design is network-oriented. Opportunities arrive through existing relationships. The 2/4 body is social in its needs and investments -- it builds and maintains the relationships through which its gifts will be called out.

The combination creates a rhythm: periods of retreat and development (Hermit) followed by engagement with the network where the developed gifts are recognized and invited (Opportunist). Neither phase should be forced into the other.

## Practical Pattern

A 2/4 may notice that they are at their best when they have had adequate alone time and are then called into engagement by someone in their network who genuinely recognizes what they carry. The calling-out by others (not self-promotion) is the natural activation mechanism for the second line.

## Angle

Right Angle. Personal destiny. The 2/4's destiny unfolds through its own natural cycle of hermitage and social engagement.
