# Profile 5/1 - Heretic/Investigator

The 5/1 profile is a Left Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a fifth-line Personality (the Heretic) with a first-line Design (the Investigator). [[Ra Uru Hu]] himself carried the 5/1 profile. The fifth line carries the most powerful projection field of any line.

## The Heretic Personality (Line 5)

The fifth-line personality is universalized and projected upon. Others see in the 5/1 someone who has a practical, universally applicable solution -- a savior, a fixer, someone who can resolve crises and bring order. This projection field is not chosen or performed; it is mechanical. It exists in the aura.

"My job is to spread the mutation. But the mutation arises out of the human experience." - Ra Uru Hu

The word "heretic" reflects the fifth line's function: taking established knowledge and universalizing it, applying it in ways that may challenge existing norms. This can be experienced by others as radical or disruptive.

The challenge for the 5/1 personality is managing the projection field. When the 5/1 fails to deliver what others projected onto them (inevitable, because the projections are often unrealistic), those same people can turn into detractors. The fifth line cycles through periods of being seen as savior and periods of being condemned.

## The Investigator Design (Line 1)

The first-line design quietly builds the solid knowledge base that the fifth-line personality will universalize. The 1-design investigates, researches, and establishes the foundation. The 5-personality takes that foundation and broadcasts it across communities and contexts.

## Left Angle: Transpersonal Karma

The 5/1 is Left Angle -- transpersonal karma. The 5/1 person's life serves a purpose larger than personal fulfillment. Their encounters with others, their universalization of what they know, serves the evolution of the collective. This does not mean the 5/1 must sacrifice themselves; it means their correct living naturally serves others as a byproduct.

## Practical Implications

5/1 people are advised to be selective about which invitations they accept. Because the projection field is strong, people will invite them into situations expecting transformation. If the 5/1 accepts invitations that are not genuinely aligned with their Strategy and Authority, they will be set up to fail the projection and bear the blame.
