# Profile 6/3 - Role Model/Martyr

The 6/3 profile is a harmonic Left Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], the final profile in the chain of twelve, combining a sixth-line Personality (the Role Model) with a third-line Design (the Martyr). It mirrors and closes the chain that begins with the 1/3.

## The Role Model Personality (Line 6)

The sixth-line personality carries the tripartite life arc:

- **Phase 1 (birth to ~30)**: Lived as third line -- trial and error, experiential intensity.
- **Phase 2 (~30-50)**: On the roof -- integration, observation, building wisdom.
- **Phase 3 (50+)**: Return as embodied Role Model.

The 6/3 in their first 30 years carries double third-line intensity: a sixth-line personality in its third-line phase AND a third-line design. This double experimental pressure tends to produce a particularly eventful and transformative early life.

## The Martyr Design (Line 3)

The third-line design is a trial-and-error body, discovering what does not work through direct encounter. For the 6/3, the Martyr design provides the raw experiential material -- the lessons learned through bumping into things -- that the sixth-line personality will eventually integrate and embody as wisdom.

The closing of the chain (6/3 mirrors 1/3) suggests that what begins as simple self-absorbed discovery in the 1/3 ends as embodied transpersonal wisdom in the 6/3, a wisdom earned through a life full of experimentation.

## Left Angle: Transpersonal Karma

The 6/3 is Left Angle. Their life serves a transpersonal purpose. The Role Model they become -- grounded in the Martyr's direct experience -- offers something genuinely useful to communities, not through formal teaching but through the example of how they live.

## Angle

Left Angle. Transpersonal karma.
