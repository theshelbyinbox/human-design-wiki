# Profile 2/5 - Hermit/Heretic

The 2/5 profile is a harmonic Right Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a second-line Personality (the Hermit) with a fifth-line Design (the Heretic). It is one of the more unusual combinations, pairing the inward-oriented Hermit with the highly projected-upon Heretic.

## The Hermit Personality (Line 2)

The second-line personality carries natural gifts and needs solitude to develop and express them. The 2/5 Hermit personality is genuinely not aware of how they are perceived -- they are in their own world, following their interests, unaware of the projection field they carry through the fifth-line design.

## The Heretic Design (Line 5)

The fifth-line design carries a strong universal projection field. Others look at a fifth-line person and project solutions onto them -- they see a savior, a fixer, a person who can provide a practical answer to their problem. The fifth line is the most projected-upon line in the system.

The "heretic" name comes from the fifth line's function: to take what has been established (through lines 1-4) and universalize it, make it practical, apply it broadly. This universalizing can appear radical or disruptive to existing systems -- hence "heretic."

## The Combination

The 2/5 person may be genuinely surprised by the expectations others place on them. The Hermit personality is not oriented toward managing projections; it simply wants to develop its natural gifts in peace. Yet the fifth-line design makes them unavoidably visible and subject to projection.

Correct living for the 2/5 involves developing genuine mastery (Hermit) and then being selective about which invitations to step into (Heretic). Accepting every invitation to "save" or "fix" others' situations leads to burnout and unmet expectations.

## Angle

Right Angle. Personal destiny, with a projection field that affects others as a byproduct.
