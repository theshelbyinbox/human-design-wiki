# Profile 3/6 - Martyr/Role Model

The 3/6 profile is a harmonic Right Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a third-line Personality (the Martyr) with a sixth-line Design (the Role Model). It is notable for having a tripartite life process built into the sixth-line design.

## The Martyr Personality (Line 3)

The third-line personality learns through direct experience and trial and error. The 3/6 person's conscious identity is shaped by encounter, discovery, and experimentation. They bump into things, find out what doesn't work, and carry the discoveries forward.

## The Role Model Design (Line 6)

The sixth-line design lives in three distinct phases:

**Phase 1 (birth to approximately age 30)**: The sixth line lives as a third line. Trial and error, experimentation, intense experiences, many encounters with what doesn't work. This first phase provides the raw experiential material that the Role Model will eventually distill into wisdom.

**Phase 2 (approximately age 30-50)**: The sixth line goes "on the roof" -- withdrawing from direct engagement, observing, integrating the lessons of the first phase. This phase can feel like a quieter, more reflective period. The person is not disengaged from life, but they are no longer in the middle of it in the same way.

**Phase 3 (age 50+)**: Return. The sixth line comes back down from the roof, now carrying the embodied wisdom of two full phases of life. This is when the Role Model emerges -- not as a title or aspiration but as a natural expression of what has been lived and integrated.

## The 3/6 Life Arc

The 3/6 experiences the most intense first 30 years of any profile: Martyr personality plus third-line design phase means double trial-and-error intensity. After 30, the sixth-line design begins its observational phase, and the personality's experimental quality continues but may feel more internally processed.

## Angle

Right Angle. Personal destiny, with a life arc built toward eventual embodied wisdom.
