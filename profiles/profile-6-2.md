# Profile 6/2 - Role Model/Hermit

The 6/2 profile is a harmonic Left Angle profile in [[Human Design System]], combining a sixth-line Personality (the Role Model) with a second-line Design (the Hermit). The sixth line's tripartite life arc gives the 6/2 a built-in developmental structure.

## The Role Model Personality (Line 6)

The sixth-line personality lives in three phases:

- **Phase 1 (birth to ~30)**: Lived as third line -- trial and error, direct experimentation, many encounters with what doesn't work.
- **Phase 2 (~30-50)**: On the roof -- withdrawal, observation, integration. The 6/2 in this phase may appear to retreat from active engagement. They are processing and building the wisdom base they will later embody.
- **Phase 3 (50+)**: Return as Role Model. The 6/2 embodies authentic wisdom earned through direct experience and observation. Others naturally look to them for guidance.

## The Hermit Design (Line 2)

The second-line design carries natural gifts that are called out by others. The Hermit needs solitude to develop what it carries. For the 6/2, the second-line Hermit quality reinforces the observational phase of the sixth line -- periods of retreat and inward focus are natural and necessary, not signs of withdrawal from life.

## Left Angle: Transpersonal Karma

The 6/2 is Left Angle -- their becoming a genuine Role Model serves a transpersonal purpose. Others are guided by the 6/2's example, not by the 6/2's instructions or advice per se, but by the way they live. Embodied wisdom transmitted through living correctly is the 6/2 gift.

## Angle

Left Angle. Transpersonal karma.
