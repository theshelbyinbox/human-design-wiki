# Profiles & Lines

The Profile is a two-number code derived from the lines of your Sun and Earth gates at birth (the Personality Sun line followed by the Design Sun line). It describes the *role* you are designed to play in life — how you naturally interact with others and the patterns through which your purpose unfolds.

There are six lines (1 through 6, each with a distinct archetype) and twelve possible profile combinations. Each profile also carries an *angle* — Right Angle (personal destiny), Juxtaposition (fixed fate), or Left Angle (transpersonal karma) — that describes the broader scope of one's incarnation.

Profile sits underneath type and authority. It does not change your Strategy or Authority, but it shapes the texture of how those tools express through you.
