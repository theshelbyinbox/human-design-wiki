# Inner Authority

Inner Authority is the body-based decision-making mechanism specific to each person's chart configuration. Paired with [[Strategy]] as the two foundational tools Human Design offers, Inner Authority answers the question: *how should I decide?*

The system's central claim is that the mind is not an appropriate decision-making organ. Each Authority is a different mechanical signal — sacral sound, emotional wave, splenic hit, heart conviction, self-articulation through speech, or for [[Reflector]]s the time-based sampling of the lunar cycle — that bypasses the mind's conditioned criteria and routes the decision through the body's own intelligence.
