# Inner Authority Overview

Inner Authority in [[Human Design System]] is the body-based decision-making mechanism specific to each person's chart configuration. It is the answer to the question *how should I decide?* — and it is determined by which centers are defined in a person's BodyGraph and in what hierarchical order. Inner Authority is paired with [[Strategy Overview|Strategy]] as the two foundational tools Human Design offers.

The system's central claim about authority is that the mind is not an appropriate decision-making organ. The mind is categorized as "outer authority" — useful for giving advice to others, but not reliable for guiding one's own life, because it is too easily influenced by the conditioning of [[Open Centers|open centers]]. The deeper rationale for this lives in [[Mind vs Body Decision-Making]].

> "There is a place within everyone that will give you with absolute clarity your truth in that moment, because it's you. It's not coming out of your openness. It's not coming out of the conditioning of the other."
> — Ra Uru Hu

## The Body Knows

Inner Authority means trusting the intelligence of your body — your vehicle — to navigate life correctly. Each Authority is a different mechanical signal that the body produces in response to actual reality: a sacral sound, an emotional wave, a splenic hit, a heart-felt conviction, a self-articulation through speech, or for [[Reflector]]s the time-based sampling of the lunar cycle.

When you make decisions from Inner Authority, you align yourself with your unique trajectory — your "correct geometry," as Ra called it. The friction goes down. The not-self themes fade. The signature state appears more often. This is the empirical evidence of the experiment working.

## The Seven Authorities

There are seven distinct authority configurations in Human Design, organized hierarchically by which body center governs the decision. They are covered in detail in their own pages:

| Authority | Configuration | Who Has It |
|---|---|---|
| [[Emotional Authority]] | Defined Solar Plexus | ~50% of population — any type |
| [[Sacral Authority]] | Defined Sacral, undefined Solar Plexus | Generators and Manifesting Generators without emotional definition |
| [[Splenic Authority]] | Defined Spleen, undefined Sacral and Solar Plexus | Some Manifestors and Projectors |
| [[Ego Authority]] | Defined Heart connected to Throat or G, no SP/Sacral/Spleen | Rare — some Manifestors and Projectors |
| [[Self-Projected Authority]] | G connected to Throat, no other definition | ~2-3% of population — some Projectors |
| [[Mental Authority]] | Definition only in Head/Ajna/Throat | Mental Projectors |
| [[Lunar Authority]] | No definition at all | [[Reflector]]s only |

[[Minor Authorities]] covers the rarer configurations (Ego-Manifested and Ego-Projected) in additional depth. [[No Direct Inner Authority]] covers the two configurations (Mental Authority and Lunar Authority) that operate without a body-center signal.

## How to Find Your Authority

To identify your Inner Authority, look at your BodyGraph and check the centers in this order:

1. **Is the Solar Plexus defined?** If yes — [[Emotional Authority]]. Stop here.
2. **Is the Sacral defined?** If yes — [[Sacral Authority]]. Stop here.
3. **Is the Spleen defined?** If yes — [[Splenic Authority]]. Stop here.
4. **Is the Heart defined and connected to the Throat or G?** If yes — [[Ego Authority]]. Stop here.
5. **Is the G connected to the Throat with no Heart definition?** If yes — [[Self-Projected Authority]]. Stop here.
6. **Are only Head/Ajna/Throat defined?** If yes — [[Mental Authority]]. Stop here.
7. **No definition at all?** — [[Lunar Authority]].

The full mechanics of this hierarchy live in [[Authority Hierarchy]].

## Authority and the Mind

Human Design positions the mind's desire to be involved in decisions as the source of most life problems. Open centers generate not-self strategies that the mind uses as its basis for deciding — fear of survival (open Spleen), need for love (open Heart), need to know (open Head), need to feel emotional clarity (open Solar Plexus). None of these strategies represent the person's actual authority.

The experiment of living by Inner Authority is the experiment of bypassing the mind's agenda and trusting the body's intelligence. It does not require belief. It requires the practice.

## Authority and Deconditioning

Living by Inner Authority is the engine of [[Deconditioning]]. Ra Uru Hu placed the full timeline at seven years — the body's cellular renewal cycle — but the early evidence appears much sooner. As correct decisions begin producing better outcomes and the [[Signature]] state begins appearing more often, trust in the body's authority grows, and the mind's grip on decision-making relaxes.

> "When you begin to follow your Inner Authority, you start to notice the difference between resistance and flow. You start to see where you've been making decisions from the mind, from conditioning, from fear. And you start to feel the ease and alignment that comes from trusting your body."

## Beginning the Experiment

The practice is simple to describe:

- Identify your Authority from your BodyGraph.
- Pair it with your [[Strategy Overview|Strategy]] (based on type).
- For each decision, run it through that combination instead of the mind.
- Watch what happens.

The system does not ask for belief. It asks for consistent application of Strategy and Authority and observation of what results. The results — the quality of life, the frequency of the Signature state, the reduction of Not-Self themes — are the evidence.
