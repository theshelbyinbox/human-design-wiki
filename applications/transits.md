# Transits in Human Design

Transits in [[Human Design System]] refer to the continuous movement of planets through the 64 gates of the Rave Mandala (wheel). As planets move through gates, they activate temporary definitions in those gates, which can form channels and define centers in the global field. These transits condition everyone with corresponding open gates or centers.

## How Transits Work

Every gate in the BodyGraph has a position in the 360-degree wheel of the Rave Mandala, corresponding to a position in the zodiac. As planets move through these positions, they activate the gates they occupy. When a transiting planet activates a gate that completes a channel with another gate (either fixed in a person's chart or fixed in another transiting planet), a temporary channel forms.

This is the same mechanism as conditioning from other people, but at a planetary scale: the neutrino stream carries the imprint of the planet's gate activation, and anyone with the corresponding open center or gate in their chart is temporarily defined by it.

## The Program

Ra Uru Hu used the term "the Program" to describe the continuous, collective conditioning field produced by planetary transits. The Program is not personal -- it is a background frequency that all life on Earth moves through. It shapes collective mood, collective themes, and the broader tone of any period.

"With every breath we take, we are influenced by the program around us. Those objects moving through various imprint points, impacting us on a day-to-day basis." - Ra Uru Hu

Individuals with more open centers are more affected by the Program. Reflectors, with all centers open, are the most directly responsive to the transit field and can serve as living barometers of the collective's current condition.

## The Human Design Year: Gate 41

Ra Uru Hu noted that the Human Design "year" does not begin on January 1 but at the moment the Sun moves into Gate 41 -- a contraction gate in the Root Center associated with the beginning of a new experiential cycle. This typically occurs around January 22 each year, at the same time as the Chinese New Year. Gate 41 is described as "the beginning before the beginning" -- the seed of new experience.

## Significant Transiting Bodies

- **The Sun**: The most powerful transiting body, representing approximately 70 percent of the neutrino imprinting that defines the chart. The Sun moves through the wheel in approximately one year.
- **The Moon**: Moves through all 64 gates in approximately 28 days. Governs the lunar cycle that is the foundation of [[Lunar Authority]] for Reflectors.
- **The outer planets** (Saturn, Pluto, Neptune, Uranus): Move slowly and can hold positions in gates for months or years, creating extended collective conditioning fields.

## Practical Use of Transit Awareness

Transit awareness in Human Design is generally not used for prediction or planning in the astrological sense. Rather, it is used to:

- Understand why certain themes feel prominent at certain times
- Recognize that intensified emotions or pressures may be coming from the transit field, not from the person's own chart
- Allow [[Reflector]] types to understand their experience through the lunar cycle map
- Contextualize collective events and moods

## Transits and the Not-Self

When a transiting planet defines an open center, the person with that open center experiences temporary definition. If they do not have awareness of this, they may make decisions from the temporarily defined center rather than from their true Authority. Awareness of active transits can help people recognize: "This urgency/clarity/emotion may be a transit, not my authority."
