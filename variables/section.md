# Variables & PHS

The Variables are the advanced layer of Human Design — the four arrows around the head and body of the BodyGraph that point either left or right. Each arrow indicates a *passive* (left) or *active* (right) orientation for a specific domain of life: digestion, environment, perspective, and motivation.

Variables are introduced after a person has spent enough time living their basic Type and Authority to be ready for nuance. Ra Uru Hu described them as the second wave of the Human Design experiment — relevant only after the not-self has been substantially loosened through years of correct decision-making.

PHS (Primary Health System) is the dietary application of the digestion arrow — the six modes of digestion (Appetite, Taste, Thirst, Touch, Sound, Light) that describe how a specific body is designed to take in food.
