# Energy Types

Type is the most fundamental layer of a Human Design chart. It is determined by which [[Centers]] are defined and how they connect to the Throat. Type sets your aura — how your energy meets the world — and dictates your [[Strategy]] (the type-level behavioral guideline) and your [[Signature]] and [[Not-Self]] themes.

There are five types in Human Design. Generators and Manifesting Generators together make up about 70% of the population. Projectors are about 20%. Manifestors are 8–9%. Reflectors are about 1%.

Your type does not change. Knowing it and living it correctly is the foundation everything else is built on.

## Pages in this Section

- [[Generator]] — sacral defined; respond strategy; satisfaction signature; frustration not-self; ~37% of population
- [[Manifesting Generator]] — sacral plus motor-to-throat; respond and inform; speed and multi-tracking; ~33%
- [[Manifestor]] — non-sacral with motor-to-throat; inform strategy; peace signature; anger not-self; ~8–9%
- [[Projector]] — non-sacral, penetrating aura; wait for the invitation; success signature; bitterness not-self; ~20%
- [[Reflector]] — no defined centers; lunar cycle strategy; surprise signature; disappointment not-self; ~1%
