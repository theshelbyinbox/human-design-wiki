# Emotional Authority

Emotional Authority is the inner decision-making mechanism for people with a defined [[Solar Plexus Center]]. It applies regardless of energy type -- Generators, Manifesting Generators, Manifestors, and Projectors can all carry Emotional Authority. [[Reflector]] types never have Emotional Authority because they have no defined centers. Emotional Authority is the most common authority in [[Human Design System]], affecting approximately 50 percent of the population.

## Core Principle: No Truth in the Now

The defining principle of Emotional Authority is that there is no emotional clarity in the present moment. The Solar Plexus wave moves continuously between hope and pain, high and low. When a person is on the high of their wave, everything looks positive and possible. When they are on the low, everything looks bleak. Neither the high nor the low represents clarity -- both are emotional distortions.

"The only thing that is going to save you in this life is understanding that there is no truth in the now. No truth in the now. Everything requires patience." - Ra Uru Hu

Decisions made at the peak of emotional excitement frequently produce regret later. Decisions made at the trough of emotional pain are often needlessly cautious or avoidant. Emotional clarity comes when the wave has moved through its range enough times that a neutral, grounded perspective becomes available.

## Waiting for Clarity

The practical application of Emotional Authority is waiting before committing. When an opportunity, invitation, or decision arises, the person with Emotional Authority says: "I need some time." They do not give a final answer immediately, regardless of how clear or obvious the answer seems in the moment.

The specific waiting time varies by person and situation. Ra Uru Hu often cited at least 24 hours as a minimum. For significant life decisions, the wait may be days or weeks. The signal that clarity has arrived is not an absence of emotion but a relative neutrality -- neither the high excitement of a peak nor the heaviness of a trough, but a grounded sense of what is correct.

"If they can't give you time, they don't belong in your life." - Ra Uru Hu, addressing Projectors with Emotional Authority

## The Four Emotional Waves

The Solar Plexus contains channels associated with four distinct emotional wave patterns, each with a different rhythm and texture:

- **Tribal wave (channels 37-40 and 19-49)**: Builds gradually and releases in bursts. Accumulates emotional pressure over time until it reaches a threshold and discharges.
- **Abstract wave (channels 35-36 and 30)**: Peaks sharply in excitement and drops steadily as expectations meet reality. The "elevator" pattern -- rapid rise, gradual descent.
- **Individual wave (channels 12-22 and 55-39)**: Spontaneous highs and lows with no predictable pattern. Can shift rapidly and without obvious external cause.
- **Combined**: Some people carry more than one wave pattern.

Understanding which wave pattern one carries allows for greater self-compassion around the emotional experience and more accurate pattern recognition.

## Emotional Authority and Other Authorities

When a person has a defined Solar Plexus, it takes precedence over all other authorities. An emotional Generator responds to their sacral but does not commit on the sacral response alone -- they wait through the wave. An emotional Projector receives an invitation but asks for time before accepting. The sacral response or the splenic hit provides useful information, but the emotional wave has the final say.
