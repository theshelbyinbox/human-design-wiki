# Ego Authority

Ego Authority (also called Heart Authority or Will Authority) is a rare inner decision-making mechanism that applies to specific [[Manifestor]] and [[Projector]] configurations where the [[Heart Center]] (Ego center) is connected to the [[Throat Center]] or [[G Center]] without interference from the [[Solar Plexus Center]] or [[Sacral Center]].

## How It Works

The Heart Center governs willpower, commitment, and the material world. Ego Authority expresses through what the heart genuinely wants to do and commit to -- not what the mind thinks should be done, or what seems socially correct. Decisions made from Ego Authority have the quality of "I want to" or "I will" rather than "I should" or "I need to."

In practice, Ego Authority often manifests through speaking. When a person with this authority talks about a decision and hears themselves say something like "I want this" with genuine conviction -- not performance or wishful thinking -- that is the ego speaking. The key signal is the presence or absence of genuine will behind the words.

## Ego-Manifested (Manifestor)

For Manifestors with Ego Authority, the heart is directly connected to the throat. Decision-making emerges from what the heart commands. These Manifestors tend to be very clear about what they want and what they will not do. Their commitments are genuine precisely because they only commit to what their heart is truly engaged with.

## Ego-Projected (Projector)

For Projectors with Ego Authority, the heart connects to the G Center, giving a sense of identity and direction rooted in the ego's desires. These Projectors must speak their decisions out loud and listen for the quality of genuine will in what they say.

## Key Distinction

Ego Authority is not permission to act selfishly or without regard for others. The ego in Human Design is the center of material-world engagement, promises, and practical willpower. Healthy Ego Authority means making only the commitments one's willpower can sustain, and not making promises from social pressure when the heart is not engaged.

People with Ego Authority who make commitments they do not genuinely want to keep tend to fail to follow through -- not from laziness but because the heart's energy was never engaged. This is not a character flaw but a mechanical reality.
