# Jovian Archive

The Jovian Archive is the organization founded by [[Ra Uru Hu]] to house, preserve, and distribute the [[Human Design System]] and its associated materials. It serves as the primary institutional source for Ra's teachings and operates the International Human Design School (IHDS), which certifies Human Design analysts, teachers, and practitioners.

## Structure

Jovian Archive Corporation holds the trademarks for core Human Design intellectual property, including the Rave BodyGraph and the Rave Mandala. All foundational Human Design educational materials distributed through the IHDS are produced and copyrighted through Jovian Archive.

The organization is headquartered in Canada and maintains the jovianarchive.com website, which provides articles, recordings, and access to Ra's recorded teaching library.

## International Human Design School (IHDS)

The IHDS operates the certification curriculum for Human Design professionals. Certification tracks include:

- **Certified Human Design Analyst**: Trained to read and analyze individual charts
- **Certified Human Design Teacher**: Trained to transmit the knowledge at an educational level
- **Living Your Design Guide**: Entry-level certification for introductory workshop facilitation

The IHDS curriculum draws directly from Ra's recorded lectures and courses, maintaining fidelity to the original transmission.

## After Ra

Ra Uru Hu died in March 2011. Jovian Archive continued operating after his death, maintaining and distributing his extensive library of recordings, books, and courses. The organization continues to certify practitioners through the IHDS framework Ra established.

## Key Publications and Products

Jovian Archive has distributed or produced multiple foundational Human Design texts, including:
- Ra's incarnation cross books (organized by quarter and profile)
- The Channels course (11-lecture series from 2008)
- Variable and PHS lecture series
- Audio course libraries covering all major system components

## Website and Online Presence

The Jovian Archive website (jovianarchive.com) maintains a growing library of articles covering Human Design basics, including explanations of types, strategies, authorities, centers, and the not-self. These are among the most authoritative online summaries of Ra's core teachings.
