# Ra Uru Hu

Ra Uru Hu (born Robert Alan Krakower, 1948–2011) was the founder of [[Human Design System]]. He described himself as the messenger of the knowledge rather than its originator. He taught Human Design for 25 years, dying in March 2011 at the age of 62.

## The Revelation

In January 1987, while living on the island of Ibiza, Spain, Ra Uru Hu experienced what he described as an eight-day encounter with a Voice. During this experience, he received the knowledge that became the Human Design System -- including the bodygraph structure, the gate and channel system, the types and strategies, the authority hierarchy, and the broader cosmological framework.

Ra Uru Hu did not present this origin as a credential or proof of validity. He consistently directed people toward experimentation rather than belief: "You experiment with your strategy and authority. You don't have to believe me. Try it."

## Teaching Style

Ra taught primarily through lectures, workshops, and recordings. His style was discursive and wide-ranging -- he would move between technical mechanics, cosmological context, psychological observations, and personal anecdote within a single talk. He described himself as having the 5/1 profile (Heretic/Investigator), which shaped his universalizing approach: taking the knowledge and making it practically accessible across many contexts.

"Human Design is the science of differentiation. It is not esoteric. It is a logical system." - Ra Uru Hu

Ra consistently argued against treating Human Design as a spiritual belief system or a new age doctrine. He positioned it as a mechanical, logical framework that anyone could test empirically through their own experiment.

## Key Concepts

Ra introduced and systematized the following among many others:

- The four types (Generator, Manifesting Generator, Manifestor, Projector, Reflector) and their strategies
- The nine-center BodyGraph
- The seven inner authorities
- The profile system and the twelve profiles
- The incarnation cross structure and the four quarters
- The concept of the Not-Self and deconditioning
- The seven-year deconditioning timeline
- The Variable system (PHS, colors, tones, bases)
- The prediction of a global shift around 2027 related to the Solar Plexus mutation

## Jovian Archive

Ra founded [[Jovian Archive]] as the organization responsible for maintaining and distributing Human Design materials, certifications, and teachings. The International Human Design School (IHDS) operates under this structure. After Ra's death, Jovian Archive continued distributing his recorded teachings.

## Profile

Ra Uru Hu carried the 5/1 profile -- Heretic/Investigator. In Human Design terms, this is a Left Angle profile with a strong universalizing projection field. Ra acknowledged that this profile shaped his role: the 5/1 is designed to take knowledge and make it broadly applicable across populations and contexts.

## Legacy

Ra's 25 years of teaching produced an extensive body of recorded material covering virtually every aspect of the Human Design system. This material -- lectures, seminars, books, and audio courses -- forms the primary source record for the system. Teachers including [[Maike Gabriela]] and numerous certified analysts trained within the framework Ra established.
