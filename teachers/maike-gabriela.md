# Maike Gabriela

Maike Gabriela is a Human Design reader, teacher, and educator based in Barcelona, Spain. She is the founder of Uprising Human Design, a platform that teaches Human Design with an emphasis on accessibility, embodiment practices, and practical application. She is a [[Projector]] with a 3/5 profile.

## Discovery of Human Design

Maike Gabriela describes discovering Human Design in a moment of personal crisis. In her account: "I was desperate, broke, sick, scared, lost, and continuously praying to the universe. One night in my desperation I listened to a podcast and it said something about being a Projector and I was like, I have no idea what that means -- that's what I am. And my whole life changed."

She subsequently trained as a Human Design analyst and began reading charts professionally through direct message before building a formal business structure.

## Teaching Approach

Maike Gabriela's approach emphasizes making Human Design accessible to people who might find the raw system intimidating or overly complex. Her stated goal is to bring clarity and beauty to the knowledge rather than added overwhelm.

She works extensively with embodiment practices -- meditations, EFT (Emotional Freedom Technique), and affirmations -- as tools for internalizing Human Design knowledge rather than just intellectually understanding it. Her membership community (Soft Power) provides ongoing access to these resources.

Her teaching consistently connects energy type and authority to practical business and life decisions, especially for women navigating self-employment and leadership.

## Business: Uprising Human Design

The name "Uprising" reflects her view that adult self-development is the continuation of the raising process: "As children we had our parents raising us, but when we become adults it's our own responsibility to take care of us."

Her YouTube channel and social media presence include chart reading demonstrations, concept explainers, and Q&A sessions covering types, authorities, centers, profiles, and practical application across areas including relationships, business, parenting, and health.
