# Strategy

Strategy is one of the two practical tools Human Design offers (the other is [[Inner Authority]]). Determined by your type, Strategy describes *how* you are designed to engage with life — when to act, when to wait, what to wait for. There is one Strategy per type, with Manifesting Generators carrying a hybrid of two.

The purpose of Strategy is mechanical: it reduces resistance, bypasses the conditioned mind, and over time produces the [[Signature]] state. Strategy does not require belief. It requires the experiment.
