# Channel 21/45 — The Channel of Money (The Materialist)

The Channel 21/45 connects Gate 21 in the Heart (Ego) Center to Gate 45 in the Throat. It is one of the manifesting channels of the BodyGraph and the dominant tribal channel governing the material plane. Ra Uru Hu described this channel as the wiring through which 93% of human beings flow — meaning the vast majority of people on this planet are organized by the kind of leadership and resource management that the 21/45 represents. It is the channel of the king and queen of the tribe, the boss of the corporation, the head of the family, the one who controls the material domain.

This channel sits in the Ego sub-circuit of the Tribal circuit. The Tribal circuit is about support, bonds, loyalty, and the survival of the lineage. The Ego portion of the Tribal circuit is where willpower meets resources — where the person who controls what the tribe has and what the tribe does emerges. People with Channel 21/45 defined are wired for this kind of authority. They are not built to be told what to do.

## Mechanics

The Channel of Money connects the [[Heart Center]] (will, ego, material, courage) to the [[Throat Center]] (manifestation, expression, voice). Gate 21 sits in the Heart end; Gate 45 sits in the Throat end. Both gates active means both centers are defined and the channel is a manifesting channel — a direct line from a motor (the Heart) to the Throat. Manifesting channels give the chart manifesting capacity regardless of type, though only certain combinations produce Manifestors.

The channel belongs to the Ego sub-circuit of the Tribal circuit. The Ego circuit also contains the 26/44 (Surrender), 40/37 (Community), and the 19/49 (Synthesis). Together these channels carry the tribe's bargains, contracts, resources, and willpower.

If one person carries Gate 21 and another carries Gate 45, an electromagnetic connection forms between them and the channel completes whenever they are in each other's aura — typically producing a strong tribal pull around resources and leadership.

## The Gates

### Gate 21 — Biting Through (The Hunter / Huntress)

Gate 21 sits in the Heart Center as the Gate of the Hunter, with the function of an Ego Manifesting Gate — the potential to dominate and manage the community. People with Gate 21 are wired to establish authority and inspire loyalty. When given control of a domain, they thrive. When others try to tell them what to do, they resist sharply. They have a deep need for material independence.

The challenge of Gate 21 is learning when to release control. The reactive expression of Gate 21 is the controller who must control every detail of every situation, out of an underlying fear of not being supported. The repressed expression is the person who hides their natural authority and lets others dominate them. The corrected expression is someone who controls their own domain — and only their own domain — with confidence and integrity.

In the 21/45, Gate 21 brings the willpower and the manifesting motor. It is the gate that makes sure things actually happen, that the tribe's resources are managed, that the work gets done.

### Gate 45 — Gathering Together (The Gatherer / The King)

Gate 45 sits in the Throat Center as the Gate of the Gatherer. It is the Ego Expression Gate — the voice of dominance, of "I have" or "I don't have." Ra noted that of all 11 gates in the Throat, the "I have / I don't have" language of Gate 45 is the most prevalent on everyone's lips. This is the language of the material world, the language of the haves and have-nots.

Gate 45 is also called The Royal Gate because of its natural ability to gather people, oversee them, and lead them. With Gate 45, the person establishes authority and brings community together through their knowledge of what the tribe needs. The challenge is to use resources for the greater good and to teach as a way of sharing. Repressed, Gate 45 allows itself to be dominated. Reactive, Gate 45 becomes obsessed with authority and puts others down to maintain it.

In the 21/45, Gate 45 provides the voice that the Gate 21 willpower is connected to. It is the king or queen speaking on behalf of the tribe.

## What This Channel Brings

The gift of the Channel of Money is correct material leadership. People with this channel defined are here to manage resources for the tribe — to be the boss, the executive, the head, the one who controls the material domain. When they live in alignment with their Strategy and Authority, they generate the material conditions that allow the tribe to thrive. The community they manage benefits from their authority, and the resources they oversee multiply.

Ra was clear that this channel is the channel of the material plane: "The boss of the corporation, what that boss says everybody is going to do, they do. It is controlled by the 21 who makes damn sure that everybody does what they're supposed to do, otherwise they get fired." This is not metaphor. This is mechanics. The 21/45 is the channel through which the material world is organized, and the people who carry it correctly are the ones who hold that organization together.

Material wealth is a natural expression of the 21/45 lived correctly — not because the person is greedy but because resource management is what this channel does. The challenge is to remember that the resources are not for the self alone. The 45 voice is "we have" — it is the voice of the king or queen who has the tribe in their care.

## The Shadow / Not-Self Expression

The shadow of the 21/45 is dictatorial control. When the not-self mind runs this channel, the person becomes the controller who must control everything in their life — not just their domain but everyone else's domain too. They impose their will, they dominate, they treat the people around them as resources to be managed rather than as members of a tribe in their care. Ra used the language of the dictator and the inner tyrant to describe this expression.

Equally distorted is the repressed expression: the person with this channel who never claims their authority, who lets themselves be dominated, who feels chronically out of control of their own life. The Heart Center wants to claim power, and when it cannot, the person experiences a kind of slow-burn rage or material precariousness.

Ra also pointed to the broader cultural distortion of the 21/45. He said that in the not-self world, this channel is the wiring through which oppressive tribal leadership operates — the warlord, the corrupt boss, the dominating elder. The 21/45 itself is not the problem; the not-self running it is. "You will not free these beings. You cannot. But their lives can be enhanced, improved, enriched. It's a matter of how they are ruled." — Ra Uru Hu

## In Relationship — The Electromagnetic Connection

When one person carries Gate 21 and another carries Gate 45, the electromagnetic completion produces a strong tribal magnetism. The Gate 21 person feels the Gate 45 person's voice as the expression their willpower has been seeking. The Gate 45 person feels the Gate 21 person's manifesting motor as the engine their voice has been waiting for. The dynamic often plays out around money, resources, work, and leadership.

These connections can be especially powerful in business partnerships and family-business contexts. The completion of the channel between two people gives them a temporary experience of the full material-leadership wiring whenever they are together.

## Circuit Context

The Tribal circuit is about support and survival of the lineage. The Ego sub-circuit specifically is about willpower and the material plane — how the tribe organizes its resources, makes its bargains, supports its members, and controls who gets what. The 21/45 is the leadership channel of this sub-circuit. The 40/37 is the community channel. The 19/49 is the synthesis channel of needs and loyalty. Together these channels make up the tribal infrastructure that has organized human society for millennia.

Ra was clear that we are living at the end of a long tribal age. The Cross of Planning that has dominated since 1610 ends in 2027, and the background frequency that has held tribal organization together is fading. The 21/45 will continue to operate after 2027, but the larger social structure it has supported is changing. "The power of the tribe is something that is beginning to fade." Correct expression of the 21/45 — leadership in service of the tribe rather than in service of personal control — becomes more important, not less, in this transition.
