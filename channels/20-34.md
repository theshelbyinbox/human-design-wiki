# Channel 20/34 — The Channel of Charisma

The Channel of Charisma connects Gate 20 in the Throat to Gate 34 in the Sacral. It is one of only four motors that link directly to the Throat in the BodyGraph, and the only one that does so through the Sacral. That makes the people who carry this channel pure Manifesting Generators with a very specific signature: powered action expressed in the now. Ra Uru Hu called this "the driving force of the integration being" and described it as one of the most common channel definitions in the BodyGraph, in part because Gates 20 and 34 sit in direct opposition in the wheel and are frequently activated together by the Sun/Earth axis or the nodal axis.

This channel sits in the Integration sub-circuit of the Individual circuit. Like the other Integration channels, it is survival-oriented and selfish by design. When lived correctly, it produces a being whose action emerges from awareness in the present moment — and that quality is what gives the channel its name. Charisma here is not a performance. It is the natural radiance of a person who is doing what is correct for them, right now.

## Mechanics

The Channel of Charisma connects the [[Sacral Center]] (life force, generative power, response) to the [[Throat Center]] (manifestation, expression). Gate 34 sits in the Sacral; Gate 20 sits in the Throat. Both gates active means both centers are defined, and the channel produces a continuous motor-to-throat connection. Anyone with this channel and Sacral definition is a Manifesting Generator.

The channel belongs to the Integration sub-circuit of the Individual circuit. Integration includes the 20/10 (Awakening), 20/34 (Charisma), 34/57 (Power), 10/57 (Perfected Form), and 10/34 (Exploration). Ra noted that 20/34 is one of three channels in the BodyGraph (along with 43/23 and 40/37) that are oppositions in the wheel, which is why this channel is so commonly defined.

If one person carries Gate 20 and another carries Gate 34, the electromagnetic completion of the channel forms between them, producing a particularly intense magnetic dynamic — especially when one is a Generator and the other a Projector who guides them.

## The Gates

### Gate 20 — Contemplation (The Now)

Gate 20 sits in the Throat as the "I am" gate. Standing alone it can articulate, but in the 20/34 it becomes the voice of the powered Sacral expressing itself in the present moment. Ra said the 20 in this channel articulates "I am busy now," "I am busy doing something for myself now," "I know what I am busy about for myself." It is a voice of present-moment action.

The challenge of Gate 20 is patience. The energy wants to be immersed in the now, but the not-self mind weaponizes that wanting into compulsive busyness. Repressed, Gate 20 disappears from the present. Reactive, Gate 20 drowns itself in activity. The corrected expression of Gate 20 in the 20/34 is action emerging from response, voiced in the moment it is happening.

### Gate 34 — The Power of the Great (Power)

Gate 34 is the only gate of pure individual power in the Sacral Center. It is described as an energy display gate for self-improvement and independence. In the 20/34 specifically, Gate 34's power is paired with Gate 20's voice in the Throat — meaning the most concentrated motor power in the BodyGraph has a direct line to expression.

This is also what makes Gate 34 so dangerous in the not-self. The energy wants to lock in to a process, and the Throat wants to manifest in the now. Together, in the not-self, they produce action initiated from the mind, met by resistance, repeated obsessively. Ra described not-self 20/34s as the people earning materially far below their potential, because they spend their power on the wrong things. The corrected expression is patient response — staying still until the right thing arrives, then jumping with the full power of the gate.

## What This Channel Brings

The gift of Channel 20/34 is action that emerges from awareness. When the person follows their Sacral response and lets the 20 voice that response in the present moment, the result is extraordinarily impressive. "If they are intuitively with awareness empowered to action, that is truly charismatic." — Ra Uru Hu. The person becomes the embodiment of correct doing — taking action only when action is necessary, and putting their entire power behind it when they do.

This is the original Manifesting Generator wiring at its most pure. The energy is generative — it requires something to respond to — and when something correct arrives, the response is immediate and powered. There is no manifestation in the initiating sense here. The 20/34 cannot decide what to do from the mind and have it work. The corrected pattern is to wait for life to bring something, respond with the Sacral, and then act with the 34's power and the 20's voice in the now.

Ra was emphatic that this channel is one of the great gifts of the BodyGraph when corrected. It is the wiring that gets things done in the world. It is the predator that lies still in the savanna until the moment of action arrives, then jumps. It is action guided by awareness in response — the gold standard for survival on this plane.

## The Shadow / Not-Self Expression

The shadow of the 20/34 is what Ra called "the Manifestor disease" of pure Manifesting Generators. The not-self 20/34 has been conditioned to think they have to decide mentally what to do, then put their energy to work on whatever they decided. They meet resistance everywhere. They become enormously frustrated, and because Gate 34's energy locks in to processes, they pour their power into things that do not deserve it.

"Not-self 20/34s are the most pathetic beings one sees. To be a Generator and not use your power correctly means that you can never find satisfaction and you can never reach your Signature." — Ra Uru Hu. The not-self 20/34 disempowers rather than empowers — both themselves and the people around them. The voice of the 20 becomes commentary on busyness rather than expression of correct action. The shadow shows up as chaos, constant initiation, projects abandoned mid-stream, and physical deterioration.

Ra named another extreme of the shadow when the 20/34 is not paired with the 57 or the 10: a particularly violent, destructive, misguided expression of the power. Without the stabilizing influence of the rest of Integration, the raw 20/34 can become dangerous to itself and others.

## In Relationship — The Electromagnetic Connection

When one person carries Gate 20 and another carries Gate 34, the electromagnetic pull between them is among the strongest in the BodyGraph. The Gate 20 person finds that their "I am" voice suddenly has the Sacral motor to back it. The Gate 34 person finds that their power suddenly has a Throat to express through. Both feel temporarily completed in each other's aura.

Ra spoke especially of the natural pairing between a 20/34 and a 43/23 — the Generator and the Projector. The 20/34 has the response power; the 43/23 has the insight to recognize what is worth responding to. Together they form what Ra called the essential partnership, where the Projector guides and the Generator responds with charismatic action.

## Circuit Context

Integration is the survival force of the unique self — the lone-wolf circuitry concerned with the survival of this particular bioform. Inside Integration, the 20/34 is the action piece. It is the channel that produces the actual jump, the actual doing, the actual physical engagement with the world. Without this channel, Integration is awareness and behavior without the engine to execute. With it, the unique self has the power and the voice to act on what its awareness recognizes.

Within the broader Individual circuit, the 20/34 is what makes the rest of individual mutation possible in the material world. The mutation needs a body that survives long enough to carry it. The 20/34 is the action force that keeps that body in motion through the survival demands of life on this plane.
