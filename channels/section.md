# Channels (36)

A Channel is formed when both ends of a pair of complementary [[Gates Overview|gates]] are activated in a chart. There are 36 channels in Human Design — each one connects two [[Centers]] and creates *definition* between them.

Each channel carries a specific quantum of energy, awareness, or expression. Channels are grouped into three circuits — Individual (mutation and knowing), Tribal (support and reproduction), and Collective (sharing and logic) — each with its own purpose and flavor.

When two charts share a hanging gate that completes one of the other's hanging gates, they form an *electromagnetic connection* — the mechanism behind attraction and partnership in Human Design.
