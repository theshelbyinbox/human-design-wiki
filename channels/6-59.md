# Channel 6/59 — The Channel of Mating (Intimacy)

The Channel of Mating connects Gate 6 in the Solar Plexus to Gate 59 in the Sacral Center. Ra Uru Hu called it "the genetic channel" — the wiring through which the species reproduces itself, the deepest life force of the bioform, the channel of intimacy and friction whose purpose is the continuation of life on this plane. People with this channel defined carry a particular kind of penetrating aura: they can break through other people's defenses with a quality Ra described as a hot knife through butter. The 6/59 is built to bond, and the bonding it produces is the most fundamental bond in the BodyGraph.

This channel sits in the Ego sub-circuit of the Tribal circuit. The Tribal circuit is about the support and survival of the lineage; the Ego portion of it is about willpower, resources, and the bargains that hold the tribe together. The 6/59 is the reproductive engine that the rest of the tribal infrastructure exists to support. Ra was emphatic that this is the root life force of the species — and that it is currently undergoing one of the most profound mutations in human history.

## Mechanics

The Channel of Mating connects the [[Sacral Center]] (life force, generative power, response, fertility) to the [[Solar Plexus Center]] (emotional awareness, the wave, intimacy, friction). Gate 59 sits in the Sacral end; Gate 6 sits in the Solar Plexus end. Both gates active means both centers are defined, and the channel produces a continuous emotional-generative bond — the engine of human reproduction at the genetic level.

The channel belongs to the Ego sub-circuit of the Tribal circuit. The Ego circuit also contains the 21/45 (Money), 40/37 (Community), 26/44 (Surrender), and 19/49 (Synthesis). Together these channels constitute the support and bonding infrastructure of the tribe.

If one person carries Gate 6 and another carries Gate 59, an electromagnetic completion of the channel forms between them — and this is the wiring most directly responsible for the attractions that lead to mating and reproduction.

## The Gates

### Gate 59 — Dispersion (Sexuality)

Gate 59 sits in the Sacral Center as the Gate of Sexuality. Its function is sexual role, fertility, and reproduction. People with Gate 59 are wired to break down barriers in order to create intimacy and achieve union — not only sexual union, but union of any kind that brings something new into being. The energy is generative and penetrating. It seeks to birth — children, businesses, creative projects, partnerships.

The challenge of Gate 59 is to collaborate and initiate others into sustainable bonds from a place of sufficiency rather than need. Repressed, Gate 59 fears intimacy and rejection and excludes itself. Reactive, Gate 59 acts forcefully or intrusively so that others reject it. The corrected expression is the Sacral aura's natural penetration combined with discernment about who is worth bonding with.

Ra noted that the 59 is currently mutating. As the perfect mirror of the 55 in the wheel, the 59 is becoming more selective. Where it once bonded indiscriminately for reproduction, it is becoming more discerning. This is part of the larger shift in human genetics that Ra associated with the rise of autism, the rise of prostate cancer, and the gradual emergence of what he called the Rave being.

### Gate 6 — Conflict (Friction)

Gate 6 sits in the Solar Plexus Center as the Gate of Friction. Its function is the Awareness/Compassion gate that determines whether to bond or not. People with Gate 6 create friction to stimulate growth and forge deep connections through it. They are selective about who they let into their lives, but once someone is in, they fight for the relationship and for the resources to make it work.

Ra emphasized that the 6 is what creates the emotional wave itself. "It is out of the 6th gate that the three waves of the emotional system emerged." Without Gate 6, there is no emotional motor in the chart. The wave that the 6 generates is the same wave that powers intimacy, friction, growth, and ultimately the chemistry of bonding.

The challenge of Gate 6 is to master the emotional energy and trust that its impact is in service. Repressed, Gate 6 avoids emotional bonds out of fear of conflict or intimacy. Reactive, Gate 6 lashes out and creates conflict it cannot regulate. The corrected expression uses friction productively — as the stimulation that deepens intimacy rather than as the eruption that destroys it.

## What This Channel Brings

The gift of the Channel of Mating is the capacity for deep, generative intimacy. People with the 6/59 are wired to penetrate the auras of others and to form the bonds that produce new life — biological, creative, or relational. They are equally wired for the friction that those bonds require. Intimacy in the 6/59 is not the soft cliché of closeness; it is the encounter that produces something new. Friction is the mechanism. The wave produces the chemistry.

Ra was clear that the 59/6 is the root life force of the species. "The continuation of that force, the continuity of that force is something that is embodied in the 59/6." This channel is what keeps humanity going at the genetic level. Every reproductive bond, every creative collaboration that produces something new, every partnership that births a project — at the deepest level, this is the wiring.

For people with this channel defined, intimacy is non-negotiable. They need the bonds and the friction. They need the wave and the discharge. When this channel is correctly lived through Strategy and Authority, the partnerships and projects it produces have a depth that other configurations cannot match.

## The Shadow / Not-Self Expression

The shadow of the 6/59 is intimacy without discernment — or intimacy refused entirely. The not-self 6/59 either penetrates everyone's aura indiscriminately, producing a string of bonds that go nowhere, or shuts down the penetrating quality of the channel altogether and walls itself off from intimacy. The friction of Gate 6 becomes either constant fighting or complete avoidance of conflict. The fertility of Gate 59 either becomes promiscuous bonding or barren isolation.

When the emotional wave of Gate 6 is not honored, the person rides the high to push for intimacy and then crashes into withdrawal. The bonds formed at the top of the wave are not the same bonds the person wants at the bottom of the wave. Without emotional clarity over time, the 6/59 produces relationships that begin in heat and end in confusion.

Ra also placed the larger evolutionary shadow of this channel inside the mutation of the Solar Plexus. As the 59 becomes more selective and the 55 mutates toward Spirit, fewer viable conceptions occur, fewer bonds produce children, and the species moves toward the kind of population contraction Ra had predicted since the early 1990s. The not-self 6/59 navigates this shift blindly, producing the rising rates of fertility difficulty, relational instability, and developmental conditions that Ra associated with the broader genetic transition.

## In Relationship — The Electromagnetic Connection

When one person carries Gate 6 and another carries Gate 59, the electromagnetic completion of the Channel of Mating produces what is arguably the most powerful magnetic pull in the BodyGraph. The Gate 59 person feels the Gate 6 person's emotional friction as the wave their fertility has been waiting for. The Gate 6 person feels the Gate 59 person's penetrating Sacral aura as the bond their wave has been building toward. This is the wiring of sexual chemistry at its most direct.

The connection is not necessarily harmonious. Friction is part of the channel by design. Two people who complete the 6/59 between them often experience an intense pull combined with significant conflict. Both can be correct. Ra's caution was always to honor emotional authority — to wait for clarity over time before acting on the intensity, because the wave is not the same as the truth, and the intimacy that is real is the one that survives multiple wave cycles.

## Circuit Context

The Tribal circuit is about the support and survival of the lineage. The Ego sub-circuit specifically organizes the willpower, resources, and bonds of the tribe. Within this sub-circuit, the 6/59 is the reproductive root — the engine that produces the next generation that all the other tribal channels exist to support. The 21/45 manages the resources. The 40/37 brokers the community bargains. The 19/49 carries the loyalty. The 6/59 makes the babies that the rest of it is for.

Ra placed the 6/59 at the center of the current human mutation. The era of the Cross of Planning is ending in 2027. The era of the Sleeping Phoenix — the 55-59 — is rising. The mutation in the Solar Plexus that is reshaping the wave is centered exactly here, on this channel. People with the 6/59 defined are carrying one of the most actively mutating pieces of wiring in the BodyGraph. Whatever the channel produces in their life is also part of the larger evolutionary story of the species.
